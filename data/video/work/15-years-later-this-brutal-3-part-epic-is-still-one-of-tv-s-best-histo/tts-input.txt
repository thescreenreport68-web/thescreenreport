Spartacus is still a streaming hit, 60 years later. The 1960 Stanley Kubrick epic remains widely available for rental and purchase. It follows enslaved Thracian Spartacus leading a massive revolt against Rome. The film was a huge success, winning four Academy Awards. It earned sixty million dollars globally on a twelve million dollars budget. Today, you can rent or buy Spartacus on Amazon, Apple, Google, and YouTube.
