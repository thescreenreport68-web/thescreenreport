The director of Apple T-V's Silo just revealed a massive new influence for season three. Michael Dinner says the new Washington D-C storyline channels classic political thrillers. He cited films like Three Days of the Condor and The Parallax View as key inspirations. Dinner, who's directed through season four, called both narratives detective stories.. He initially committed to the show for only six months. But creator Graham Yost convinced him to stay on for three years. Season three explores manipulation and erased memories.
