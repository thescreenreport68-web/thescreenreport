Josh Gad just joined the Ocean's prequel in a major role. The actor joins Margot Robbie and Bradley Cooper in the new film. Variety reports Gad has a significant part in the period heist movie. This prequel focuses on Danny Ocean's parents, set in 1962. Robbie confirmed they pull off an epic heist at the Monaco Grand Prix. Bradley Cooper co-stars, directs, and wrote the screenplay.
