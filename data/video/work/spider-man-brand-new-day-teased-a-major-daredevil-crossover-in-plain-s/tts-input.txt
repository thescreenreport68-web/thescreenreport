A new Spider-Man movie will kick off a New York City gang war. But it's the absence of a certain blind lawyer that starts it. Spider-Man, Brand New Day follows Daredevil, Born Again's second season. Collider reports this film connects directly to the show's cliffhanger. Daredevil's disappearance creates a power vacuum in New York's criminal underworld. This allows new villains, like the Rose, to rise. The movie reportedly adapts the Gang War comic storyline. Spider-Man even teams up with the Punisher to stop it. But the Punisher and Spider-Man rarely agree. Will Daredevil return to clean up the mess?
