Jodie Foster says A-I is ALREADY making major Hollywood films. The Oscar winner points to the Brad Pitt racing drama 'F one' as an example. She describes its structure as exactly what you'd learn in school.. Actors deliver lines exactly the way it would be written if a computer was writing.. Foster says filmmakers can dominate the technology to make something big and beautiful.. She uses A-I for small helpful things like pre-visualization and storyboarding.
