Tom Hanks' new movie just landed a surprise summer release. Sony greenlit The Comebacker for July 30, 2027. Hanks reunites with director Marielle Heller, who guided his Oscar nomination for A Beautiful Day in the Neighborhood.. He stars as a sportswriter covering a major league team. The cast includes Callum Turner, Bad Bunny, and Colman Domingo. It faces direct competition from A Quiet Place III.. This summer slot positions it for blockbuster success.
