Marvel just cast a new Thor. Voice actor Liam Karlsson. He plays a younger, brash God of Thunder. And he's joined by a new Loki. Nylan Parthipan voices the trickster god. They debut in the Disney Junior series 'Iron Man and His Awesome Friends.'. The episode sees Loki enchant the team's vehicles. So Thor has to team up with Ironheart and Iron Hulk. This kid-friendly Thor is distinct from Chris Hemsworth's M-C-U version. He'll return in a new Avengers series for Disney Junior in 2027. Full story at The Screen Report, link in bio.
