Everyone thought Rose Tyler was gone. She isn't. Billie Piper reprises her iconic Doctor Hoo role this August. She joins Christopher Eccleston in new audio series Dark Tides. The story sees Rose and The Doctor in a brutal, gothic space adventure. It's available for preorder now from Big Finish Productions. This marks Piper's first audio return since 2025's TV finale.
