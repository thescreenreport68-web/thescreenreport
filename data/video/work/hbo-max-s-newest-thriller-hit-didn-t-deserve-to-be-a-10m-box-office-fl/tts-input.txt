Zazie Beetz's new movie flopped, then became a hit. Her 2026 action-horror film They Will Kill You lost money at the box office. It grossed just 10.9 million domestically on a 20 million dollar budget. But now, per Collider, it's a top three film on HBO Max. The film stars Beetz as Asia, an ex-con searching for her sister. She infiltrates The Virgil, a NYC high-rise controlled by a deadly cult. Fans are now discovering its unique blend of action, horror, and dark comedy. Full story at The Screen Report, link in bio.
