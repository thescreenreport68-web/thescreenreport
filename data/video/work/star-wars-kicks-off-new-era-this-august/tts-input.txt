What if a blue lightsaber didn't make you a Jedi? That's the question at the heart of a new Star Wars anime series. In The Ninth Jedi, the blades change color based on the wielder's heart. So red doesn't confirm a Sith. And blue doesn't guarantee a Jedi. The story follows Lah Kara, hunting for her missing father, a master lightsaber smith. Her quest to rebuild the Jedi order puts her against a masked villain, Nawaam. The limited series premieres August fifth on Disney Plus and Hulu. So if you were waiting for a Star Wars story that breaks all the rules? Your wait ends in just over two years.
