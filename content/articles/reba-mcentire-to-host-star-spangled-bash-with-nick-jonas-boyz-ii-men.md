Reba McEntire will perform in a major patriotic television special on ABC this Fourth of July, according to Billboard. The country music icon is part of the lineup for "Disney Celebrates America: Nashville’s Star-Spangled Bash."

## Who Else Is Performing?

The three-hour live event, hosted by Ryan Seacrest, features a roster of music stars. Alongside McEntire, performers include Nick Jonas, Boyz II Men, Tim McGraw, and Lauren Daigle. The All-American Rejects, Ne-Yo, and Sublime are also set to appear.

## What Makes the Special Unique?

Beyond the music, the special promises a massive visual spectacle. Billboard reports the event will feature one of the largest fireworks and drone shows in the United States. The Nashville Symphony will provide a live score for the display, which takes place in downtown Nashville.

## How Can I Watch?

"Nashville’s Star-Spangled Bash" airs on Saturday, July 4, at 8 p.m. ET/PT on ABC. Viewers can watch through their local ABC affiliate or stream it live via services that carry the network, such as Hulu + Live TV.
