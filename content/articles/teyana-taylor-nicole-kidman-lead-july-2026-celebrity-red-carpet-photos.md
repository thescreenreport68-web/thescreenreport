Teyana Taylor attended a party ahead of the FIFA World Cup 2026 Final in New York City in July 2026, according to Variety. The singer and actress arrived at the Tequila Don Julio 1942 Port of Champions x Palm Tree Crew and Mary Lou’s event.

[Nicole Kidman](/celebrity/nicole-kidman-shares-rare-photos-of-daughter-sunday-rose-for-her-miles/) was also a highlight of the month's celebrity red carpet photos. The actress attended Wimbledon on July 12, wearing Ralph Lauren.

## Where else did celebrities appear?

[Dwayne Johnson](/celebrity/wil-wheaton-slams-coward-dwayne-the-rock-johnson-over-keeping-his-poli/) launched his live-action adaptation of “Moana” with a blue carpet and screening at the Hollywood Bowl on July 7. The event was the first film premiere at the venue since the original “Mulan” in 1998.

Zendaya delivered several striking looks at premieres for “The Odyssey.” In New York, she wore a Matières Fécales gown with large wings at the film's premiere at AMC Lincoln Square on July 14. She also appeared at the film's premieres in London and Paris earlier in the month.

Other notable appearances included Giannis Antetokounmpo at a watch party in Atlanta, Karol G at a Telemundo event in New York, and Matt Damon and Anne Hathaway on a SiriusXM show. The cast of the new Peacock series “The Five-Star Weekend,” including Jennifer Garner and Gemma Chan, reunited at its Los Angeles premiere.

## What events are coming up?

Variety notes that July started strong with events following Taylor Swift’s wedding and the premieres for “The Odyssey.” The outlet will continue to update its gallery with the best celebrity red carpet and event photos throughout the month.
