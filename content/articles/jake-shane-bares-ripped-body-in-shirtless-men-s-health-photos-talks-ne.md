What happens when a comedian known for his self-deprecating humor gets seriously ripped? Jake Shane is finding out.

The 26-year-old influencer, comedian, and podcast host is showing off a dramatic body transformation in a new feature for *Men's Health*, as reported by Just Jared. The spread includes shirtless photos of Shane demonstrating his intense workout routine, which the magazine described as including everything "from supersets of TRX pushups and pullups to explosive landmine presses to sled pushes with almost 300 pounds of resistance."

For Shane, the journey is about more than aesthetics. He opened up about the mental health benefits that have become central to his routine. "If I don't work out, my day feels off. It's more mental health than anything else," he said. A key moment of progress came when he realized, "I started feeling comfortable walking around my neighborhood with my shirt off, which I never thought I’d be able to do."

But the transformation presents a unique professional challenge for a comic whose act has often leaned on personal insecurities. Jake Shane

His plan is to stay grounded. "When I feel like I’m in shape, I go back into this cycle of ‘Okay, now I need to dress cool. Now I need to be with the cool kids.’ If I continue working out and looking good and continue being myself, I’m golden," Shane said.

The feature, photographed by Jose Mandojana, offers a glimpse into Shane's disciplined fitness regimen, which appears to be a significant shift for the host of the *Therapuss* podcast. While the photos confirm a notable physical change, the full details of his training timeline and specific fitness goals remain topics for his dedicated followers to explore in the full *Men's Health* article.
