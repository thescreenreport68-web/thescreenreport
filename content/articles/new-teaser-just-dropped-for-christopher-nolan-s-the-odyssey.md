A new teaser has reportedly dropped for [Christopher Nolan](/celebrity/zendaya-the-odyssey-christopher-nolan/)'s long-awaited epic, *The Odyssey*, according to a report from IMDb. This marks the first official look at the director's $250 million film, which is slated to hit theaters on July 17, 2026.

## What is the story of Nolan's 'The Odyssey'?

The film follows the legendary King of Ithaca, Odysseus, on his long and perilous voyage home following the Trojan War. The official synopsis promises a journey where the hero is forced to confront the whims of gods, mythological monsters, and trials that will test his cunning and humanity. Nolan's take is described as an adventure, action, and fantasy film, suggesting a grand, visually ambitious interpretation of the classic myth.

## Who is in the cast?

Matt Damon leads the ensemble as the cunning hero, Odysseus. He is joined by Tom Holland as his son, Telemachus, and Anne Hathaway as his faithful wife, Penelope. The supporting cast is packed with A-list talent, including Robert Pattinson as the suitor Antinous, Lupita Nyong'o in a dual role as Helen or Clytemnestra, and Charlize Theron as the nymph Calypso. Zendaya will portray the goddess Athena, with Benny Safdie and Jon Bernthal as the kings Agamemnon and Menelaus, respectively. John Leguizamo rounds out the cast as the loyal swineherd, Eumaeus.

## Why is this film so anticipated?

*The Odyssey* represents a major swing for Nolan, with a reported budget of $250 million and a runtime of 172 minutes. The combination of Nolan's directorial vision, a timeless epic story, and one of the most impressive casts assembled in recent memory has generated immense excitement among fans and industry watchers alike.
