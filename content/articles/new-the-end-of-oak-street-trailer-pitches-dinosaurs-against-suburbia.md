The official marketing push for David Robert Mitchell’s latest genre-bending film has begun. According to a report from Bloody Disgusting, a new trailer and a series of posters have been released for *[The End of Oak Street](/movies/anne-hathaway-and-ewan-mcgregor-battle-dinosaurs-in-the-end-of-oak-street/)*, a sci-fi thriller that pitches suburban life against a prehistoric threat.

**What is The End of Oak Street about?**

Its official synopsis describes a mysterious cosmic event that rips Oak Street from suburbia and transports the entire neighborhood to an unknown place. The story follows the Platt family—parents Denise and Greg, and their children—as they struggle to survive in their now unrecognizable surroundings. The report explicitly frames the premise as bringing the terror of *Jurassic Park* into a domestic setting.

**Who stars in the film?**

[Anne Hathaway](/movies/anne-hathaway-confirms-princess-diaries-3-story-breakthrough-after-starting/) and Ewan McGregor lead the cast as Denise and Greg Platt. They are joined by Christian Convery and Maisy Stella as their children, Brian and Audrey. The supporting cast includes Jordan Alexa Davis, P.J. Byrne, and Chris Coy. Director David Robert Mitchell, known for *It Follows*, has cited inspirations including *Jurassic Park*, *The Twilight Zone*, and M. Night Shyamalan’s *Signs*.

**When does The End of Oak Street come out?**

The film is scheduled for a theatrical release on **August 14, 2026**. It is produced by J.J. Abrams and marks Mitchell’s return to directing a major studio feature.
