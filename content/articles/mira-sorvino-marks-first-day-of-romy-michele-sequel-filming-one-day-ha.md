Mira Sorvino has confirmed that production on the long-awaited *Romy and Michele* sequel has officially started. The Oscar-winning actress shared the news on Friday, marking a major step forward for the project. She expressed her gratitude and excitement about the first day of filming.

According to Deadline, the sequel is being directed by Tim Federle. The original film's writer, Robin Schiff, is also returning. This development follows years of speculation and hopeful updates from the cast.

## What Is the Status of the Sequel?

Sorvino and her co-star Lisa Kudrow have been finalizing their deals to return since 2024. In early 2026, Kudrow told Vanity Fair she had read a hilarious script and that everyone involved wanted to make the film. Actor Alan Cumming, who played Sandy Frink, later confirmed on *The Jennifer Hudson Show* that filming was set to begin in the summer of 2026. The project is now in active production at 20th Century Studios, with a planned 2027 release on Hulu.

## Why Is the Original Film So Beloved?

*Romy and Michele's High School Reunion* was released in 1997 to mixed reviews but found a massive audience later. It has since become a definitive cult classic. The story of two friends pretending to be successful businesswomen to impress their old classmates struck a chord. Its legacy is built on the buoyant chemistry between Sorvino and Kudrow, which turned a slight premise into an enduring comedy.

The film's journey from modest box office to beloved favorite is a testament to its heart and humor. You can explore more about its enduring appeal in our feature on cult classic comedies. A made-for-TV prequel aired in 2005, and a stage musical adaptation premiered in 2017.

## What Can Fans Expect Next?

With filming now underway, the next major updates will likely be first-look photos or a confirmed title. The core creative team from the original is involved, which bodes well for capturing the film's unique spirit. The sequel aims to revisit Romy and Michele decades after their iconic reunion antics.
