Johnny Knoxville, Steve-O, and the rest of the Jackass crew have returned for their final curtain call. '[Jackass: Best and Last](/movies/jackass-best-and-last-box-office-day-24-adds-70-000-as-domestic-total-hits-18/)' is the fifth and concluding chapter in the long-running franchise of outrageous stunts and painful pranks. Directed by franchise stalwart Jeff Tremaine, this 92-minute film promises one last insane crusade, blending action, comedy, and documentary styles into a nostalgic farewell tour for its dedicated fanbase.

The cast is the heart of the film, featuring the original gang all playing heightened versions of themselves. Johnny Knoxville leads as the fearless ringleader, joined by Steve-O as the self-destructive daredevil, Chris Pontius as the party-loving wildcard, and Jason 'Wee Man' Acuña as the diminutive dynamo. The full ensemble includes Dave England, Preston Lacy, Ehren McGhehey, and the returning Bam Margera, ensuring the chaotic chemistry that defined the series is present for its final act.
