Jeff Bridges lends his iconic voice to the rambunctious, ridiculous, and totally true story of '[Minions & Monsters](/movies/minions-monsters-box-office-day-21-adds-3-4-million-as-domestic-total-hits-141/).' The 90-minute animated adventure-comedy, directed by Pierre Coffin, chronicles how the Minions conquered Hollywood, became movie stars, lost everything, and then unleashed monsters onto the world, forcing them to band together to save the planet from the mayhem they created.

Coffin also voices James, Henry, Ed, Dick, and the Minions themselves. He's joined by an all-star cast: Trey Parker as Goomi, Allison Janney as Olivia, Christoph Waltz as Max, Jeff Bridges as Frank and Elwood, Jesse Eisenberg as Dort, Zoey Deutch as Debbie, and Bobby Moynihan as Phillips. The film has earned Illumination’s top critical response of its 17 movies, achieving a franchise-best 91% on Rotten Tomatoes and an "A-" grade on CinemaScore exit polls.

## At the Box Office

Minions & Monsters has grossed $143,860,945 at the domestic box office, 22 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 3,510 theaters. The film added $2,277,815 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $364 million. It carries a reported production budget of $85 million.
