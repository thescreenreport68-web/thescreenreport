Sony Pictures has acquired the global distribution rights for a major film adaptation of the Indian epic poem *Ramayana*, according to The Hollywood Reporter. The news and the film’s first trailer were revealed at San Diego Comic-Con in July 2026.

Prime Focus Studios, the film's producer, will handle the release within India. The epic centers on Prince Rama, his wife Sita, and their exile, which leads to Sita's kidnapping by the demon king Ravana.

## Who is directing and starring in 'Ramayana'?

Nitesh Tiwari directs the project. The cast includes Ranbir Kapoor, Yash, and Sai Pallavi in leading roles. The film boasts music from Oscar winners Hans Zimmer and A.R. Rahman, with action choreography by Terry Notary and Guy Norris.

## What is the goal for the film's release?

The producers intend for *Ramayana* to reach a global audience. It will be released in multiple languages worldwide, including an English-language version.

Producer Namit Malhotra, founder of the visual effects company DNEG, stated the film's ambition. "For generations, the Ramayana has inspired people through its timeless humanity and enduring values," he said. "Our ambition has always been to honor that legacy while creating a cinematic experience worthy of audiences everywhere."
