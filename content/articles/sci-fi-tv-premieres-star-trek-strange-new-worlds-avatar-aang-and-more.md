A wave of new sci-fi and fantasy television is set to arrive across streaming and cable this week, according to a schedule report from Cancelled Sci Fi.

Four high-profile premieres are on the calendar, headlined by returning favorites and a fresh entry in a beloved animated universe. The schedule offers a mix of spacefaring adventure, apocalyptic drama, and multiversal comedy.

## What is premiering this week?

The crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise returns for more adventures in **'Star Trek: Strange New Worlds'** on Paramount+. The first new episode will be available on Thursday.

HBO Max debuts the comedic series **'Stuart Fails to Save the Universe'** on Thursday. The show follows a comic book store owner who must fix a multiverse he accidentally created.

Paramount+ premieres **'Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender'** on Saturday. This new story sees Aang and his friends embark on a global quest to find an ancient power that could save his culture.

The series follows Maggie and Negan as they work to build a community in post-apocalyptic Manhattan.

## Are any other genre shows ending?

The report notes it is a busy week with several season finales alongside the premieres. 'Rick & Morty' concludes its ninth season on Sunday, and 'My Adventures with Superman' wraps its third season the same day.
