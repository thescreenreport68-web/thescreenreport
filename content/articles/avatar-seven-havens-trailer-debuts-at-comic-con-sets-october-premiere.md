The next chapter in the beloved Avatar universe has a release date and a first look.

The series is set in a world shattered by a cataclysm, where a young Earthbender discovers she is the new Avatar following Korra. In this dangerous era, the title marks her as humanity's destroyer, not its savior. Hunted by human and spirit foes alike, she must join forces with her long-lost twin to uncover their origins and save the Seven Havens before civilization's last strongholds collapse.

## Who is in the cast of 'Avatar: Seven Havens'?

The voice cast is led by Saheli Khan as Pavi and Aishwarya 'Aishu' Devan as Nisha. They are joined by Akshay Khanna as Karthik, Major Curda as Jae, and Sakina Jaffrey as Agam. Darren Barnet, Dianne Doan, and Dee Bradley Baker round out the ensemble, with Baker voicing both Geet and Ruhi.

## What is the show's connection to the Avatar franchise?

The series is the latest entry from the original creators, Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko, who serve as co-creators and executive producers. It continues the franchise's tradition of exploring new Avatars across generations, picking up the story after the events of 'The Legend of Korra'. Produced by Nickelodeon and Avatar Studios, the show's first season is confirmed for 26 episodes.
