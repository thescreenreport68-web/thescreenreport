The feature documents the final nights of the band's monumental Skeletour World Tour, captured over two sold-out, phone-free shows at Palacio de los Deportes in Mexico City in September 2025. Directed by Amir Juan Chamdin, the project is described as a definitive document of the tour and a love letter to the band's fans.

## What makes this concert film different?

‘2 Big To Rig’ marks a stylistic shift from Ghost's previous live film, ‘Rite Here Rite Now.’ Where that film blended concert footage with a narrative story, this new release is expected to focus primarily on the raw live performances. It will also offer exclusive behind-the-scenes and backstage access, immersing viewers in the full Ghost ritual.

## Who appears in the film?

The film features frontman Tobias Forge in his role as Papa V Perpetua, leading his band of Nameless Ghouls. The cast list includes Per Eriksson, Cosmo Sniderman, Hayden C Scott, Laura Scarborough, Olivia Morreale, Randy Moore, and Gabriela Gunčíková, all performing as Nameless Ghouls. The setlist promises to rip through material from the band's six albums, including tracks from their 2025 global chart-topper ‘Skeletá’ and non-LP favorites.

Kymberli Frueh of Trafalgar Releasing, which is distributing the film, called it a "fitting farewell to an era for fans" and "the perfect way to close such a significant chapter in the band’s history." The 115-minute film is designed to share the Skeletour experience with audiences in territories where the massive live show could not be staged.
