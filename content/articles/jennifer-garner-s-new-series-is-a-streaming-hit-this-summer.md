Jennifer Garner's new limited series has become a quiet streaming hit for Peacock this summer, according to IMDb. The drama debuted on the platform on July 9, 2026.

The series is based on Elin Hilderbrand's 2023 bestselling novel. It follows a woman whose meticulously planned vacation takes an unexpected turn, weaving together family drama, friendship, and self-discovery across eight episodes.

## What is Jennifer Garner's new series about?

The show is a heartfelt drama developed by Bekah Brunstetter, a writer known for her work on *This Is Us*. It blends emotional storytelling into what has been described as an easy binge-watch. The series has drawn rave reviews from both fans of the source material and new viewers.

## When did the series premiere?

The limited series made its debut on Peacock on July 9, 2026. Its positive reception has solidified its status as one of the standout streaming successes of the season.
