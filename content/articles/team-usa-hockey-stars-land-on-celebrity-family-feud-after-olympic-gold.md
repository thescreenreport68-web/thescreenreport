Gold medal-winning Team USA hockey players are set to appear on an upcoming episode of *Celebrity Family Feud*, according to a report from Fox News. The episode will air Thursday at 8 p.m. ET on ABC.

The appearance features members of the US men’s, women’s, and Paralympic teams who swept gold at the 2026 Winter Olympics. The men’s team will include NHL players Dylan Larkin and Connor Hellebuyck, while the women’s side will be captained by Hilary Knight.

## Who is playing on the show?

According to the report, the teams will be split by gender. The men’s and Paralympic squad features Larkin, Hellebuyck, Griffin LaMarre, Jack Wallace, and Declan Farmer. They will compete against the women’s team of Knight, Hannah Bilka, Cayla Barnes, Laila Edwards, and Caroline Harvey.

## When does the episode air?

The special hockey edition of *Celebrity Family Feud* is scheduled for Thursday at 8 p.m. Eastern Time on ABC. The show, hosted by Steve Harvey, is currently available to stream on Hulu, fuboTV, and YouTube TV as of July 2026.

The report frames the TV appearance as a celebration of a landmark year for American hockey, coming after the program’s historic Olympic success. With the next major international tournaments years away, the game show spot offers a chance to keep the team in the public eye.
