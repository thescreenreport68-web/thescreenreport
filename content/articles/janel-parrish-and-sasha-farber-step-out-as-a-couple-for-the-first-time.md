Janel Parrish and Sasha Farber stepped onto the red carpet in Los Angeles wearing matching white outfits, their affection for each other on full display.

Parrish, 37, and Farber, 42, have been romantically linked since April of this year. Their relationship began shortly after Parrish finalized her divorce from Chris Long. Farber also finalized his divorce from Emma Slater earlier this year.

## From Friends to More

The pair are hardly strangers. "We were friends for years," Parrish told Us Weekly. That long friendship laid the groundwork for their current romance.

In an exclusive interview with Us Weekly, the mutual admiration was clear. Parrish described Farber as "the sweetest, most brightest light to everybody that he meets," and said he has "the biggest heart and is so caring and loving." Farber reciprocated, telling the outlet, "She is so sweet, down to earth, so loving, so real, and I just feel so much support coming from her side."

On social media, Farber posted a video of the couple dancing, captioning it, "She’s still got it @janelparrish 🔥."
