Megan Fox is set to return for a sequel to the 2009 supernatural cult classic *Jennifer's Body*, according to a report by MovieWeb. The update follows earlier confirmations from original writer Diablo Cody and director Karyn Kusama that they were developing a follow-up.

## What is the sequel about?
While specific plot details are under wraps, director Karyn Kusama has expressed strong enthusiasm for the project. In a recent interview with MovieWeb, she teased the script, calling it a "wild and crazy and smart and funny" re-imagining of the first film. The original movie followed a high school cheerleader, Jennifer, who becomes possessed by a demon and begins killing her male classmates.

According to industry reports cited by MovieWeb, the sequel is set to start shooting this fall. It is not yet confirmed whether Amanda Seyfried, who co-starred in the original, will also return.

## Why is the original film a cult classic?
Upon its initial release, *Jennifer's Body* was a box office disappointment and received mixed reviews, earning a 47% critics score on Rotten Tomatoes. However, in the years since, the film has found a passionate niche audience, aided by its availability on streaming platforms. Its blend of horror and dark comedy has cemented its status as a modern cult favorite.
