Is Dua Lipa back to work on her next era? The singer was spotted heading to Electric Lady Studios in New York City on Tuesday, according to photos published by Just Jared. She was seen carrying a bottle of water and on her phone before a gentleman in a blue suit opened the studio door for her.

Dua wore a white tank and pants for the outing, continuing a bridal-themed look fresh off her honeymoon with husband Callum Turner. The couple recently held a star-studded ceremony in Sicily, Italy, following a civil ceremony in London on May 31st.

The studio visit has fans speculating that new music could be on the way. It's been two years since the release of her last album, *Radical Optimism*. Since then, she has released singles including "End of an Era" and "Two Hearts," and teamed up with K-pop star Jennie for "Handlebars." This has not been confirmed by Dua Lipa, their representatives, or any official source — it is unverified and currently circulating as speculation.

Beyond music, Dua is also set to appear in the upcoming A24 comedy *Peaked*, which stars Emma Mackey, Connor Storrie, Simone Ashley, and Owen Thiele.
