On Sunday, July 19, actress Lauren Tom posted a series of photos of her daughter on Instagram, accompanied by a heartfelt and difficult message. "I wanted to share some tough news today …", the 64-year-old star wrote, according to Us Weekly.

She shared that her 25-year-old daughter, Ellie, has been diagnosed with osteosarcoma, a form of bone cancer. "This is my beautiful daughter, Ellie. She is currently battling osteosarcoma, (bone cancer) and my family and I are doing everything we can to aid in her healing.", Tom wrote.

## A Call for Helpers

In her post, Tom quoted Fred Rogers, recalling his mother's advice to "look for the helpers" in scary times. She publicly thanked their "friends and family and the incredible doctors and nurses" at Cedars Sinai hospital for their "ongoing boundless support, love, and care.". "It is truly humbling to see how willing people are to reach out and help," she added.

Tom described her daughter as "an amazing, deep, sensitive, wise, strong, brave, intelligent, and funny soul." She concluded by asking for prayers for Ellie's complete recovery, noting, "We have a long journey ahead of us."

## Celebrity Support Pours In

The post quickly drew supportive comments from fellow actors. Ken Jeong wrote, "Sending all our love and prayers for Ellie. Anything we can do let us know. Love you. ❤️❤️❤️❤️🙏.". According to Us Weekly, Kurtwood Smith wrote, "There is a place for you both in my heart Lauren and Ellie please stay!❤️," and Lilan Bowden added, "Ellie sending you love and energy every day!! You are so cool and we want you here with us!! You’re in my thoughts a lot and next time I see you I am giving you the biggest hug. Also your hair color is fire, I love it. 💙💜 @eliatom_."

Tom, who is best known for playing Julie on the second season of *Friends*, also reportedly left a message on her daughter's own Instagram account. "You are strong, brave and will recover from this completely to live a beautiful, meaningful life," she wrote.
