Souheila Yacoub's 'Evil Dead Burn' Conjures a Solid Start

Souheila Yacoub leads the latest chapter in the storied horror franchise, '[Evil Dead Burn](/movies/evil-dead-burn-box-office-day-6-adds-1-2-million-as-domestic-total-hits-18-2/),' a film that finds terror not in a remote cabin but within the walls of a grieving family home.

Directed by Sébastien Vaniček, this 110-minute horror-thriller follows Alice (Souheila Yacoub), a woman seeking solace with her late husband's family after his sudden death. She soon learns they are descendants of a leading researcher on demonic possession. Her search for comfort turns into a fight for survival as her in-laws begin to transform, one by one, into monstrous creatures that feed on fear, forcing Alice to discover that the vows she took in life endure even in the face of unspeakable horror.

The ensemble cast is led by Souheila Yacoub as the besieged widow, Alice. She is joined by Tandi Wright as Susan, Hunter Doohan as Joseph, and Luciane Buchanan as Thya, portraying members of the afflicted family. Rounding out the key roles are Erroll Shand as Edgar, Maude Davey as Polly, George Pullar as Will, and Victory Ndukwe as Leo.

## At the Box Office

Evil Dead Burn has grossed $20,650,512 at the domestic box office, 8 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 3,004 theaters. The film added $1,500,000 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $30 million. It carries a reported production budget of $20 million.
