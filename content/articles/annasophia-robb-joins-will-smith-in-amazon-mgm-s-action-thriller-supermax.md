AnnaSophia Robb is set to join [Will Smith](/celebrity/will-smiths-ex-friend-requests-his-testimony-in-jada-pinkett-smith-lawsuit/) in the upcoming Amazon MGM Studios and Miramax action thriller *Supermax*, according to Deadline. The actress will play Smith's young partner in the film, which is being directed by David Gordon Green.

The film follows two FBI agents who investigate a murder that’s taken place in the world’s most secure prison. Sources tell Deadline that Robb is set to play Smith's young partner as the two detectives enter the depths of a Supermax prison to solve a mysterious murder.

## Who is directing 'Supermax'?

David Gordon Green is directing the picture. The film is written by David Weil and David J. Rosen, known for their work on the TV series *Hunters* and *Invasion*. Weil and Rosen will also executive produce.

## Where will the movie be available?

Deadline reported in May that Amazon MGM Studios is closing a deal for worldwide distribution rights for the Miramax film, which will be available in more than 240 countries and territories on Prime Video. The Picture Company partners Andrew Rona & Alex Heineman will produce alongside Will Smith and Adam Fishbach for Smith’s Westbrook.

## What is AnnaSophia Robb's next project?

Robb's next leading role is in Amazon MGM’s biographical drama *I Play Rocky*, directed by Peter Farrelly. The film follows the behind-the-scenes true story of the production of the seminal 1976 classic *Rocky* and is set to open this year’s Toronto Film Festival. She is also set to join the recurring cast of the hit Prime Video comedy *Overcompensating* for its second season.
