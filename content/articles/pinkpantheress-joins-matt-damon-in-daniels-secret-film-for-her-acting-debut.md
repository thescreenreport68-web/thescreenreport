PinkPantheress is set to make her feature film acting debut in the upcoming secret project from directing duo Daniels at Universal, according to Deadline. The Grammy-nominated British recording artist joins a previously announced ensemble cast that includes Matt Damon, Sandra Oh, and Charles Melton.

The film, which remains shrouded in secrecy, is being produced by Daniels and Jonathan Wang through their company Playgrounds. Universal executives Sara Scott and Jacqueline Garell are overseeing the project for the studio. The movie is slated to open in theaters on November 19, 2027.

Universal had no comment regarding PinkPantheress's involvement in the project.

## Who else is in the cast?

PinkPantheress joins a previously announced ensemble that includes actors Matt Damon, Sandra Oh, Charles Melton, Sean Kaufman, Silvia Dionicio, Jackson Kelly, Kerrice Brooks, Thalia Dudek, and Michael Gandolfini. The Daniels, the Oscar-winning directing team behind *Everything Everywhere All at Once*, are at the helm.

## What is PinkPantheress known for?

The artist, whose real name is Victoria Walker, rose to prominence in 2021 with her R&B and alternative pop tracks shared on platforms like TikTok and SoundCloud. Her eight-song album *Fancy That* earned her two Grammy nominations this year: Best Dance Pop Recording for "Illegal" and Best Dance/Electronic Album. She also won the BRIT Award for Producer of the Year, becoming the first woman and youngest ever recipient of that honor at age 25.
