A Fresh Nightmare for the Evil Dead Franchise

Souheila Yacoub leads the latest chapter in the iconic horror saga, '[Evil Dead Burn](/movies/evil-dead-burn-box-office-day-6-domestic-total-climbs-to-18-1-million/),' a chilling sequel that introduces a new family to the franchise's signature brand of terror.

Directed by Sébastien Vaniček, 'Evil Dead Burn' is a 110-minute horror-thriller that rekindles the classic Evil Dead premise. The story follows Alice, a woman reeling from her husband's sudden death. Seeking comfort, she retreats to the home of her in-laws, only to witness a horrifying transformation as they are possessed one by one, turning into the franchise's infamous Deadites. The film explores a grim new vow: that the bonds of marriage can persist even in the face of demonic annihilation.

### The Cast and Their Characters

The ensemble cast is led by Souheila Yacoub as the grieving widow, Alice. She is joined by Tandi Wright as Susan, Hunter Doohan as Joseph, and Luciane Buchanan as Thya, portraying the family members whose descent into evil forms the core of the horror. Erroll Shand plays Edgar, with Maude Davey as Polly, George Pullar as Will, and Victory Ndukwe rounding out the cast as Leo. Together, they face a nightmare that tests the limits of familial loyalty.
