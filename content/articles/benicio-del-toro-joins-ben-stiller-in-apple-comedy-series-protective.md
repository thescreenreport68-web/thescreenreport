Benicio Del Toro is set to star in the upcoming Apple TV+ comedy series *Protective Custody* alongside Ben Stiller. This marks the Oscar-winning actor's first leading role in a comedy series.

The project reunites Del Toro with Stiller, who will also executive produce the show. The two previously collaborated on the Showtime limited series *Escape at Dannemora*.

## Who is making 'Protective Custody'?

The series comes from creators [Mike Judge](/tv/mike-judge-s-king-of-the-hill-returns-for-15th-season-on-hulu-after-1-revival/), Steve Hely, and Dave King, who will co-write, co-showrun, and executive produce. Judge is also attached to direct. The show hails from Apple Studios, Stiller's Red Hour, Propagate Content, and 3 Arts Entertainment.

## What is the show about?

*Protective Custody* will follow "a disgraced financier accused of massive fraud who is thrown into protective custody, where he must navigate prison politics, salvage his reputation and confront the consequences of his actions while awaiting trial," according to the official synopsis. Character details for Del Toro's role are being kept under wraps.

## When was the series announced?

The project was first announced in May with Stiller attached to star and executive produce. Del Toro's casting was reported in July 2026.
