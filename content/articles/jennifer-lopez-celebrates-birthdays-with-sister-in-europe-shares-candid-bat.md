Jennifer Lopez shared an intimate, unfiltered look at her recent European birthday vacation with her sister, including a photo of herself nude in a bathtub. The singer posted the images on Instagram on July 19, according to E! News, offering a glimpse into her celebrations with younger sibling Lynda Lopez.

In one photo, Lopez is pictured nude in a bathtub eating a dessert, while Lynda sits on the bathroom floor snacking on her own dish. The sisters were marking a double birthday: Lynda turned 55 on June 14, and Jennifer will turn 57 on July 24. "A double birthday sister trip is always a good idea," she wrote in the caption, adding, "I love you Lynnie!! Happy birthday!!"

## Parisian Glamour

The trip included sightseeing at La Galerie Dior in Paris and a dinner where the pair were surprised with two birthday cakes. Lopez's style was predictably extravagant. For her birthday dinner, she wore over 400 carats of diamonds from Chopard, including a statement necklace with over 190 carats and a 73.69-carat bracelet.

No further details about the trip or future posts have been confirmed.
