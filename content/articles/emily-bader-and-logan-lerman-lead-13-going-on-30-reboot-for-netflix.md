Netflix is officially bringing a new version of the beloved 2004 fantasy rom-com to its service. According to the streamer, Emily Bader and Logan Lerman will lead the cast of a reimagined *13 Going on 30*, directed by Brett Haley.

[Jennifer Garner](/movies/jennifer-garner-and-judy-greer-spotted-on-set-of-netflix-s-13-going-on-30/), who starred as Jenna Rink in the original film directed by Gary Winick, is executive producing the project. Haley expressed his excitement for the pairing of his two leads. "She and the amazingly talented Logan Lerman are a magical pairing," the director said.

## Who else is in the cast?

The film has assembled a large, star-studded ensemble to support Bader and Lerman. The announced cast includes Jessica Alba, Taylor Zakhar Perez, Owen Thiele, Nasim Pedrad, and Supriya Ganesh. Tim Meadows and Dan Bucatinsky, who co-starred in the series *Our Little Secret*, are also set to appear.

## What is the creative team behind the reboot?

Brett Haley, whose previous Netflix film *People We Meet on Vacation* spent weeks on the service's global Top 10 list, is directing. The screenplay was written by Hannah Marks, with revisions by Flora Greeson. Haley called the original film "one of those rare, perfect films" and acknowledged the "tremendous responsibility" of stepping into the reimagining, especially with Garner's involvement.

The film is produced by Joe Roth and Jeff Kirschenbaum for RK Films. Executive producers include Garner, Donna Roth, Susan Arnold, and screenwriter Marks.
