PART 1 — THE MOVIE

Olivia Wilde leads the charge in *[Obsession](/movies/obsession-box-office-day-66-adds-730-000-as-domestic-total-hits-258-5-million/)*, the 2026 horror sensation that has shocked the box office. Directed by Curry Barker, the 109-minute horror-thriller follows a hopeless romantic who, after breaking the mysterious "One Wish Willow" to win his crush's heart, gets exactly what he asked for—only to discover that some desires come at a dark, sinister price.

The ensemble cast brings this chilling premise to life. Michael Johnston stars as Bear, the romantic protagonist, with Inde Navarrette as his crush, Nikki. Cooper Tomlinson and Megan Lawless play Ian and Sarah, respectively, with supporting turns from Andy Richter as Carter, Haley Fitzgerald as Viola, Darin Toonder as Harry, and Anthony Pavone as Reggie.
