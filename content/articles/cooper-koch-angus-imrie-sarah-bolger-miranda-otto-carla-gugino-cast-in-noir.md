Cooper Koch, Angus Imrie, Sarah Bolger, Miranda Otto, and Carla Gugino have joined the cast of the new noir drama film 'Fog City', according to Deadline. The project is currently in production under the direction of Dan Pritzker.

Set during the construction of the Golden Gate Bridge in Depression-era San Francisco, the film blends historical fact and fiction. It examines the interwoven stories of an underground abortionist (Gugino), a police inspector (Koch), a Dust Bowl transplant (Bolger), a blackmailing socialite (Otto), and her son (Imrie).

## Who is directing 'Fog City'?

Dan Pritzker is directing from his own script and producing the film alongside Jon Cornick and Michele Tayler. Pritzker told Deadline, “It’s a privilege to work on a story about San Francisco, a city I love, and to bring together such a great group of actors. I’m excited to work with such a talented cast and crew to tell this story.” The film is shooting through October between San Francisco and Melbourne.

## Who else is in the cast?

The supporting cast includes Perry ‘Pez’ Warner, Les Hill, Johnny Carr, Robert Coleby, Russell Dykstra, and David Wenham.
