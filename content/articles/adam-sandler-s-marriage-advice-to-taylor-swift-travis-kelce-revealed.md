He elaborated on Sandler's philosophy: "It’s hard to argue when you give your wife a kiss or your wife gives you a kiss. Make sure you do it every day, every minute that you have an opportunity to do it. Knock it out, and you won't have problems."

"In a simple, hysterical way, he was phenomenal," the 68-year-old coach said. "He's crazy, but he did a great job in getting them married with a lot of humor."

Swift herself gave the film a rave review on her Instagram story last July, writing, "Happy Gilmore 2 had me cackling and cheering the whole movie!"
