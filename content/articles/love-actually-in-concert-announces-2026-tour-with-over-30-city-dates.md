The “Love Actually in Concert” tour will visit more than 30 cities across the United States and Canada during the 2026 holiday season, according to Billboard. A live orchestra will perform the film’s soundtrack while the complete 2003 movie is projected on a screen.

## When do tickets go on sale?

Presales for the tour begin on Wednesday, July 22, 2026. General public ticket sales start on July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.

## What is the concert format?

The event promises to bring the “heart, humor and music” of Richard Curtis’s holiday classic to life. The film’s soundtrack, which includes Mariah Carey’s “All I Want for Christmas Is You” and Craig Armstrong’s score, will be performed live. The tour is mounted by production companies GEA Live, RoadCo, and Fifth Season.
