The Story of the King of Pop

Directed by Antoine Fuqua, '[Michael](/movies/michael-box-office-day-87-adds-45-000-as-domestic-total-hits-372-2-million/)' is a 128-minute music drama that tells the story of Michael Jackson, one of the world's most influential artists. The film chronicles his journey from the discovery of his extraordinary talent as the lead singer of the Jackson Five to his relentless pursuit to become the biggest entertainer in the world. It highlights his life off-stage alongside some of the most iconic performances from his early solo career.

## A Family Affair On Screen

The film features a stellar cast led by Michael Jackson's own nephew, Jaafar Jackson, who steps into the iconic role of Michael. Colman Domingo and Nia Long portray his parents, Joseph and Katherine Jackson, while Juliano Krue Valdi plays a young Michael. The supporting cast includes KeiLyn Durrel Jones as bodyguard Bill Bray, Miles Teller as attorney John Branca, Larenz Tate as Motown founder Berry Gordy, and Laura Harrier as talent manager Suzanne de Passe. This ensemble brings the complex world of the Jackson family and the music industry to vivid life.
