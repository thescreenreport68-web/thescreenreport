Ever wonder what it's like to help someone get ready for the celebrity event of the year? Ashley Williams just gave us a peek.

The Hallmark Channel actress shared an Instagram video on Friday, July 3, showing her dancing around a room in New York City while her sister, Kimberly Williams-Paisley, got dressed. The occasion? Kimberly and her husband, Brad Paisley, were headed to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding, according to a report from Just Jared.

Ashley, who was not a guest, leaned into the humor of the situation. She captioned the post, "My invitation might have gotten lost in the mail, no idea why."

The video offers a fun, familial glimpse into the lead-up to the high-profile nuptials. Brad Paisley has a long history with Swift, having mentored her early in her career nearly two decades ago.

According to the Just Jared report, the wedding week included significant charitable giving. Swift and Kelce made donations, including a major gift to The Store, the Paisleys' charity in Tennessee, which provides free groceries to food-insecure families.

While Ashley Williams's role was strictly as a supportive sister (and videographer), her post sparked a wave of fan appreciation online for the lighthearted, behind-the-scenes moment. It's a reminder that even at the most A-list events, there's often a sibling in the background making sure everything—and everyone—looks just right.
