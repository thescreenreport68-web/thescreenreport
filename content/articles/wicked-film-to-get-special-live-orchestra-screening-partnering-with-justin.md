Universal Studios announced a special concert experience featuring the 2024 film *Wicked* accompanied by a live orchestra, according to a report from Billboard. The event, titled "Wicked in Concert: Film With Live Orchestra," was announced on Thursday, July 23, 2026.

It comes in partnership with La La Land composer and conductor Justin Hurwitz's company.

The news was shared on the franchise's official Instagram account and by Hurwitz Concerts. A promotional poster featured the hands of leading stars Cynthia Erivo and [Ariana Grande](/celebrity/ariana-grande-and-ricky-alvarez-are-the-real-deal-source-says/). The caption played on one of Grande's lines from the films: "Everyone, we have an announcement…"

Hurwitz's company has previously organized live orchestra-backed screenings for films like *La La Land*, *Babylon*, and *Whiplash*. For the *Wicked* films, the music was created by Stephen Schwartz, who worked with John Powell on the film adaptation of the Broadway score.

## What is the 'Wicked' concert experience?

The announced event is a concert experience that will pair a screening of the 2024 film *Wicked* with a live orchestra performance. Specific details, including dates and locations, are being kept under wraps for now, with the announcement simply stating the experience is "coming soon."

## How successful was the 'Wicked' film?

The first *Wicked* film premiered in November 2024 and was a major commercial and critical success. According to Billboard, it grossed a record $758.1 million globally and reached No. 2 on the Billboard 200 chart with its soundtrack. The film also earned significant Oscar buzz, winning Academy Awards for Best Costume Design and Best Production Design. Both Cynthia Erivo and Ariana Grande received acting nominations for their performances.

A sequel, *Wicked: For Good*, arrived almost exactly a year later in 2025. The live orchestra event continues the franchise's momentum following the conclusion of the two-film saga last fall.
