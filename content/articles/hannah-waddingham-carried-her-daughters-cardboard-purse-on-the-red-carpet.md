I’m about to go away to do press for the next season of _Ted_[_Lasso_], and the mummy guilt descends. But I have to try and combat it." Waddingham also admitted to having "The other part was going, I’ve got terrible separation anxiety. So when I watch it back, I just see a woman who doesn’t know whether she’s coming or going."

Waddingham carried a handbag made of cardboard that her daughter had handcrafted.
