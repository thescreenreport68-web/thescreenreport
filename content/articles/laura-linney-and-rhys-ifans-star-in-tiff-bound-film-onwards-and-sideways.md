Laura Linney and Rhys Ifans star in the upcoming film 'Onwards and Sideways,' which is set to world premiere at the 2026 [Toronto International Film Festival](/movies/tiff-unveils-special-presentations-lineup-for-2026-festival/), according to The Hollywood Reporter. The outlet also exclusively unveiled new images from the drama.

## Who else is in the cast?

Joining the leads are Monica Dolan and Rory Kinnear. Dolan plays Liz Evans, while Kinnear takes on the role of Professor Ridge. Writer Paul Mayhew-Archer also appears among the cast.

## What is the film about?

Linney plays Emma Dretzin, a musician and single mother. Ifans portrays Tony Evans, a deputy head of a primary school. The two characters, who do not know each other, have their lives changed by "a startling encounter" on the same morning they are both confronted with a diagnosis of Parkinson's Disease. The film follows their unexpected friendship.

Described as "emotional and surprisingly funny," the project is directed by John Madden and based on a script by Mayhew-Archer. Following its TIFF premiere, the film is scheduled for a theatrical release in the U.K. and U.S. ahead of its debut on BBC iPlayer and BBC One.
