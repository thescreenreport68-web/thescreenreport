The album ‘Petal’ is scheduled for release on July 31.

## What has she released from the album so far?

The album’s rollout began earlier this summer with the lead single ‘hate that I made you love me.’ That track debuted at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in June, according to the report. The new teaser for ‘Petal’ continues the album's promotional cycle, offering fans a first visual glimpse of the project’s aesthetic.

## When does the album come out?

*Petal* is officially set to arrive on July 31. The new teaser does not specify a release date for the full music video, focusing instead on the album's imminent arrival. Grande’s official social media posts serve as the primary source for this latest preview.
