How do you bring your husband to one of the most exclusive seats at Wimbledon? For Priyanka Chopra, the answer was a video call.

According to a report from Just Jared, the 43-year-old actress was spotted FaceTiming with Nick Jonas while sitting in the royal box during day 11 of the tennis tournament on Thursday, July 9, in London, England. Chopra was there to watch Coco Gauff's match against Karolina Muchova, which the American player ultimately lost in a close finish.

Fans caught a glimpse of the sweet moment when Chopra turned her phone around to give Jonas a view of the court. The impromptu broadcast also gave onlookers a peek at Jonas himself. Chopra was dressed for the occasion in Ralph Lauren, accessorized with Mociun hoop earrings.

The sighting comes as Chopra's professional schedule heats up. The film's premise, as reported by the outlet, follows "a woman (Chopra) who wakes up in the middle of the wilderness days from civilization with no memory."
