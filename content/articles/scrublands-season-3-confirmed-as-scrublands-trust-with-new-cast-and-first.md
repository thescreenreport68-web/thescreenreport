The Australian crime drama *Scrublands* is officially returning for a third season, according to an exclusive report from Deadline. The new installment will be titled *Scrublands: Trust*.

Luke Arnold and Bella Heathcote will reprise their leading roles as investigative reporter Martin Scarsden and Mandy Bond, respectively. They are joined by new cast members Lincoln Younes and Danielle Cormack, with Sarah Roberts and Nicholas Bell also returning.

## What is the plot of the new season?

*Scrublands: Trust* will jump the action back a decade from previous seasons. The synopsis reveals that Mandy Bond's past resurfaces when her former fiancé, Tom Molloy (played by Younes), is found dead, unearthing secrets she wants to keep hidden. Meanwhile, Martin Scarsden investigates the apparent suicide of his mentor, Max Fuller (Bell), a search that leads him into Sydney's criminal underbelly and a violent confrontation in the Blue Mountains.

## When does filming start?

Production on the four-part season has already commenced in Sydney. The series is produced by Easy Tiger, based on Chris Hammer's bestselling novel *Trust*, and will air on Australian streamer Stan and broadcast network 9Network.

In a statement, Easy Tiger co-founders and executive producers Ian Collie and Rob Gibson said, "The continued enthusiasm for this series is a testament to Chris Hammer’s compelling stories and the extraordinary work of our cast and creative team." Felicity Packard serves as writer and producer, with Lisa Matthews directing.
