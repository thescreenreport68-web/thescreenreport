The movie is directed by Lance Oppenheim and will premiere at the [Venice Film Festival](/movies/margot-robbie-andy-serkis-join-venice-film-festival-s-immersive-lineup/).

## What is 'Primetime' about?

The film follows Chris Hansen in 2006 as he sets out to make television history with the NBC Dateline series 'To Catch a Predator'. Pattinson plays the correspondent who gained fame for confronting suspected child predators using hidden camera stings and online decoys.

## Who else is involved?

Merritt Wever, Skyler Gisondo, and Phoebe Bridgers also star in the project. Pattinson produced the film alongside Brighton McCloskey through their company Icki Eneo Arlo, with additional producers including Brian Kavanaugh-Jones and Fred Berger.

## When will it be in theaters?

It will face competition that weekend from several other major releases, including Paramount's 'Heart of the Beast' starring Brad Pitt and Disney's 'Avengers: Endgame Encore'.
