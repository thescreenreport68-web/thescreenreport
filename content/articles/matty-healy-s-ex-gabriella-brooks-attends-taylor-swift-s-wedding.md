Who got an invite to the wedding of the decade? Matty Healy's ex-girlfriend Gabriella Brooks was among the guests at Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's nuptials, according to E! News.

The model, who dated The 1975 frontman for four years before Swift's brief 2023 romance with him, attended the "Love Story" singer's wedding with her fiancé Liam Hemsworth at Madison Square Garden in New York City on July 3. Brooks shared details from the event on Instagram two days later.

"The night of our lives," Brooks wrote in her July 5 Instagram post, alongside photos of the couple in their wedding attire. She added, "Congratulations T[and]T you are a match made in heaven."

For the occasion, Brooks wore a mauve halter-neck gown with feathered accents, while Hemsworth, 36, opted for a black tuxedo. The 30-year-old model also shared a picture of the custom-made handkerchiefs gifted to guests, which featured a "Blank Space" lyric: "So it's gonna be forever."

Brooks and Hemsworth are longtime Swift fans. They were reportedly among the stars who saw the "I Can Do It With a Broken Heart" singer at one of her London concerts, watching the epic show alongside Kelce and other celebrities in the VIP tent.

Healy, who is now engaged to fiancée Gabbriette, is also on Swift's list of ex-lovers.

Brooks's presence at the wedding highlights the interconnected world of Swift's personal and professional life, where past and present relationships can share a dance floor.
