The Movie
Woody, Buzz Lightyear, and their toy-box family are back for a high-stakes new adventure in *[Toy Story 5](/movies/toy-story-5-box-office-day-26-domestic-total-climbs-to-410-6-million/)*. The film sees the beloved characters facing their most modern challenge yet: when Bonnie receives a Lilypad tablet as a gift and becomes obsessed, the gang's job becomes exponentially harder as they go head-to-head with this all-new digital threat to playtime. Directed by Andrew Stanton, this 102-minute Animation, Family, Comedy, and Adventure outing delivers the heart and humor fans expect.

Tom Hanks returns as the loyal cowboy Woody, with Tim Allen once again voicing the intrepid space ranger Buzz Lightyear. Joan Cusack reprises her role as the spirited cowgirl Jessie. The new digital world introduces Greta Lee as the voice of the Lilypad tablet, while Conan O'Brien plays Smarty Pants, Craig Robinson is Atlas, Shelby Rabara is Snappy, and Tony Hale returns as the anxious spork Forky.

## The Box Office Story
*Toy Story 5* is proving to be a monumental success at the **Toy Story 5 box office**.

Playing in 3,575 theaters, the film has demonstrated exceptional theatrical strength.

## At the Box Office

Toy Story 5 has grossed $413,321,921 at the domestic box office, 27 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 3,575 theaters. The film added $2,715,354 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $882 million. It carries a reported production budget of $250 million.
