The Primetime sidebar, which showcases episodic television, will open with Netflix’s six-part supernatural thriller *Below* and close with Flanagan’s *[Carrie](/tv/prime-video-debuts-first-look-at-mike-flanagan-s-carrie-series/)* for Prime Video.

## What is the Primetime program?

The Primetime program is a festival sidebar dedicated to serialized TV storytelling. It typically screens one or two episodes of each selected series and hosts informal conversations with the cast and creators in Toronto.

## Which other shows are premiering?

In addition to *Carrie* and *Crystal Lake*, the lineup includes world premieres for Shawn Ryan’s psychological thriller *[American Hostage](/tv/american-hostage-anthology-series-starring-jon-hamm-sets-world-premiere-at/)*, the Australian true crime documentary *2.6 Seconds: Death in the Outback*, and the series *Alice* starring Bérénice Bejo. Toronto will also host an international premiere for the latest season of Apple TV’s *Slow Horses* and a North American bow for the Brazilian series *Oxygen Masks Will (Not) Drop Automatically*.

The festival previously announced that *CODA* filmmaker Sian Heder will open the 51st edition with her film *Being Heumann*.
