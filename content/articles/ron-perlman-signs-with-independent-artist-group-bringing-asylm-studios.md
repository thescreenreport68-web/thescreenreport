Ron Perlman has signed with Independent Artist Group, according to an exclusive report from The Hollywood Reporter. The agency will also represent the veteran actor’s production company, Asylm Studios.

Andrew Rogers, head of global talent, and partner Kris Heller issued a statement welcoming the actor. "Ron Perlman is one of the most respected and accomplished actors of his generation," they said. "His body of work speaks for itself, spanning iconic performances in film and television that have resonated with audiences worldwide. We are thrilled to welcome Ron to the IAG family and look forward to helping shape the next chapter of his extraordinary career."

## Who is Ron Perlman?

Ron Perlman is best known for his starring role as the title character in Guillermo del Toro's two *Hellboy* films. His portrayal of biker gang leader Clay Morrow on FX's *Sons of Anarchy* also cemented his status as a television icon. His frequent collaborations with del Toro include roles in *Blade II*, *Pacific Rim*, and *Nightmare Alley*.

## What is his recent work?

Perlman has maintained a prolific pace in recent years. His recent television appearances include acclaimed series like *Mr. & Mrs. Smith*, *Fallout*, and *Poker Face*. As of July 2026, he can be seen in the Apple TV+ series *Cape Fear*. His upcoming film projects listed in his verified filmography include *The Gentleman*, *Day of the Fight*, and *Succubus*.

## What does this agency change mean?

Signing with a new agency typically signals a fresh focus on securing future projects and partnerships. With IAG also representing Asylm Studios, the move suggests an integrated strategy for both Perlman's acting career and his production endeavors. According to the report, Perlman continues to be represented by his management firm, Link Entertainment.
