What’s the best way to get a celebrity’s attention? For one Benson Boone fan, it was a sign that hilariously acknowledged the singer’s rumored link to Alix Earle.

According to Just Jared, Earle reposted a TikTok video from a fan at Boone’s ‘Wanted Man Tour’ concert. The clip, posted by user @sweenerin, showed the fan holding up a sign that read, “Not Alix but blonde with fake boobs too.” The video captured Boone noticing the sign and pointing at the fan, which drew a big reaction from the crowd. The fan captioned her post, “My DMs are open.”

Earle didn’t comment, but she hit the “repost” button, sharing the fan’s joke with her own massive following. The sign was a clear nod to the romance rumors that swirled last month after Earle and Boone were photographed stepping out for dinner together in West Hollywood. Those rumors were later clarified when it was revealed Earle was starring in one of Boone’s music videos, playing his love interest.

While the fan’s sign and Earle’s repost have set tongues wagging again, the current status of their relationship remains unconfirmed.

Boone is currently on his ‘Wanted Man Tour,’ which reportedly launched recently. The fan’s creative sign and Earle’s playful acknowledgment prove that even clarified rumors can have a long, and funny, shelf life in the world of celebrity gossip. The source also notes that Boone launched his concert tour and that a fan hilariously referenced the rumors while attending his concert, which got Alix’s attention and was also noticed by Benson during the show.
