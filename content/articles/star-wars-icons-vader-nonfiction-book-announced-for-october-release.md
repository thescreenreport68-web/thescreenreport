The nonfiction book *Star Wars Icons: Vader* will be released on October 20, 2026, according to Star Wars News Net. The book is authored by reporter Anthony Breznican and features a foreword by actor Hayden Christensen.

A preview of the first ten pages was revealed by Gizmodo. The nearly 500-page book from Insight Editions collects behind-the-scenes photos, concept art, and memorabilia exploring the character's legacy. The publisher states it "covers the character’s journey, from his genesis in George Lucas’s first drafts of *Star Wars* to Anakin Skywalker’s tragic fall to the dark side in the prequel trilogy and beyond."

## What does the author say about the book?

Anthony Breznican told Gizmodo that writing the book revealed a central theme. "While writing this, I realized that the story of Vader is the story of many fathers," he said. "Everyone has a dad, and that person shapes your life whether you like it or not." He noted that everyone involved in bringing Darth Vader to life had powerful fatherhood stories that impacted their work on the character.

The book also features interviews with high-profile creators and fans, including director Steven Spielberg and screenwriter Lawrence Kasdan. Breznican described Spielberg's memories of being among the first to see *Star Wars* and Kasdan's reflections on writing *The Empire Strikes Back* and *Return of the Jedi*.

## How can I get the book?

Lucasfilm Publishing revealed the book will come in two formats. A standard edition will be priced at $250, and a limited edition will cost $450. The limited edition includes a specially-made cover and a bas-relief slipcase.

The book will be available where all books are sold. Attendees of the upcoming San Diego Comic-Con will be able to see it at the Star Wars publishing booth.
