Annemarie Wiley's former 'Real Housewives of Beverly Hills' castmates are reportedly watching her divorce battle with sympathy from afar, even though they've lost touch.

She joined the show as a friend and neighbor to Kyle Richards and also shared a friendly rapport with Erika Jayne, though she often clashed with Sutton Stracke and Crystal Kung Minkoff. According to the outlet, there hasn't been communication between Annemarie and her former castmates since her departure.

Despite the distance, sources tell TMZ there's a great deal of sympathy for Annemarie in the wake of her split from Marcellus Wiley. The feeling is that whatever happened on camera has been set aside, and this is about Annemarie and her three children.

This support comes in the wake of serious allegations detailed in Annemarie's court filings. She is seeking custody of their three children, exclusive use of their Los Angeles home, and spousal support.

According to TMZ's sources, those who knew Annemarie through the show were shocked by the allegations and hope she gets whatever help and protection she needs. The report follows news that Annemarie filed for divorce and requested a restraining order after Marcellus was arrested over a weekend in Florida on a domestic violence charge stemming from an alleged hotel incident. Marcellus has denied the allegations.

While the cast's reported feelings are one thing, their direct involvement appears to be another. The report suggests any past on-screen drama has been eclipsed by the real-life legal battle now unfolding.
