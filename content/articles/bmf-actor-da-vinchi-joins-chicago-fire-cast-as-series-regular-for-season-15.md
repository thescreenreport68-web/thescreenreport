Da'Vinchi has joined the series regular cast of NBC’s *Chicago Fire* for its upcoming 15th season, according to Deadline. The actor will play Marcus Burke, a firefighter candidate at Firehouse 51.

He joins returning stars Taylor Kinney, David Eigenberg, Miranda Rae Mayo, Christian Stolte, Joe Miñoso, Jocelyn Hudon, and Brandon Larracuente.

## Who is Da'Vinchi playing?

Da'Vinchi's character, Marcus Burke, is described as roguish, funny, with a wry sense of humor, and also a little defiant.

## What else is new for Season 15?

Victor Terharn will take over as showrunner for the new season. Season 14 ended on a major cliffhanger with the crew of Firehouse 51 responding to a dangerous fire at a storage facility. A logline for the new season has yet to be released. The series is executive produced by Dick Wolf and follows the lives of firefighters, rescuers, and paramedics in Chicago.

## Where can I watch Chicago Fire?

According to its official listing, *Chicago Fire* is currently streaming on fuboTV, Peacock Premium, YouTube TV, NBC, USA Network, and Peacock Premium Plus. The series first aired in 2012.
