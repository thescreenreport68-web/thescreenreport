What does a Taylor Swift wedding look like? According to a press release from her publicist, it involves a legendary New York City venue, a thousand guests, and a custom couture gown from one of fashion's hottest designers.

Taylor Swift, 36, and Travis Kelce, 36, are officially married. The singer's publicist, Tree Paine, confirmed the couple tied the knot on Friday, July 3, at Madison Square Garden in New York City. The news came packaged with a trove of fashion details that set the style world abuzz.

The bride and groom's wedding ceremony looks were created by Christian Dior Haute Couture and designed by Jonathan Anderson, Creative Director of Dior Women’s, Men’s and Haute Couture Collections. The press release noted this is "the designer’s first couture wedding dress for a world-renowned celebrity." Their shoes were custom-made by Christian Louboutin, and Swift wore Cartier jewelry for the occasion.
