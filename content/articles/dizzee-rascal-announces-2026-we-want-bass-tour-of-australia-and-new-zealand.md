Dizzee Rascal is heading back to Australia and New Zealand for a headline tour in late 2026, according to Billboard. The grime pioneer will bring his 'We Want Bass' tour to seven cities this November and December.

He won’t be alone. The run will feature U.K. dance act Example and rapper Arrdee on most dates, with Perth DJ Swaglord Savannah also joining the bill. The supporting lineup varies by city, creating a slightly different show in each location.

## What are the tour dates and venues?

The tour kicks off at Perth HPC on November 26, 2026. It then travels to Brisbane’s Eatons Hill Outdoors on November 28, Adelaide’s Hindley Street Music Hall on December 1, and Melbourne’s Royal Botanic Gardens on December 4. The Sydney Opera House Forecourt hosts a show on December 5, followed by a stop at Byron Bay’s Beach Hotel on December 6. The tour concludes at Auckland’s Shed 10 on December 8.

## Who is performing at each show?

The full four-artist bill—featuring Dizzee Rascal, Example, Arrdee, and Swaglord Savannah—will play in Perth, Brisbane, Melbourne, and Sydney. The Adelaide and Auckland dates will feature only Arrdee alongside the headliner. The Byron Bay show will be a two-artist event with Dizzee Rascal and Swaglord Savannah.

## When do tickets go on sale?

Mastercard and Live Nation presales will run from July 8 to July 9, 2026. General ticket sales begin on July 9 at 1 p.m. local time. Tickets will be available through livenation.com.au and livenation.co.nz.

The tour announcement marks a significant return for Dizzee Rascal, born Dylan Mills, whose 2003 debut *Boy in da Corner* won the Mercury Prize. His commercial peak came with 2009’s *Tongue n’ Cheek*, which spawned four consecutive U.K. No. 1 singles.
