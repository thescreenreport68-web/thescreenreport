The first trailer for *[Avengers: Doomsday](/movies/marvel-drops-ominous-avengers-doomsday-teaser-full-trailer-expected-this/)* has officially arrived, sending Marvel fans into a frenzy over its villainous voice and returning heroes.

According to TheWrap, the Monday morning release features an ominous warning that “Something’s coming,” a line many have immediately attributed to Robert Downey Jr.'s Victor von Doom. Social media lit up with praise for the actor's distinct vocal performance. "Glad to hear RDJ doesn’t just sound like Iron man," one fan wrote.

## What are the biggest moments fans are talking about?

The trailer hints at a major showdown between Chris Hemsworth’s Thor and Doctor Doom, with fans drawing direct parallels to the hero's iconic charge against Thanos. Another seismic moment is the return of Chris Evans as Steve Rogers, who proves his worthiness by summoning Thor’s hammer. The reaction was immediate: "No way Thor testing to see if Steve was real or fake handing over the mijolnir HOLY PEAK," a user posted. While excitement is high, some fans noted a perceived lack of build-up for Doom, whose only prior MCU appearance is slated for *The Fantastic Four: First Steps*.

## What is Avengers: Doomsday about?

Marvel released an official synopsis with the trailer, framing the massive crossover. “In ‘Avengers: Doomsday,’ beloved heroes from three distinct universes will be set on a deadly collision course and face an existential threat unlike anything they’ve ever encountered.” The film is directed by Joe Russo and features a sprawling cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Chris Hemsworth, Pedro Pascal, Paul Rudd, and Anthony Mackie.
