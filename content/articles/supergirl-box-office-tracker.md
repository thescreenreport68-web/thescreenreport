Milly Alcock takes flight as the Girl of Steel in a galactic quest for justice. 'Supergirl' is a 108-minute action-adventure sci-fi film directed by Craig Gillespie. The premise follows Kara Zor-El, aka [Supergirl](/movies/supergirl-box-office-day-26-adds-335-587-as-domestic-total-hits-70-5-million/), who is forced to team up with an unlikely ally when a ruthless adversary strikes close to home, launching them on an epic interstellar journey of vengeance.

The cast is a blend of rising stars and established names. Milly Alcock leads as Supergirl/Kara Zor-El. She is joined by Eve Ridley as Ruthye and Matthias Schoenaerts as Krem. The film also features major DC Universe players, with Jason Momoa appearing as the interstellar bounty hunter Lobo and David Corenswet reprising his role as Superman. Supporting roles are filled by David Krumholtz as Zor-El, Emily Beecham as Alura In-Ze, and Kadiff Kirwan as Bomar Vran. The film aimed to carve a new path for the character in a crowded superhero landscape.
