Rihanna and A$AP Rocky were photographed leaving the FIFA World Cup Final in East Rutherford, New Jersey, according to images published by Just Jared. The superstar couple joined a star-studded lineup of celebrity guests watching Argentina face Spain in the championship game.

For the sporty evening out, Rihanna dressed in a chocolate football jersey top from Vetements and a pair of Aflalo’s mid-rise cropped Bromer shorts in shade bronze. She paired the outfit with suede stiletto knee-high boots, a surprising choice considering temperatures reached the 80s that Sunday. She was seen holding a Shark Chill Pill to keep cool in the heat.

A$AP Rocky complemented her look in a large white t-shirt, ball cap, dark denim jeans, and sneakers. Rihanna finished her ensemble with gold jewelry, and her hair in a ponytail. The pair’s appearance continues their trend of fashion-forward date nights, often noted for bold style choices.
