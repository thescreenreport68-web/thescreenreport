Josh Duhamel, James Franco, Paula Patton, and Summer H. Howell have wrapped filming on the new indie action-thriller "Runaway." According to Deadline, the project is directed by James C. Clayton and produced by IndustryWorks Studios.

## What is the plot of 'Runaway'?

The film follows veteran detective Lou Coleman, played by Duhamel. After the death of his estranged daughter, he takes a solitary drive into the wilderness. His path changes when he chooses to protect a teenage runaway, played by Howell, who is carrying evidence of the town's crimes. This decision puts him in the crosshairs of a corrupt small-town sheriff, portrayed by Franco, and his militia.

## Who else is in the cast and crew?

Adam Beach and Alex Essoe also star in the film. "Runaway" was written by Jason Carroll, Marc Petey, Danny Mac, and Paul Birkett. Jonnie Broi and Marc Petey produced for Play x Play Studios. Executive producers include Evan Tylor and Caterina Scrivano of IndustryWorks Studios, Keli Price of Price Productions, and Zach Steele.
