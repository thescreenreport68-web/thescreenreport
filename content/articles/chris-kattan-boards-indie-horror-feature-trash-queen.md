Chris Kattan has joined the cast of the indie horror feature *Trash Queen*, according to Deadline. This marks his first return to the horror genre since starring in the 1999 cult film *House on Haunted Hill*.

Kattan joins a cast that includes Matty Cardarople (*Stranger Things*, *Fallout*), Lynn Lowry (*The Crazies*, *Shivers*), Jonathan Stoddard (*The Roaring Games*), Kevin Keppy, Emily Tullock, and Bonne-Vejou Dorrell. The film is billed as a female horror comedy. Gabrielle Rosson directs from a screenplay she co-wrote with Emily Tullock.

## What is the movie about?

While plot details for *Trash Queen* are being kept under wraps, the project will utilize practical effects. Filming is scheduled to begin in October.

## What did Chris Kattan say?

In an exclusive statement to Deadline, Kattan expressed his enthusiasm. “I’m really excited to be stepping back into the horror genre,” Kattan said. “*House on Haunted Hill* has remained such a special film for me, and horror fans have always been incredibly loyal. *Trash Queen* has a fun, twisted energy that immediately drew me in, and I can’t wait for audiences to see what we’ve been cooking up.”

The film is being produced by Joan of Art LLC and Trash Queen LLC.
