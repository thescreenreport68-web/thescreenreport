## Who Is Seth Rogen?

He began his career as a stand-up comedian before moving to Los Angeles for a part in Judd Apatow's series *Freaks and Geeks* in 1999. Rogen landed a leading role in Apatow's comedy *Knocked Up* in 2007. His recent and upcoming film credits include *Kung Fu Panda 4* (2024), *Mufasa: The Lion King* (2024), *The Super Mario Bros.
