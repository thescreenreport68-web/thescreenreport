Alicia Witt is open to returning to Hallmark Channel after a six-year absence, she told fans on Instagram.

The actress, best known for her nine Hallmark rom-coms including *A Very Merry Mix-Up* and *Christmas Tree Lane*, took to Instagram over the weekend to celebrate Christmas in July. She shared a post about *A Very Merry Mix-Up* re-airing, writing, "It’s always a joy to revisit this special film, and I hope it brings a little Christmas cheer to your summer. Grab your favorite festive treat, get comfy, and enjoy!"

A fan then asked, "When will you make another?" Witt replied, "I remain open to making another, very much so! it’s been 6 years since my last one at @hallmarkchannel but i hear there may be new management now."

When another fan asked who to email at the network, Witt responded, "aww! i suppose the CEO?"

Witt's last Hallmark movie was 2020's *Christmas Tree Lane*. She has starred in nine rom-coms for the network.

While Witt's comment about potential new management adds industry intrigue, no official announcement has been made.
