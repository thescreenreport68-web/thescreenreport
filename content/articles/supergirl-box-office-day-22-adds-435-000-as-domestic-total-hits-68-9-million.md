Directed by Craig Gillespie, '[Supergirl](/movies/supergirl-box-office-day-20-adds-539-877-as-domestic-total-hits-68-million/) (2026)' is a 108-minute action-adventure sci-fi epic. The premise follows Kara Zor-El, aka Supergirl, who, after a ruthless adversary strikes close to home, reluctantly joins forces with an unlikely companion on a journey of vengeance and justice across the stars.

The cast is led by Milly Alcock as Supergirl, with Eve Ridley playing Ruthye, her unlikely companion. Matthias Schoenaerts portrays the adversary Krem, while Jason Momoa appears as the interstellar bounty hunter Lobo. David Krumholtz and Emily Beecham play Kara's parents, Zor-El and Alura In-Ze. David Corenswet appears as Superman, and Kadiff Kirwan plays Bomar Vran. The film is a bold new entry into the DC universe, aiming to carve its own path.

## The Box Office: A Historic Collapse

The financial performance of 'Supergirl' has been stark. It is now projected to officially earn the lowest fourth-weekend domestic take of any movie in either the DCEU or the DCU.

## At the Box Office

Supergirl has grossed $68,867,228 at the domestic box office, 22 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 1,572 theaters. The film added $435,000 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $120 million. It carries a reported production budget of $175 million.
