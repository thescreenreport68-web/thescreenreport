Ever tried to be nice to a fan, only to realize you've approached a complete stranger? Joe Jonas just lived that cringe-worthy moment, and he's spilling the details.

The 36-year-old singer recounted the awkward tale on the Thursday (July 2) episode of his podcast, *Hey Jonas!* with brothers Nick and Kevin Jonas. The story involved a quiet date with his girlfriend, Tatiana 'Tati' Gabriela, 28, that took an unexpected turn.

"So we’re sitting there, me and Tati. We’re having a nice glass of orange wine. Actually, I was having a Guinness. She was having an orange wine, and we were sharing a charcuterie board. It was actually just a pickle board," Joe said, giving a shoutout to Nick who he says is a big fan of pickles.

He turned her down because he was mid-conversation. After hanging up, feeling guilty, he spotted who he thought was the same woman still in the area.

"I turned around, and I was like, ‘Excuse me, let’s get that picture now,’" he continued. The response was not what he expected. "And she goes, ‘What?’ She’s like, ‘I didn’t ask you for a picture.’ I could have sworn it was the same person. So I just looked at a random person and I was like, ‘Hey, want to get that picture now?’"

The woman remained adamant she hadn't approached him. Joe's recovery attempt? Telling her, "You must have a doppelganger here."

The anecdote offers a rare, playful glimpse into Joe's relationship with Gabriela. The couple have been linked since summer 2025 and have been spotted together at events like Coachella and on a trip to Miami earlier this year. While they keep their romance relatively private, this podcast story shows a relaxed, humorous side to their time together.

Joe's podcast, a family affair with his brothers, often features personal stories and brotherly banter, making this lighthearted mix-up a perfect fit for the show's tone.
