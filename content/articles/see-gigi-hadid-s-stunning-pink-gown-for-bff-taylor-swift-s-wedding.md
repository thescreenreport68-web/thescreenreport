What does a soft-serve ice cream have to do with a star-studded wedding? Everything, if you're Gigi Hadid's hairstylist.

The first look at Hadid's wedding guest attire came not from a paparazzi shot, but from her hairstylist, Dimitris Giannetos. According to Just Jared, Giannetos shared photos of the 31-year-old model getting ready for Taylor Swift's wedding, wearing a pink Wiederhoeft gown.

Giannetos called the look a "soft serve blowout!!" He elaborated on Instagram, writing, "**Gigi** is wearing a gorgeous pink dress so I wanted to keep her hair very soft and sweet. I wanted to have this sweet feeling when u see **Gigi** same feeling when u have a soft serve ice cream :)," He added that he wanted the hair to be timeless with vintage waves and an extreme side part, aiming for a "film noir" star effect.

The photos offer a behind-the-scenes glimpse into the preparations for one of the most anticipated celebrity events. While the wedding itself has been the subject of intense speculation, this is a confirmed detail of a guest's look.

Just Jared noted they were spotted heading to the wedding together and also leaving at the end of the night.

The styling reveal fits the romantic, vintage-inspired aesthetic many predicted for Swift's wedding. Hadid, a longtime close friend of Swift's, was always expected to be in attendance. Her hairstylist's post confirms her presence and gives fans a taste of the event's fashion, even as full details of the ceremony remain private.
