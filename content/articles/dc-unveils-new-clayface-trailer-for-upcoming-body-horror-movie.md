DC Studios has dropped a fresh look at its upcoming monster movie, releasing a new trailer for the body horror film '[Clayface](/movies/new-clayface-trailer-drops-promising-body-horror-revenge-in-dcu/).' According to TechRadar, the trailer offers a glimpse of the Batman villain's terrifying first solo outing, which is set to premiere worldwide on October 23.

## What is the story of 'Clayface'?

The film follows the tragic story of Matt Hagen, a promising Hollywood actor whose meteoric rise is cut short. After being horribly disfigured by a local crime boss, he is abandoned by his team and left with nothing. Desperate, he seeks out a cutting-edge experimental serum that heals him but also unravels his grip on reality, sending him spiraling down a path of all-consuming revenge. The official synopsis describes it as a terrifying descent into hell, exploring loss of identity and the dark side of scientific ambition.

## Who stars in the film?

Tom Rhys Harries leads the cast as Matt Hagen, the man who becomes the shapeshifting monster Clayface. He is joined by a supporting cast that includes Naomi Ackie as Caitlyn Corr, Eddie Marsan, Max Minghella as John Borrelli, and David Dencik. The film is directed by James Watkins, known for horror films like *Speak No Evil* and *The Woman in Black*.

## How does it fit into the DC Universe?

'Clayface' is confirmed to be a part of James Gunn's DCU, specifically as the fifth project in its Chapter One slate. According to reports, the Clayface character in this film is the same one who appeared in the animated series *Creature Commandos*.
