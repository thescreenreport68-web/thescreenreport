A host of A-list celebrities were photographed arriving at New York’s Madison Square Garden on July 3 for the wedding of [Taylor Swift](/celebrity/taylor-swift-s-wedding-look-included-a-subtle-eras-tour-nod/) and Travis Kelce, according to a report from Variety. The Associated Press confirmed the morning of the event that the nuptials were taking place that day at the famed arena.

## Who Was Spotted at the Wedding?

Variety, citing photos from People, reported that stars seen heading to the venue included Ed Sheeran, [Bradley Cooper](/movies/josh-gad-to-join-margot-robbie-and-bradley-cooper-s-ocean-s-prequel-ex/), Zoë Kravitz, Jack Antonoff, Gigi Hadid, Dakota Johnson, Ethan Hawke, Machine Gun Kelly (MGK), Jason Sudeikis, Hugh Grant, and Camila Cabello. The outlet described a scene of celebrities “dressed to the nines” making their way to the Swift-Kelce union.

## What Do We Know About the Event?

Variety reported that digital wedding invitations contained watermarks with guest names and were packaged with non-disclosure agreements.

Swift and Kelce went public with their engagement on August 26, 2025. They both shared a photo on social media of Kelce proposing on one knee with the caption, “Your English teacher and your gym teacher are getting married.” In an August 2025 GQ cover story, Kelce opened up about their connection.

“I hadn’t experienced somebody in the same shoes as me, having a partner who understands the scrutiny, understands the ups and downs of being in front of millions,” he told the magazine.
