Jason Statham's 'Meg 2: The Trench' Surges into Netflix Top 5

Jason Statham’s return as Jonas Taylor in the prehistoric shark thriller 'Meg 2: The Trench' is commanding attention on Netflix this week. The film has surfaced at #5 on Netflix's Top 10 chart, amassing an impressive 7.0 million hours viewed during its first week streaming. This strong debut marks a fresh wave of popularity for the movie following its theatrical and premium video on demand releases.

' Meg 2: The Trench' is an action-packed sci-fi horror film that plunges audiences into the ocean's abyss. The story follows a daring research team on an exploratory dive that turns chaotic when a risky mining operation threatens their mission. Faced with terrifying underwater monsters and a fight for survival, the film delivers high-stakes thrills over a 116-minute runtime.

## Star-Studded Cast and Creative Force

Directed by Ben Wheatley, the sequel boasts a talented ensemble led by Jason Statham as Jonas Taylor. This diverse cast drives the film’s intense underwater adventure.

## Streaming Success: Numbers That Bite

Holding a spot in the Top 10 so early signals strong viewer interest and wide accessibility.
