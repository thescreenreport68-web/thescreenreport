The set features eight minifigures, including the show's iconic FBI agents Fox Mulder and Dana Scully.

The development was done in partnership with Gillian Anderson, who played Scully. The set is part of Lego's Ideas line, where fan submissions are voted on for production.

## What does the Lego X-Files set include?

The build includes a detailed replica of Fox Mulder's FBI office, a forest clearing scene, and a hovering UFO. Minifigures beyond the two leads include Walter Skinner, Mr. X, Alex Krycek, Carl Busch, The Flukeman, and a grey alien. Details inside the office feature an "I Want to Believe" poster, an "X-Files" dossier, and a nod to Mulder's sunflower seed habit.

"Seeing myself in minifigure form is both surreal and oddly delightful – there’s something very charming about being ‘miniaturised'," Anderson said in a statement provided to Variety. "The whole set is a brilliant celebration of the ‘X-Files’ which I hope fans enjoy revisiting in this way."

## When can you buy the Lego X-Files set?

The retail price is $199.99.

The original concept came from Australian fan designer Brent Waller, who submitted it to the Lego Ideas "'90s Nostalgia Challenge." "I was obsessed with ‘The X-Files’ from the moment I first saw it in the ’90s," Waller said in a statement. "I originally built Mulder’s office back in 2014 while rewatching the series, and it grew from there as a personal project."
