Hamish Linklater, Michelle Buteau, and Simu Liu have joined the cast of Elizabeth Banks' upcoming untitled comedy series for Apple TV+, according to an exclusive report from Deadline.

They join a previously announced ensemble. The series will shoot in Los Angeles later this year.

## Who are the new cast members playing?

Linklater will play Evan, Heidi's ex-husband. Buteau has been cast as Melissa, a talented ad executive. Liu will portray Dr. Zhou, a doctor who treats Heidi's son.

## What is the Apple TV+ series about?

The series, from creators Liz Heldens and Matt Ward, follows Heidi (Banks), who is fresh off a messy divorce and sets out to secure a lively second act for herself and her kids. But when she stumbles into coordinating her father’s (Danson) retirement community sexcapades, Heidi is forced into an unlikely alliance with his girlfriend’s perpetually single son.

Heldens and Ward will serve as showrunners and executive producers. Banks executive produces through her Brownstone Productions banner alongside Max Handelman and Krissy Wall. Jonathan Krisel is set to direct the pilot and also executive produce.
