Taylor Swift knows how to tell a story, and her wedding fashion was no exception. The designer worked "in close collaboration" with the newlyweds on the creation, which the release noted was Anderson's "first couture wedding dress for a world-renowned celebrity." She completed the look with Christian Louboutin heels, a footwear line she reportedly selected for the nuptials.

The choice itself wasn't a shock — Swift has long had a relationship with the French fashion house. She wore a plaid corset Dior dress to the 2024 MTV Video Music Awards. But in the months leading up to her Madison Square Garden vows, fans were quick to notice that Dior became her purse brand of choice, sparking speculation she was dropping hints about her wedding dress designer.

It started in October, when Swift stepped out in New York City to celebrate the release of *The Life of a Showgirl*. She paired a plaid Miu Miu skirt with Dior's $3,450 Montaigne Avenue Top Handle Bag. The look came mere weeks after Kelce, 36, proposed with a Kindred Lubeck engagement ring.

The Dior bag parade continued this spring. In April, she carried a "teeny Dior" bag to a dinner with friends Ashley Avignone and actor Jerrod Carmichael. Weeks later, she was spotted with dad Scott Swift and Avignone wearing a $9,500 sunny yellow limited-edition mini Lady Dior bag. She stuck with the brand in May and again in June, accessorizing with a seasons-old Dior tote at an NBA finals game at MSG.

For a musician who's never met an Easter egg she wasn't eager to hide, the repeated choice felt deliberate. It seems Swift was building a subtle, stylish narrative leading to the big day. As for the pain of those iconic red-bottom soles? Swift has embraced that ache before. "I mean, dancing all night, yeah my feet ache," she said in her 2025 documentary. "But I'm wearing Louboutins. Like, it is a privilege for my feet to ache like this."

The wedding itself was a star-studded affair, with guests reportedly arriving in a mix of formalwear. While the dress details are now confirmed, the full guest list and other ceremonial specifics remain private. As always, Swift has masterfully controlled the narrative, giving fans just enough to decode while keeping the most intimate moments for herself.
