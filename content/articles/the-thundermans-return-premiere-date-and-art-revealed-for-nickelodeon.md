Nickelodeon has announced the premiere date for *The Thundermans Return*, the streaming movie reuniting the cast of the popular superhero sitcom.

The network also revealed the official key art for the project, showcasing the entire Thunderman family.

## What is the plot of the movie?

The story follows twins Phoebe and Max Thunderman, who are enjoying their superhero careers. After a rescue mission goes wrong, the entire family is sent back to their hometown of Hiddenville. While parents Hank and Barb embrace the return, and siblings Billy and Nora look forward to a normal high school life, Phoebe and Max are determined to get their super status back.

## Who is in the cast?

The main cast from the original series returns, led by Kira Kosarin as Phoebe and Jack Griffo as Max. They are joined by Addison Riecke as Nora, Diego Velazquez as Billy, Maya Le Clark as Chloe, Chris Tallman as Hank, and Rosa Blasi as Barb. The film also features Dana Snyder as Dr. Colosso, with Paul F. Tompkins as King Crab and Michael Wayne Foster as Strongdor.

## Where can I watch it?

*The Thundermans Return* is a streaming-original film and will be available on Paramount+ platforms, including Paramount+ Premium, Paramount+ Essential, and the Paramount+ Roku Premium Channel. The movie has a runtime of 70 minutes and is rated TV-G.

Trevor Kirschner directed the film, which has garnered two award nominations since its release.
