Madonna is celebrating the release of her new album, *Confessions II*, with a series of limited-time pop-up experiences in New York, Los Angeles, and London, according to Billboard. The album, a sequel to her 2005 record *Confessions on a Dance Floor*, was released on Friday, July 3, via Warner Records.

Four different installations were open to fans over the July 3-5 weekend. They offered immersive visuals, shopping for exclusive merchandise, and opportunities for fans to create content themed around the new music.

## Where are the pop-ups located?
Two primary **Confessions II Pop-Up** locations were open in Los Angeles and New York from July 3-5. These offered free access and featured album-themed décor, photo opportunities, and exclusive vinyl variants. Separately, TikTok presented **House of Confessions** installations in New York and London on July 3-4. These also offered free entry but required advance reservation through Citizen Ticket.

The pop-ups serve as a physical launch event for *Confessions II*, marking the superstar's latest era. The album's release and accompanying experiences follow [Madonna](/music/madonna-gets-vulnerable-about-turbulent-relationship-with-daughter-lou/)'s recent global partnership with TikTok, which also included a live broadcast premiere event on July 2.
