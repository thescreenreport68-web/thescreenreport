The BET Awards 2026 will broadcast live on Sunday, June 28, with comedian Druski hosting the 26th annual ceremony. According to Billboard, the performance lineup includes Cardi B, Doechii, and Nas. The event promises a night celebrating Black excellence in music, television, film, and sports.

## How can I watch the BET Awards live for free?

The official, free live stream for the BET Awards will be available on BET.com and through the BET app. This is the primary method for cord-cutters to watch the ceremony live online without a cable subscription. Viewers will need to create a free account on the platform to access the stream.

For those with a traditional cable, satellite, or live TV streaming service subscription, the ceremony will air live on the BET channel. Subscribers can also watch through authenticated apps like the BET app or their provider's app by logging in with their cable credentials. Services like YouTube TV, Hulu + Live TV, Sling TV, and FuboTV typically carry the BET network.

## Who is performing and hosting?

Druski, the popular comedian and social media personality, takes the stage as host for the 2026 ceremony. The performer list, as reported by Billboard, is headlined by Grammy-winning rapper Cardi B. She is joined by rising star Doechii and hip-hop legend Nas, ensuring a dynamic mix of generations and styles.

Additional performers and presenters are expected to be announced closer to the event date. The BET Awards are known for high-energy collaborations and tribute segments, often becoming the night's most talked-about moments.

## What time do the BET Awards start?

While the official start time for the 2026 ceremony has not been announced, the BET Awards traditionally begin at 8 p.m. ET / 5 p.m. PT. The live red carpet pre-show usually starts one to two hours before the main ceremony. Check the official BET social media accounts or website for the confirmed schedule as the date approaches.

Viewers tuning in via the free live stream on BET.com should log in early to ensure their connection is stable before the broadcast begins. The ceremony typically runs for approximately three hours.
