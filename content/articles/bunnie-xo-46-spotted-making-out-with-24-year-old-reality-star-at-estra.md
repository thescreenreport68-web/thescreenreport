What happens when a new romance plays out at the scene of the old one? For Bunnie Xo, it was a Fourth of July kiss at her estranged husband's bar.

Video obtained by TMZ shows the 46-year-old "Dumb Blonde" podcast host making out with 24-year-old reality star Dylan Wolf at Jelly Roll's Goodnight Nashville bar in Nashville, Tennessee. According to the outlet, the pair were in the rooftop's VIP section when they locked lips as blue lights flashed around them.

The sighting sparked immediate fan chatter, especially after Wolf posted a cryptic TikTok video with Bunnie on Monday. He captioned it, "@everyone thinking they’re connecting the dots" and "Undeleted Pod coming soon @Bunnie Xo." Fans were quick to note that Wolf wore the same black cap reading "I love a girl from Malibu" in the TikTok as he did in the TMZ video.

The moment comes as Bunnie navigates her split from country star Jelly Roll, 41, after more than a decade together. According to Page Six, Jelly Roll filed for divorce in May in Williamson County, Tennessee. Notably, fans observed that Bunnie's podcast episode from last month, where she discussed the surprising split, appears to have been taken down.

Wolf, who stars in Netflix's new reality series "Calabasas Confidential," was previously linked to model Delilah Belle Hamlin, 28. According to Decider, he said in May that their relationship was over, stating, "She’s a lovely lady. That kind of just happened out of the blue over the summer, as well... We’re no longer tied together. That was just kind of short-lived."

While the video confirms the kiss, the exact nature of Bunnie Xo and Dylan Wolf's relationship remains unconfirmed. Neither has publicly commented on the footage or their status.

In a past interview about Jelly Roll moving on, Bunnie reportedly said, "He is ready — raring and ready to go," adding that he had started dating. It seems both parties are exploring new chapters.
