Margaret Qualley and Jack Antonoff are calling it quits, but insiders say the split is more about communication than drama.

According to Page Six, the pair are working together to handle their separation in "the best way that they can" — with "love and kindness." A source told the outlet, "They’re figuring this all out together." The same insider was quick to shut down swirling rumors, stating that speculation about an alleged infidelity "aren’t true."

"For what it’s worth, they’ve had a beautiful and loving relationship. Sometimes, these things just don’t work, and it doesn’t need to be for any dramatic reason," the source added.

A separate Page Six source elaborated on those challenges, citing communication issues. "Communication was definitely a challenge between him and Margaret," the insider added.

Reps for Antonoff and Qualley could not be reached for comment by Page Six.

While the reasons behind the split are becoming clearer, the exact timeline and full details of their separation agreement remain private. The couple appears focused on an amicable process, emphasizing the love that was there even as they part ways.
