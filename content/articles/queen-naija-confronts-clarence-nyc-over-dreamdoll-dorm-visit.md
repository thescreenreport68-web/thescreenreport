The internet saw a clipped moment of tension. But the full conversation revealed a deeper debate about double standards and online perception.

Queen Naija confronted her partner, Clarence NYC, about being in DreamDoll's room during Kai Cenat's Streamer University event, according to The Shade Room. The viral clip, which spread rapidly this week, showed the initial confrontation and ignited a flurry of social media reactions.

Clarence and Queen later sat down in their own dorm to hash it out. He argued her reaction was taken out of context, pointing out he encourages her to network with male creators, including doing a song with PlaqueBoyMax. Clarence said the moment left him discouraged from collaborating with other creators and claimed there's a double standard. "I’m still going home after these five days of Streamer U. I’m going home to you.", he reassured her.

## Social Media Weighs In

Others defended Queen, with one fan writing, "She gives mean girl. And I don’t think Queen bugging she’s so genuine and nice and everybody don’t deserve that she ain’t have to explain nothing to her.".

Following the private talk with Clarence, Queen Naija headed to a Bible study session at Streamer University. There, she took the opportunity to clear the air with DreamDoll, effectively ending the brief feud. The entire situation unfolded during Kai Cenat's Streamer University, a five-day creator event.
