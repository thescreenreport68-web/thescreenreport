Netflix has renewed its young adult drama *My Life With the Walter Boys* for a third season, according to Deadline. The announcement comes ahead of the show's sophomore season premiere.

The renewal was confirmed alongside a premiere date for the new season. Season 3 wrapped production in late November and is scheduled to debut on the streamer on August 6, 2026.

## Who is joining the cast for Season 3?

Three new actors are set to join the series. Deadline exclusively reported that Chad Rook will appear in a recurring guest role, though his character details remain unknown. Netflix has also confirmed the casting of Erin Karpluk, who will play Hannah, the sister of family patriarch George Walter. Naveen Paddock will portray Elliot, an intern who provides some competition for Jackie's affections.

## What is My Life With the Walter Boys about?

The series, based on Ali Novak's novel, centers on teenager Jackie Howard. After a tragedy, she moves in with her guardian's large family in the small town of Silver Falls, Colorado. The show explores themes of found family and features a central love triangle between Jackie and two Walter brothers, Alex and Cole.

Starring Nikki Rodriguez as Jackie, the main cast includes Noah LaLonde as Cole Walter, Ashby Gentry as Alex Walter, and Sarah Rafferty and Marc Blucas as the Walter parents. The series was created by Melanie Halsall, who also serves as showrunner.

In a further sign of confidence in the series, Deadline also reports that Netflix has renewed *My Life With the Walter Boys* for a fourth season.
