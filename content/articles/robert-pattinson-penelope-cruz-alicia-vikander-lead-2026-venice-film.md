The lineup for the 2026 [Venice Film Festival](/movies/margot-robbie-andy-serkis-join-venice-film-festival-s-immersive-lineup/) was unveiled on Thursday, featuring a slate of new films from major directors and starring top talent. According to The Hollywood Reporter, the competition will be headlined by actors Robert Pattinson, Penélope Cruz, and Alicia Vikander, with the Oasis rockers Noel and Liam Gallagher also expected to attend.

Martin McDonagh returns to Venice with his black comedy *Wild Horse Nine*, starring John Malkovich and Sam Rockwell. Florian Zeller's psychological thriller *Bunker*, starring Javier Bardem and Penélope Cruz, will also compete for the Golden Lion. Robert Pattinson will premiere *[Primetime](/movies/robert-pattinson-s-primetime-sets-september-theatrical-release/)*, his feature debut as a producer, in which he plays NBC newsman Chris Hansen.

## Which Directors Are Returning?

Several festival favorites are back in competition. Werner Herzog presents *Bucking Fastard*, starring Rooney and Kate Mara. Korean auteur Lee Chang-dong returns with the Netflix-backed melodrama *Possible Love*, his first Venice appearance since 2002. The sole female director in the 20-film competition is May el-Toukhy with her period drama *Woman Unknown*.
