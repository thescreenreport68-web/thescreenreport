How does a reality TV icon celebrate turning 46? By turning a Las Vegas nightclub into his personal party.

Pauly D kicked off his birthday celebration on Saturday night at Marquee Las Vegas, according to TMZ. The "Jersey Shore" star took over the DJ booth for a packed house, hyping up the crowd with his trademark energy. A giant "Happy B-Day Pauly D" sign lit up behind him as he soaked in the moment, grinning from ear to ear.

The party rolled on well into the night, with clubgoers singing along and dancing shoulder-to-shoulder. TMZ reports the bash gave the reality TV favorite the kind of celebration Vegas does best, suggesting the DJ isn't slowing down anytime soon. The outlet described the scene as a packed Vegas nightclub with booming music and a whole lotta love from the crowd, with Pauly D looking right at home behind the decks and pointing out to fans.

This high-energy birthday set comes just as Pauly D is slated for another major gig. According to TribLIVE.com, he is scheduled to perform at the Pennsylvania World Cup Fan Zone at Acrisure Stadium in Pittsburgh on July 5. He will spin tunes following an 8 p.m. match as part of festivities celebrating both the international soccer tournament and America's 250th birthday. The Pennsylvania World Cup Fan Zone will be open in Pittsburgh on July 4, 5, 9 and 19, offering free entry to watch select World Cup games and enjoy other entertainment. The programming begins on July 4 with pop country singer Brett Young.

"Pittsburgh is ready to welcome soccer fans from across the region and around the world, and adding DJ Pauly D to the lineup gives fans another exciting reason to come experience the Fan Zone," said Jay Roberts, vice president of stadium operations and management for Acrisure Stadium, in a release cited by TribLIVE.com. Roberts added, "This will be a one-of-a-kind celebration of sports, music, family entertainment and civic pride during one of the biggest global sporting events."

The birthday bash at Marquee shows Pauly D's enduring appeal, seamlessly blending his reality TV fame with his established DJ career. From the Vegas strip to a World Cup watch party, his schedule remains as packed as the dance floors he commands. The TMZ report concluded that if the Vegas party is any indication, Pauly D isn't slowing down anytime soon.
