NBA Rookie of the Year Cooper Flagg and his family are scheduled to appear on *Celebrity Family Feud* on Thursday night on ABC, according to a report from the Sun Journal.

Flagg will be joined by his twin brother, Ace, a sophomore on the University of Maine men’s basketball team. The family team also includes his older brother Hunter, mother Kelly, and father Ralph.

## Who are they playing against?

The Flagg family will compete against a team from Barstool Sports. That team will be represented by personalities Dave Portnoy, Kirk Minihane, Ryan Whitney, Jerry Thornton, and Rico Bosco.

## What are they playing for?

According to the report, the Flagg family will be playing for the Ronald McDonald House of Dallas. The Barstool Sports team is playing for the Lifeline Animal Project, based in Atlanta, Georgia.

The episode of *Celebrity Family Feud* is set to air at 8 p.m. on ABC.
