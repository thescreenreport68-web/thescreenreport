According to Deadline, the star and producer shared the news in a behind-the-scenes video posted to his Instagram. The film is set for a March 17, 2028, theatrical release, concluding a series that began in 2001.

## What Did Vin Diesel Say?

In the video, Diesel thanked the franchise's dedicated fanbase for their support. "I’m on set. People are grinding. Incredible crews are working," he said. The actor acknowledged the long wait for the finale, adding, "You’ve been patient to the industry, you’ve been patient to the studio, and you’ve been patient with me. Over the past three and a half years, we’ve been grinding to try to make the most amazing finale."

## What Happens After the Movie?

While the main film series is ending, Diesel has previously indicated that the universe will continue to expand. Deadline's report notes he has teased that NBCUniversal has four television shows in development set within the Fast & Furious world.
