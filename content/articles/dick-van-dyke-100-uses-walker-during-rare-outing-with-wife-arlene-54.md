How does a centenarian Hollywood icon spend a Tuesday afternoon? For Dick Van Dyke, it involved a walker, a Starbucks run, and the company of his wife.

Photos obtained by Page Six show the 100-year-old actor using a walker during a stroll in Los Angeles with his wife, Arlene Silver, 54. The couple stopped by a Starbucks drive-thru for refreshments. At one point, Silver assisted her husband as he transitioned from their car to the walker.

For the outing, Van Dyke wore dark blue sweatpants and a white collared shirt. Silver sported a black, off-the-shoulder graphic shirt with black pants.

The sighting comes a few months after the couple was last seen in public at the opening of Malibu Urgent Care in April. During that event, Van Dyke was spotted dancing and happily chatting, though he used a cane while seated beside musician Herb Alpert.

Van Dyke and Silver have been a couple since meeting in 2006. They tied the knot in 2012, with the "Mary Poppins" star crediting the makeup artist for keeping him young. He has been vocal about the rejuvenating power of their relationship.

The couple's rare public appearance offers a glimpse into their life together, showcasing both the realities of aging and the enduring bond that Van Dyke credits for his vitality.
