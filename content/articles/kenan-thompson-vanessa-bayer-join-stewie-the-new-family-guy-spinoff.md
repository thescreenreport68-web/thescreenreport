Kenan Thompson, Vanessa Bayer, and four other actors have joined the voice cast of the upcoming *Family Guy* spinoff *Stewie*, according to Variety and Deadline. The series, which will focus on the Griffins' diabolical toddler, was announced in March with a two-season order from Fox.

Seth MacFarlane will reprise his role as the title character and also voice a new classmate named Bean. The newly announced cast includes *Saturday Night Live* veterans Thompson and Bayer, *The Rookie* star Melissa O’Neil, *Family Guy* voice actor Mike Henry, and actors Aaron Lee and Jessica Lowe.

## Who is playing what?

According to the reporting, each actor will voice a distinct character in Stewie's new, less-than-ideal preschool. Kenan Thompson will play Royal, a friend of Stewie’s with a skeptical attitude toward their adventures. Vanessa Bayer voices Morgan, Stewie’s teacher who prioritizes partying over supervision.

Mike Henry, who voices multiple characters on the flagship series, will play BJ, a gossipy classmate who is the son of Henry’s *Family Guy* character Bruce. Jessica Lowe is Caroline, a wannabe influencer and Stewie’s classroom rival. Melissa O’Neil voices Wanda, Stewie’s intellectual soulmate, and Aaron Lee is Skunk, the class’s elderly, opinionated turtle.

## When does the show come out?

The series is slated to premiere on Fox during the 2027-28 broadcast season. Episodes will be available to stream on Hulu the day after they air. The show was created by MacFarlane and writer Kirker Butler, who also serves as showrunner.

*Stewie* follows the toddler after he is expelled from his old preschool and enrolls in a subpar new one. The official synopsis describes a miserable classroom that Stewie enlivens by using his gadgets to transport the group on wild adventures through space and time, turning boring school days into surreal escapades.

## What is the show's connection to Family Guy?

Beyond MacFarlane and Henry’s involvement, the spinoff is produced by 20th Television Animation. The project represents a significant expansion of the *Family Guy* universe, centering on one of its most popular characters as the parent series continues into its 25th season.
