Netflix has released the official trailer for [Ariana Grande](/celebrity/ariana-grande-and-ricky-alvarez-are-the-real-deal-source-says/)'s concert film, *ariana grande: excuse me, i love you*. The documentary captures the pop star's Sweetener World Tour.

## What is the film about?
Directed by Paul Dugdale, the project is categorized as a Music Documentary.

## When was it released?
*ariana grande: excuse me, i love you* is a 2020 release.

## Who appears in it?
The film's cast is led by Ariana Grande and includes key members of her touring team such as Scott Nicholson, Brian Nicholson, and Joesar Alva, alongside performers Cory Graves and Bong Buño.
