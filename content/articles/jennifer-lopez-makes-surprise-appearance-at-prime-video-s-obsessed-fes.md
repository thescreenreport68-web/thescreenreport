Jennifer Lopez made a surprise appearance at Prime Video's Obsessed Fest on June 7, 2026, to introduce a panel for the upcoming series *Off Campus*. According to a report from The Hollywood Reporter, the singer and actress kicked off the panel featuring the show's creator, Louisa Levy, and cast members Belmont Cameli, Ella Bright, Stephen Kalyn, Mika Abdalla, Antonio Cipriano, Jalen Thomas Brooks, and Josh Heuston.

Lopez's appearance served as a high-profile introduction for the new Prime Video series. The event, designed for superfans of the streamer's content, provided an exclusive first look at the show.

## What is 'Off Campus'?

The series *Off Campus* was the focus of the panel Lopez introduced. While specific plot details were not disclosed at the event, the gathering of its creator and core cast suggests it is a significant upcoming title for Prime Video's slate. The panel likely offered fans early insights into the show's characters and premise.

## Why was Jennifer Lopez there?

Lopez's involvement with the event underscores her established relationship with Amazon Studios. She recently starred in the 2024 sci-fi film *Atlas* for the streamer. Her surprise appearance functions as a major promotional boost, leveraging her star power to generate buzz for a new Amazon original series. This cross-promotion is a common strategy within the streaming ecosystem.

Obsessed Fest is a fan convention hosted by Prime Video, celebrating its popular movies and series. The event typically features cast reunions, exclusive screenings, and panel discussions. Lopez's surprise cameo represents a major get for the festival, creating immediate social media buzz and headlines.

## What is Jennifer Lopez working on now?

As of June 2026, Lopez remains one of the most influential entertainers in the industry. Her recent projects include the musical film *This Is Me... Now: A Love Story* and the Netflix action film *The Mother* from 2023. She continues to be a prolific figure in film, music, and business.

Her filmography has grossed over $1.9 billion worldwide, and she has sold over 80 million records. Lopez's career spans acclaimed performances in films like *Hustlers* and *Selena*, establishing her as a barrier-breaking Latina artist in Hollywood.
