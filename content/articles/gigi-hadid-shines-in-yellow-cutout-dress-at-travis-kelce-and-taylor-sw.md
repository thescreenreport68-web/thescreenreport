When the guest list is a who's who of Hollywood and music royalty, the fashion stakes are sky-high. Gigi Hadid, a close friend of the bride, met the moment with not one, but two standout looks for Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding festivities at Madison Square Garden.

According to photos shared on Ashley Avignone's Instagram and reported by Page Six, Hadid wore a yellow Christopher Esber dress to the couple's rehearsal dinner on Thursday night. The dress was trimmed with white lace and featured a row of twisted cutouts at the bodice. She accessorized with white Andrea Wazen mules and a colorful Fendi Baguette bag. Her friend and fellow guest Ashley Avignone wore a vintage purple silk Yves Saint Laurent Rive Gauche gown.

The pair snapped a selfie en route to the soirée.

Hadid also pulled out all the sartorial stops for Swift’s big day on Friday, sporting a sparkling pink gown designed by Wiederhoeft.

The rehearsal dinner and wedding were star-studded affairs. The choice of Madison Square Garden as the venue added an epic, larger-than-life scale to the celebrations, fitting for one of the world's most famous couples.

Hadid's dual fashion moments highlight her status as a top model and a trusted member of Swift's inner circle. While the wedding details were tightly guarded, these glimpses through friends' social media offer fans a curated peek into the glamour of the weekend. It underscores how, even as a guest at a friend's wedding, a supermodel's style choices become a headline event of their own.
