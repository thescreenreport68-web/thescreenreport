Alex and Ani has launched a new jewelry collection inspired by Stephenie Meyer’s bestselling “[Twilight](/tv/netflix-s-twilight-tv-show-begins-casting-jacob-billy-black-after-two-year/)” saga, according to a report from Billboard.

The collection, announced on Friday, July 17, features bracelets, rings, and necklaces filled with subtle references to characters and moments from the vampire romance series.

## What’s in the collection?

Pieces are designed for “Twihards” looking to celebrate the franchise. The offerings include a Spider Monkey Locket Necklace engraved with the quote “You better hold on tight spider monkey,” a Bella and Edward Locket Cuff that reveals the couple’s meadow scene, and a “Hoa Hoa” Tension Bangle engraved with the fan-favorite phrase. A Bella-inspired engagement ring that opens to reveal the wedding day is also available.

Fans loyal to Bella’s werewolf friend are not forgotten. The collection includes a Jacob Pendant Necklace with an “I 3 Jacob” charm and a dog tag engraved with Jacob’s “loca” quote.

## Are the items available now?

Some pieces from the collection have already begun selling out.

The launch arrives as the “Twilight” fandom continues to thrive, proving the enduring appeal of Bella, Edward, and Jacob’s story nearly two decades later.
