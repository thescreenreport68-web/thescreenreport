The upcoming thriller anthology series **"American Hostage," starring Jon Hamm and Giovanni Ribisi, is set to world premiere at the Toronto Film Festival**, according to Deadline. The series will debut as part of the festival's Primetime Program.

## What is 'American Hostage' about?

Set in the 1970s, the psychological thriller tells the true story of Fred Heckman, a beloved Indianapolis radio reporter played by Jon Hamm. He is thrust into a life-or-death crisis when hostage-taker Tony Kiritsis, portrayed by Giovanni Ribisi, demands to be interviewed on his popular radio news program.

The series is co-created and executive produced by Shawn Ryan (*The Night Agent*) and Eileen Myers (*Big Love*). It is based on the first season of the scripted podcast of the same name. Additional cast members include Mireille Enos, Kristoffer Polaha, and [William Jackson Harper](/tv/william-jackson-harper-joins-hulu-legal-drama-conviction/).

Following its festival premiere, *American Hostage* will debut exclusively on MGM+ in the United States and on Crave in Canada. The release is scheduled for this fall.
