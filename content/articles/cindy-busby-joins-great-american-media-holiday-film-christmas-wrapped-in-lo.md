Cindy Busby is set to star in the Great American Media holiday film *Christmas Wrapped in Love*, which has just begun production, according to Deadline.

The film will have its world premiere across Great American Pure Flix, Great American [Family](/movies/the-sheep-detectives-trailer-arrives-promising-farmyard-fun/), and GFAM+ during the network's top-rated Great American Christmas programming event this October.

## What is the plot of Christmas Wrapped in Love?

In the film, based on Jennifer Snow's bestselling novel *The Christmas Gift List*, Busby plays Natasha Lloyd, who runs a business finding unique gifts for clients. Overworked surgeon Ryan Bartlett hires her to find presents for his family. To get to know them better, Natasha agrees to pose as his girlfriend for the holidays, a professional arrangement that soon turns into real romance.

## What did the network say about the casting?

Bill Abbott, President & CEO of Great American Media, praised the star in an official statement. "Cindy Busby represents everything audiences have come to expect from Great American Media – authenticity, heart, and performances that feel both timeless and deeply personal," he said.

Busby is best known for her series regular role as Ashley Stanton in the long-running Canadian drama *Heartland*. She recently starred in *When Hope Calls* as Nora Anderson. Her other credits include Hallmark's *Cedar Cove* and films like *Unleashing Mr. Darcy* and *Royal Hearts*.
