Sacha Baron Cohen was at Wimbledon today with his alter-ego, Ali G, according to Deadline. The appearance is reportedly connected to filming for a brand new Ali G movie, which is being shot in secret.

The character, known for his blunt, track-suit and gold-chain wearing hip-hop persona, was seen in the flesh at the prestigious tennis tournament. The news outlet reports the film is being made "sub rosa," a signature method for Cohen, who has famously kept productions secret to surprise his subjects.

## What is the new Ali G movie about?

No details about the plot or where the film has landed for distribution have been announced. The project would mark the second Ali G feature film, following 2002's *Ali G Indahouse*. That film, which grossed over $23 million abroad, saw the character unwittingly become a pawn in a plot to overthrow the British Prime Minister.

## Where can I watch the first Ali G movie?

The 2002 film *Ali G Indahouse* is available to rent or buy in the U.S. on platforms including Amazon Video, Apple TV Store, Google Play Movies, and YouTube. The film has a 59% critics score on Rotten Tomatoes.
