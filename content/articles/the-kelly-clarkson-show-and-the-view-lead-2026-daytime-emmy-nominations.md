The 2026 Daytime Emmy nominations have arrived, and the daytime talk show field is once again led by *The Kelly Clarkson Show* and *The View*. According to Billboard, both shows scored nominations for Best Daytime Talk Series and Best Daytime Talk Series Host.

## Which shows are nominated for Best Daytime Talk Series?

The competition for Best Daytime Talk Series is a crowded field. Alongside *The Kelly Clarkson Show* and *The View*, the nominees are *Live with Kelly and Mark*, *The Drew Barrymore Show*, *3rd Hour of Today*, and *Today with Jenna & Friends*.

## Who is nominated for Best Daytime Talk Series Host?

In the host category, Kelly Clarkson is nominated for her eponymous show. The six hosts of *The View*—Joy Behar, Whoopi Goldberg, Alyssa Farah Griffin, Sara Haines, Sunny Hostin, and Ana Navarro—are nominated as a group. The other nominees are Tamron Hall for *Tamron Hall*, Jennifer Hudson for *The Jennifer Hudson Show*, and Sherri Shepherd for *Sherri*.

This marks the seventh consecutive season Clarkson has been a double nominee. *The View* has a long history in the category, with its hosts winning in 2009 and the show itself winning Best Daytime Talk Series in 2003. The winners of this year's awards will be announced on October 30.
