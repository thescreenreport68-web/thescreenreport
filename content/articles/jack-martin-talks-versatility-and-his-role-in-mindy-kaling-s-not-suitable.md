Actor Jack Martin is discussing his role in the new Mindy Kaling series for Hulu, **'Not Suitable for Work'**. He plays Josh Teitelbaum, one of five work-obsessed twenty-somethings navigating career and life in Manhattan's Murray Hill neighborhood.

## How did Jack Martin get the role?

According to an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Martin actively pursued the part. "I really wanted to be in it, and I just got really lucky," he said. His audition was a positive experience, especially his interaction with creator Mindy Kaling. "That’s the nicest person in an audition room I’ve ever interacted with," Martin recalled. He praised her for being polite, thoughtful, and generous with notes.

## Who does Jack Martin star with?

Martin was particularly excited to book the role because his friend, actor Will Angus, was also cast in the series. "I freaked out. I got so excited," Martin said upon learning the news. He immediately called Angus, who was filming abroad, and asked him to be roommates. Nicholas Duvernay rounds out their on-screen trio of friends. Martin says if the show is renewed for a second season, he and Angus plan to convince Duvernay to live with them.

## What's next for Jack Martin?

Looking ahead, Martin is focused on finding strong writing. He has a specific genre goal. "I’d really like to do romance," Martin said, citing films like 'Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind' as inspiration.

'Not Suitable for Work' is streaming now on Hulu.
