What does one wear to a Taylor Swift wedding rehearsal dinner? If you're Suki Waterhouse, you bring your own rose garden. The actress and musician was snapped in an Instagram selfie en route to Taylor Swift's wedding rehearsal dinner, dressed to impress in a floral-appliquéd look topped with a fluffy feathered jacket similar to those she often wears for her onstage performances. She finished the dreamy getup with a festival-ready eye makeup look, including a pop of pink at the inner corners of her eyes.

Waterhouse, 34, had already confirmed she’d be attending pal Swift’s nuptials. Back in May, she told Variety, “Nothing scarier than the actual planning part, right? I’m gonna go to Taylor’s wedding, and maybe I’ll get some inspiration. It will be amazing.” It seems she’s taking her own wedding planning with fiancé Robert Pattinson seriously, looking to Swift's highly anticipated event for ideas.

The pair have been close for about a decade, with Waterhouse opening for Swift’s Eras Tour in London. In her Variety interview, Waterhouse gushed about her friend’s influence. “The coolest thing about her is she gives other artists these insane opportunities that really change people’s lives,” she said. “Knowing her as a friend, she’s actually been through so much.”

Waterhouse’s rehearsal dinner look, styled with florals and feathers, was shared on her Instagram on Thursday. The “Daisy Jones The Six” star is clearly a trusted member of Swift’s inner circle, making her appearance at the pre-wedding festivities a given. Her attendance at the main event is all but confirmed, following her earlier statement.

As the wedding weekend kicks off at Madison Square, all eyes are on the guests and their fashion choices. Waterhouse has set a high bar with her romantic, ethereal ensemble, proving she’s ready to celebrate one of music's biggest power couples in style.
