On the Sunday episode of her podcast "Plan Bri Uncut", Brianna "Chickenfry" LaPaglia leaned into the mic and called bull. She wasn't buying Bunnie Xo's recent public encouragement for her estranged husband, Jelly Roll, to start dating again.

LaPaglia tore into the situation, attributing Bunnie's stance to lingering loyalty. "She doesn’t mean that. She’s still under the mind control of [Jelly Roll]," LaPaglia said, according to Page Six. "Bunnie is just doing the thing where she’s still protecting him. I went through that at a point too, where it’s like you’re not fully stepped out of the situation yet."

She predicted a future reckoning. "She’s going to step out of this a year from now and be like, ‘Holy f–king s–t, LaPaglia’"

## The Weight Loss Theory

LaPaglia's speculation then turned to Jelly Roll himself. "He thought that he can do better. He can date someone younger. He’s going to go for a ‘tradwife.’ One hundred percent gonna go for a ‘tradwife,’" she speculated.

The trigger for LaPaglia's comments was Bunnie Xo's own podcast episode. On June 18, Bunnie discussed her breakup and pending divorce on "Dumb Blonde". She also opened up about encouraging her ex to start dating again, saying that it’s “great” his “DMs are open.”

LaPaglia pointed to Bunnie's friends' social media activity as evidence the well wishes aren't genuine. She noted that Bunnie's closest friends are "reposting the most asinine, crazy, insulting videos about Jelly Roll."

Jelly Roll quietly filed for divorce from Bunnie Xo after nearly 10 years of marriage in May. The split became public knowledge when Bunnie addressed it on her podcast. LaPaglia, an alum of "Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test", is now adding her own layer of interpretation to the unfolding story.
