The actor posted from the set of the eleventh and final chapter in the main franchise.

## What Did Vin Diesel Say?

In the Instagram post, Diesel expressed gratitude to the fans who have waited for the film's development. "I’m on set. People are grinding. Incredible crews are working," he said. He acknowledged the audience's patience over the past three and a half years as the team worked on the finale.

## What is 'Fast Forever'?

*Fast Forever* is confirmed as the concluding chapter of The Fast Saga. The film is currently in production and is scheduled to hit theaters on March 17, 2028. Louis Leterrier is directing the project.

Diesel noted that during the wait for this film, he has been "lucky to embark on other characters." He specifically mentioned the *Rock 'Em Sock 'Em Robots* movie, which he will star in and write for Mattel Studios.

## What's Next for the Franchise?

The actor concluded his message by returning his focus to the current production. "But through it all, one thing I know is, I can feel your support, and that means the world to me," Diesel said. "So, let me get back to this filming, and just know that I hope to make you all proud."

Universal previously announced a separate expansion of the franchise into television. Back in May, Diesel revealed at an NBCUniversal event that Peacock is developing four shows set within the Fast & Furious universe.
