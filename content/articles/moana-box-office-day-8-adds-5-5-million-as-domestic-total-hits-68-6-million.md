A Voyage Beyond the Reef

Catherine Lagaʻaia and Dwayne Johnson lead the charge in Disney's latest live-action reimagining, bringing the beloved oceanic tale of *[Moana](/movies/moana-box-office-day-6-adds-4-3-million-as-domestic-total-hits-59-2-million/)* to a new generation. Directed by Thomas Kail, this 115-minute family adventure fantasy follows teenage Moana as she heeds the Ocean's call. For the first time, she voyages beyond the reef of her island home, Motunui, accompanied by the infamous, shape-shifting demigod Maui. Their mission is an unforgettable journey to restore prosperity and balance to her people.

## The Cast Setting Sail

The film boasts a dynamic cast bringing the vibrant characters of the Pacific to life. Catherine Lagaʻaia takes on the titular role of the courageous and determined Moana. Dwayne Johnson returns to voice the charismatic and powerful demigod Maui. The supporting cast is rich with talent, featuring Rena Owen as the wise Gramma Tala, John Tui as Chief Tui, Frankie Adams as Sina, and Jemaine Clement as the villainous crab Tamatoa. Young actors Emma Puahi-Shapazian and Amaya Masoli also portray Moana at different ages, rounding out the central heroine's journey.

It achieved this figure while playing in a wide release of 4,200 theaters across the country.

## At the Box Office

Moana has grossed $68,581,410 at the domestic box office, 8 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 4,200 theaters. The film added $5,500,000 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $116 million. It carries a reported production budget of $250 million.
