What does the mother of the groom have to say about one of the most talked-about weddings of the decade? Donna Kelce is finally speaking out, and her review is short, sweet, and utterly enchanted.

The Kansas City Chiefs tight end's mom described Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift's wedding as "magical, man, magical" in an Instagram video shared on July 5. The video was posted by Macy's, where Donna made the brief but glowing comment about the July 3 nuptials at Madison Square Garden in New York City.

While Donna has largely kept details of the event close to the vest, the family-centric nature of the day was no secret. According to a press release obtained by E! News, Travis' brother, Jason Kelce, served as best man, while Taylor's brother, Austin Swift, was the bride's Man of Honor. The celebration, according to E! News, boasted a 1,000-person guest list.

The wedding marks the culmination of a whirlwind romance that began in the summer of 2023. Donna Kelce has been a vocal supporter of the relationship from the start. "I can tell you this," she told The Wall Street Journal in November of that year. "He’s happier than I’ve seen him in a long time…. God bless him. He shot for the stars!"

Taylor's mother, Andrea Swift, echoed that sentiment in *The End of an Era* docuseries last year, saying, "Travis Kelce, he brings a lot of happiness." Andrea even played a role in the couple's origin story, recalling how she pointed out early headlines to her daughter: "I’m looking at the headlines and perusing around what’s on the internet. I see that this guy came to your show, and he brought a friendship bracelet and wants to meet you."

The family approval appears to be mutual. In a recent interview, Travis' father reportedly shared his excitement, calling Taylor "truly the girl next door" and "a sweetheart."

Donna Kelce's "magical" remark is the first public comment from the Kelce family since the wedding, offering a heartfelt, if cryptic, glimpse into the private celebration. While the full guest list and specific wedding details remain under wraps, the overwhelming theme from both families is one of pure joy and support for the newlyweds.
