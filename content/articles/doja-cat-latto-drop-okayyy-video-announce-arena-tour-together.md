[Doja Cat](/music/doja-cat-adds-latto-to-tour-ma-vie-world-tour-after-okayyy-video-release/) and Latto have teamed up for a new music video and a joint tour announcement. The pair released the video for their collaboration "Okayyy" on Tuesday, July 21, according to Billboard. The visual leans into a nostalgic, early-2000s aesthetic.

## What is the 'Okayyy' video like?

The clip opens with Latto rapping in lingerie from her bed before moving to a bathroom scene styled with a classic digital camera, evoking the MySpace era. Doja Cat enters the frame in pink lingerie for a scene set in a prison visitation room. The artists reunite for shots against a baby-blue backdrop and later appear with a crew of bodybuilders washing luxury cars outside a mansion. Fan reactions on YouTube praised the pairing, with one comment reading, "WHOEVER PUT THEM TOGETHER DESERVES A RAISE."

## When does their tour start?

In a surprise follow-up, Doja Cat revealed via a clip posted from the video set that Latto will be joining her for the North American leg of the Tour Ma Vie World Tour. This will mark Latto's first run of arena performances since she announced the birth of her daughter in May.
