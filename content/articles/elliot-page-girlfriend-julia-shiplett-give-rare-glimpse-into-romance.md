What does a star-studded movie premiere look like from the inside? Elliot Page's girlfriend, comedian Julia Shiplett, offered a rare and witty glimpse into their private celebration.

According to E! The couple, both 39, got dressed up, screened the mythological movie, and enjoyed martinis at the after-party.

Shiplett's caption was pure comedy. "Saw this charming little indie called the odyssey (maybe based off a book?? not sure)," she wrote. She added, "Really loved it, you guys should try to find it if you can."

Despite the private celebration, Page walked the red carpet solo, joined by his high-profile castmates including Matt Damon, Anne Hathaway, Lupita Nyong'o, Zendaya, and Tom Holland.

This London soiree marks a rare public outing for the pair, who first debuted their romance publicly in June 2025 with a Pride appreciation post. However, Shiplett has shared sweet tributes before. For Page's birthday in February, she posted a roundup of their moments, writing, "Happy birthday E baby!! How lucky I am to have such a sweet, patient, supportive boo thang who always wants to order fries." She also wrote, "You’ve cracked my lil crone heart open and celebrating you is a breeze."

"Imagine spending this one short life obsessing over other people’s anatomy and leaving vile comments under a stranger’s cute birthday tribute," she said. She concluded, "Gonna pray for a lot of you tonight."
