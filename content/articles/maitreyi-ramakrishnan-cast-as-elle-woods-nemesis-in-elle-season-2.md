Maitreyi Ramakrishnan has been cast in the second season of the TV series *Elle*, according to *The Hollywood Reporter*. The *Never Have I Ever* alum will play Sam, a nemesis to Lexi Minetree’s Elle Woods in the *Legally Blonde* prequel.

Ramakrishnan discussed the role at the recent *Elle* premiere in New York. “Without spoiling too much, I like my character’s dynamic in the gang because it’s not something I’ve done before,” she told *The Hollywood Reporter*. Her character, Sam, has been described as possessing Elle’s ambition but none of her optimism, serving as the editor-in-chief of the school newspaper and an “enemy of joy.”

## Who is Sam in Elle?

According to the character description from *The Hollywood Reporter*, Sam is the founder and editor-in-chief of the school newspaper. She’s portrayed as the “commissioner of the grammar police” and someone whose “holy grail” is the AP Stylebook, positioning her as a sharp contrast to the fashion-forward Elle Woods.

## What did Ramakrishnan say about the show's producers?

Having worked with Mindy Kaling on *Never Have I Ever* and now with [Reese Witherspoon](/tv/elle-showrunners-reveal-how-involved-reese-witherspoon-was-in-writers-/) on *Elle*, Ramakrishnan shared her perspective on the two powerhouse producers. “The biggest thing they have in common, I would say, is that they are both women with audacity, and that’s why I think they hire women with audacity as well,” she said. She described both as hard-working women dedicated to telling good stories.

## When does Elle Season 2 come out?

The second season of *Elle* has already been filmed. The first season, consisting of eight episodes, is currently streaming on Amazon Prime Video, as confirmed by the show's official listing.

The series, created by Laura Kittrell and Caroline Dries, is a prequel set in 1995 that explores Elle Woods' high school years before she attended Harvard.
