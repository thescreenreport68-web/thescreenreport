When you're heading to your best friend's wedding rehearsal dinner, you dress the part. For Abigail Anderson, that meant channeling the bride's signature style.

Anderson, Taylor Swift's close friend of over two decades, was spotted heading to the rehearsal dinner for Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding at Madison Square Garden on Thursday, according to Page Six. Her choice? A butter yellow slip dress from Reformation, one of Swift's go-to fashion labels for well over a decade.

The Lilibeth Silk Dress, featuring a cowl neckline and a detachable sheer cape, retails for $328. Anderson styled the sleek maxi with a sculptural gold Cult Gaia clutch, strappy sandals, and a red lip. She was joined by her husband, Charlie Berard, who wore a sharp blue suit.

Swift has been a fan of Reformation's cool-girl dresses, cashmere pieces, and practical shoes for years, and the brand is a firm fixture in her street style rotation.

She's also been spotted in the brand's Jadey Cashmere Oversized V-Neck Sweater, Waldena Block Heeled Mule, and Agathea Chunky Loafer, among other standout styles.

Anderson will be in stylish company at the pre-wedding soirée. Other reported attendees include Gigi Hadid and boyfriend Bradley Cooper, Jack Antonoff and his sister Rachel, and bridesmaid Selena Gomez, who wore a black lace dress paired with plenty of dazzling diamonds.

As for whether Reformation will earn a spot in Swift's own wedding-weekend wardrobe? That remains to be seen. The rehearsal dinner look is just the latest stylish nod from a friend who knows the bride's taste inside and out.
