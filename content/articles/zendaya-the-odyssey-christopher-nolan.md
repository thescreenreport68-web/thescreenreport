**Zendaya has joined the cast of Christopher Nolan's epic fantasy film *The Odyssey*, set for release in 2026.** The actress, fresh from acclaimed roles in *Challengers* and *Dune: Part Two*, will appear in the adaptation of Homer's ancient Greek poem. Nolan and his wife, producer Emma Thomas, are making the film through their Syncopy production company.

The film stars Matt Damon as Odysseus, the Greek king of Ithaca, and Anne Hathaway as his wife, Penelope. The ensemble cast also includes Tom Holland, Robert Pattinson, Lupita Nyong'o, and Charlize Theron. The story chronicles Odysseus's long, perilous journey home after the Trojan War and his encounters with mythical beings.

## What Role Will Zendaya Play in *The Odyssey*?

The specific character Zendaya will portray in **Christopher Nolan's *The Odyssey*** has not been officially disclosed. The film's announced cast features a mix of Greek heroes, gods, and mythical creatures from the epic poem. Her casting was noted in her career biography, confirming her involvement in the high-profile 2026 project alongside her other scheduled films like *Dune: Part Three*.

## Why Is Zendaya's Casting a Major Career Move?

Joining a **Christopher Nolan** film is a significant milestone, signaling her ascent among Hollywood's most sought-after actors. Nolan is one of the industry's premier auteurs, with his films grossing over $6 billion worldwide and earning numerous accolades, including two Academy Awards. For Zendaya, whose leading films have grossed over $3.9 billion, this collaboration represents a powerful alignment of commercial success and directorial prestige.

This move continues her strategic shift into prestigious, director-driven projects following her work with Denis Villeneuve on the *Dune* saga and Luca Guadagnino on *Challengers*. It further distances her from her Disney Channel origins and solidifies her status as a serious, bankable leading actress.

## What Other Projects Does Zendaya Have in 2026?

2026 is shaping up to be a monumental year for Zendaya. In addition to *The Odyssey*, she is set to reprise her role as Chani in Denis Villeneuve's *Dune: Part Three*. She will also star opposite Robert Pattinson in the romantic drama *The Drama*, return as Rue Bennett for the third season of HBO's *Euphoria*, and reprise her role as MJ in *Spider-Man: Brand New Day*.

Her upcoming slate demonstrates her remarkable range and workload, balancing franchise blockbusters, auteur-driven epics, and intense character drama.

## How Has Zendaya's Career Led to This Point?

Zendaya's path from Disney Channel star to Nolan collaborator has been marked by deliberate, acclaimed choices. She broke out with her Emmy-winning performance in *Euphoria*, which redefined her public image. Major film roles in the *Spider-Man* series and the *Dune* franchise established her global box-office appeal. Her recent leading turn in *Challengers* earned her a Golden Globe nomination and proved her ability to anchor a sophisticated adult drama.

Her accolades, including two Primetime Emmy Awards and a Golden Globe, alongside being named one of *Time* magazine's 100 most influential people, have cemented her as a formidable creative force. Nolan's casting is a testament to her proven talent and star power.

## When Will 'The Odyssey' Be Released?

*The Odyssey* is currently scheduled for release in 2026. As with most Nolan productions, specific plot details and character breakdowns remain closely guarded. The film is one of the most anticipated projects on the horizon, combining a legendary director with a timeless story and a star-studded cast.
