Gold Derby has released its early odds for the 2026 Emmy casting races, and they point to three front-runners with major series implications. According to the outlet's predictions, *The Pitt* leads for Drama Casting, *Widow's Bay* is favored for Comedy Casting, and *Beef* is the top contender for Limited/Movie Casting.

## What does a casting win mean for the series?

The predictions are more than just category forecasts. Gold Derby notes a strong historical correlation between winning a casting award and winning the corresponding top series prize. Over the past 12 years, the drama series and casting winners have matched eight times, the comedy winners have aligned nine times, and the limited series winners have mirrored each other 11 times. That’s a combined accuracy rate of 78%.

## Who are the predicted winners?

*The Pitt* holds a dominant 94% chance to repeat its Drama Casting win from last year, buoyed by 13 acting nominations for its second season. In comedy, the freshman Apple TV+ series *Widow's Bay* leads with 79% odds, having secured six acting nods.
