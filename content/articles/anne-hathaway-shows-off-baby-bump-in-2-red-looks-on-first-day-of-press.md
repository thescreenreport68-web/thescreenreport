What's in a color? For Anne Hathaway, a pair of red looks on her first day of press for *The Odyssey* has set tongues wagging.

The actress was seen in New York City on Tuesday, June 30, beginning promo for the upcoming Christopher Nolan film. According to photos published by Just Jared, she showcased two all-red outfits from Ashlyn, accessorized with red heels, jewelry, and sunglasses. The looks prominently featured her baby bump.

This sighting follows another from the day before, where she was photographed cradling her bump while holding a book from Sarah J. Maas's *Crescent City* series. The timing of these appearances, combined with the choice of a form-fitting red ensemble for a major press day, has led to online speculation among fans about the significance of the moment. Some are interpreting the coordinated, bold fashion statement as a potential signal or celebration related to her pregnancy journey.

This has not been confirmed by Anne Hathaway, their representatives, or any official source — it is unverified and currently circulating as speculation.

The press day itself was for *The Odyssey*, a "mythic action epic" adaptation of Homer's poem. Hathaway stars as Penelope, wife to Matt Damon's Odysseus and mother to Tom Holland's Telemachus. The film boasts a star-studded cast including Zendaya, Jon Bernthal, Charlize Theron, Lupita Nyong'o, and Robert Pattinson, and hits theaters on July 17.

Interestingly, Hathaway's red carpet choice coincided with the 20th anniversary of *The Devil Wears Prada*, which premiered on June 30, 2006.
