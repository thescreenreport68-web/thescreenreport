A new trailer for Gal Gadot's upcoming Prime Video film *The Runner* has arrived, according to IMDb. The first look shows the star in a relentless race for survival, setting the stage for a high-stakes thriller.

The project sees Gadot taking on a central role in a suspenseful narrative built around a frantic chase. The premise centers on a woman who finds herself on the run, forced to navigate a dangerous and unpredictable situation. The trailer promises a tense, action-driven story for the streaming service.

## Who is starring in 'The Runner'?

Gal Gadot leads the cast of the new film, directed by Kevin Macdonald, alongside co-stars Damian Lewis and Alfred Enoch. The film represents another major streaming venture for the actor following her work in other high-profile projects.

*The Runner* streams exclusively on Prime Video starting September 2, 2026. The release of the trailer indicates the marketing campaign is now underway ahead of the film's premiere.
