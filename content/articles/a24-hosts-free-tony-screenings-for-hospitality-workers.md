The studio is partnering with OpenTable and Square for the events.

The screenings, dubbed “Industry Night,” are intended for servers, dishwashers, line cooks, maître d’s and other restaurant staff. They will take place across 25 cities, from Austin and Phoenix to Philadelphia and San Diego.

## Who is hosting the screenings?
Each screening will be hosted by a local chef. The list of hosts includes Federico Castellucci in Atlanta, Tavel Bristol-Joseph in Austin, and Brian Howard in Las Vegas. Staff from OpenTable and Square’s restaurant networks, as well as workers from other local restaurants, will be invited.

## When does 'Tony' open in theaters?
It stars Dominic Sessa as a 19-year-old Anthony Bourdain spending a summer in Provincetown, where he finds work in a restaurant kitchen run by an enigmatic chef played by Antonio Banderas.

The “Industry Night” initiative serves as a targeted preview for the industry that inspired Bourdain's career.
