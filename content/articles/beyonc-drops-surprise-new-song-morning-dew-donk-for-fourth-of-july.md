Beyoncé surprise-released a new song, "Morning Dew (Donk)," as a Fourth of July gift to fans, according to Deadline.

The track marks her first new music in two years and was released via her Parkwood Entertainment company. It was written by Beyoncé, Pharrell Williams, The-Dream, and Darius Dixon, and produced by Beyoncé and Pharrell Williams.

## What is 'Morning Dew (Donk)'?

Per the announcement, the song is a direct nod to her fans, the BeyHive, and commemorates the upcoming celebration of her album *B'Day*. The release of "Morning Dew (Donk)" kicks off a 60-day countdown to the singer's next birthday and the re-issue of the album. The track is accompanied by a lyric video directed by frequent collaborator Cliff Watts, which repurposes old footage.

## Where will the new song appear?

The original album, released on September 4, 2006, was Beyoncé's second #1 album. It debuted atop the US Billboard 200 chart and achieved similar success internationally.
