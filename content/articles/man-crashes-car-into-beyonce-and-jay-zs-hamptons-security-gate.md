A Subaru drove at a high rate of speed up the driveway toward the closed mechanical gate at Beyoncé and Jay-Z’s Georgica Pond property in the East Hamptons. It did not stop.

Police released a report on Friday, July 17, detailing the Wednesday, July 15, incident and the arrest of Keith Webster, a 63-year-old Bronx resident, per *Us Weekly*. A security guard witnessed the crash, which caused "significant damage" to the checkpoint. Webster was later charged with second degree criminal mischief and third degree criminal trespass.

## A Puzzling Scene

When police arrived, Webster was restrained and sitting in front of his vehicle. "He wasn’t making threats. He seemed confused," Detective Sgt. Jennifer Dunn told *The East Hampton Star*. Webster complained of pain and was taken to a nearby hospital before being released for arraignment. His vehicle was impounded.

Detective Sgt. Dunn noted the matter is under investigation. "This could be a health issue but we have to treat it as a crime until proven otherwise," she clarified. Police traced Webster's route via license plate readers and found he had stopped at another home in the area beforehand. According to a detective's statement, he reportedly had "no criminal history" and there were "no weapons found, no notes found, no direct threats made and no direct mention of residents of the house."

*Us Weekly* has reached out to their representatives for comment.
