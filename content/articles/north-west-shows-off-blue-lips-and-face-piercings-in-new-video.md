What's the latest from North West's style laboratory?

In a series of TikTok videos shared on July 2, North debuted a head-to-toe cobalt aesthetic. She rocked vibrant blue hair, blue lips, and an array of face piercings while dancing to tracks like Kesha's "Grow a Pear" and Eiffel's "A Decade in Blue." Kesha, for her part, was feeling the vibes, commenting on one of North’s videos "ICONIC."

This isn't North's first foray into bold body art. The budding singer, who released a track called "PIERCING ON MY HAND," recently turned heads at Paris Fashion Week in June. At the Vetements menswear spring-summer 2027 show, she stepped out with dermal piercings on her hand, angel lip piercings, and additional piercings across her cheeks.

As North experiments, her mom Kim has a clear message for critics: back off. In an October TikTok comment responding to a fan's concern, Kim plainly stated, "This is such a non-issue." The SKIMS founder elaborated on her parenting approach during an appearance on the *Call Her Daddy* podcast. "I'm like, ‘Babe, if you want blue hair, it is what it is,’" Kim recalled telling her daughter. "It makes her so happy. I would never take that creativity away from her."

"I think like the one misconception that people might have is like, ‘Oh, she's trying to be her bestie and lets her get away with a lot,’" Kim explained. "Like, no. North actually has a lot of rules. The one area is I do let her express herself, and I do let her do that. And I love her for it."

The mom of four—which includes Saint, 10, Chicago, 8, and Psalm, 7, with ex Kanye West—sees it as part of the normal journey of growing up. As she put it, "Every kid has phases and growing moments and learning moments and we’ve gotten through all of that." For now, North's phase is a brilliant shade of blue.
