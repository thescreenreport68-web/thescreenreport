Toni Collette and Karren Karagulian are among five actors newly added to the cast of Netflix’s upcoming film *Positano*, according to Deadline. They join previously announced leads Matthew McConaughey and Zoe Saldaña in the project.

The film is directed by Daniel Roher. The story is described as a romantic heist caper set in the titular Italian coastal town, following a charming jewel thief who teams up with a mysterious woman for a high-stakes score.

## Who else joined the cast?

Alongside Collette and Karagulian, the new additions include Salvatore Esposito, Michele Savoia, and Lorenzo de Moor. Character details for all newcomers are currently under wraps. The script for *Positano* was penned by Alessandro Tanaka and Brian Gatewood.

## Who is making the film?

Eric Fellner and Tim Bevan for Working Title are producing the film alongside Derrin Schlesinger. Executive producers include Amelia Granger and Katherine Pomfret for Working Title, as well as the screenwriters Tanaka and Gatewood.

The film adds to Netflix's slate of original features and marks a genre shift for Roher, who is known for documentary work.
