Musician Devon Allman has been cast in his first major acting role in AMC's upcoming NASCAR drama series 'Thunder Road,' according to Deadline. He will star opposite Dennis Quaid in the multi-generational family saga set in the world of stock car racing.

Allman will play Jimmy Fuchs, described as a silver-tongued Southern operator whose easy charm and credentials barely conceal the danger underneath. The character is part of the recurring cast for the series, which is set to begin production this summer and debut on AMC and AMC+ in 2027.

## What is Devon Allman's role?

In 'Thunder Road,' Allman's character, Jimmy Fuchs, is a key figure in the show's world. The series follows the fictional Whitlock family, a blue-collar dynasty whose racing legacy is built from a history of moonshine runs and dirt tracks, now fighting to hold onto their empire. Dennis Quaid stars as the family patriarch, racing legend Duane 'The Wrecking Ball' Whitlock.

## What did the creators say?

Allman expressed his excitement about the role. "Hot damn! Being cast in AMC's Thunder Road is such a thrill," he said. "I'm excited to act alongside this incredible cast and bring the character of Jimmy Fuchs to life. He's quite an ornery cuss, and I am right here for every bit of it."

Series creator John Fusco highlighted Allman's fit for the Southern-rooted story. "We couldn't be more thrilled to welcome Devon Allman to Thunder Road," Fusco said. "As the son of Southern rock icon Gregg Allman, Devon carries a legacy in his blood — but he's a gifted performer and a magnetic presence entirely in his own right. That's exactly the alchemy this show runs on."

Fusco added that Allman brings "a lived-in authenticity you simply can't manufacture" to a story world that mines deep family legacy.

## Who else is in the cast?

The series features an ensemble cast that includes Chase Stokes, Michael Rooker, Matt Barr, [Maggie Grace](/tv/maggie-grace-joins-amc-s-nascar-drama-thunder-road-as-series-regular/), and West Duchovny alongside Quaid and Allman. 'Thunder Road' is created by John Fusco, who will also executive produce alongside showrunner Taylor Elmore and others. The show is produced in association with NASCAR.
