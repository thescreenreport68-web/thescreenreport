The first halftime show in FIFA history is set for July 19, 2026, featuring a star-studded lineup of [Madonna](/music/madonna-celebrates-confessions-ii-album-with-limited-time-pop-ups/), Shakira, BTS, and Justin Bieber, according to The Hollywood Reporter. The 11-minute performance, curated by Coldplay frontman Chris Martin, will take place during the World Cup final between Argentina and Spain.

The final match begins at 12 p.m. PT/3 p.m. ET, with the halftime show expected to start between 12:45 and 1 p.m. PT.

## How can I watch the halftime show live?

The World Cup final and halftime show will be broadcast live on Fox. To stream it, you can use any service that carries the network. Options include DirecTV, which offers a five-day free trial; Fubo; Sling; and Hulu + Live TV, which has a three-day free trial. Subscriptions for these services start at various monthly rates.

## Is there an official streaming service for the World Cup?

Yes. The official 2026 World Cup streamer is Fox One. The halftime show will stream live on this platform. For Spanish-language coverage, Peacock is the official World Cup streamer.
