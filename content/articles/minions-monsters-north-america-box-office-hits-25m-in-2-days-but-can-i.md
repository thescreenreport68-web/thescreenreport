**Minions & Monsters** has reportedly earned $25 million at the North American box office in its first two days, according to IMDb. This strong start puts the animated film in a direct battle with *[Toy Story 5](/movies/toy-story-5-box-office-update-flying-high-supergirl-low/)* for the number one spot this weekend.

## Did Minions & Monsters beat Toy Story 5?

The weekend crown is still up for grabs. The two-day haul for *[Minions](/movies/minions-monsters-trailer-drops-promises-wild-hollywood-origin-story/) & Monsters* is a promising start, but the final weekend rankings will depend on how both films perform on Saturday and Sunday.

## What is the film's global performance?

Globally, the film has grossed a total of $63 million, per verified data. The film was produced on a budget of $85 million before marketing costs.

The film, directed by Pierre Coffin, tells the "rambunctious, ridiculous and totally true story" of how the Minions became Hollywood movie stars, lost everything, and accidentally unleashed monsters upon the world.
