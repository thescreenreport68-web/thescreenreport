The first look at *The [Rings of Power](/tv/jamie-campbell-bower-joins-the-rings-of-power-season-3-as-celeborn/)* season 3 is imminent, with its official trailer expected to premiere online today, July 26, 2026, according to a live blog from TechRadar.

The trailer is set to be unveiled following its debut at the Amazon MGM Studios presentation at [San Diego Comic-Con 2026](/tv/marvel-studios-hbo-apple-tv-return-to-san-diego-comic-con-in-2026/).

## When does season 3 come out?

Fans won’t have to wait long for the full season after the trailer arrives. Amazon confirmed in mid-May that *The Rings of Power* season 3 will debut on Prime Video on **November 11, 2026**. The relatively quick turnaround from the conclusion of filming in December 2025 to a late 2026 premiere has been noted as a positive development for the series.

## What’s the story so far?

TechRadar’s coverage notes that the season 2 finale aired nearly two years prior, setting the stage for the upcoming chapter. While the new trailer will provide the first proper glimpse of the story to come, specific plot details for season 3 remain under wraps. The live blog humorously references the long wait for the trailer, with the reporter joking about staying fueled for the multi-hour countdown.

With the trailer's release, the final promotional push for the highly anticipated return to Middle-earth begins.
