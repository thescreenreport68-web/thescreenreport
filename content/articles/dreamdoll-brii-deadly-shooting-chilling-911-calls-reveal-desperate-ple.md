What does the final, terrifying moment sound like? TMZ has released the frantic 911 calls made in the immediate aftermath of the drive-by shooting that killed influencer DreamDoll Brii, and they paint a harrowing picture of panic and desperation.

The outlet obtained the emergency calls made to Miramar Police following the deadly shooting early Sunday morning. In one call, a man can be heard pleading with the dispatcher, repeatedly shouting, "Please help me! Please help me! Please! Please!" as a woman cries in the background. Another caller, a nearby resident, urgently tells the dispatcher there's a fight and pleads for police and an ambulance.

This audio provides a chilling soundtrack to a tragedy that has stunned the influencer's community. According to Miramar police, 21-year-old Brianna Johnson, known as DreamDoll Brii, was riding in her green Lamborghini Urus around 5:30 AM on Sunday when a white sedan, reportedly a BMW, pulled alongside and opened fire. The Lamborghini, reportedly struck multiple times, then crashed into a nearby home after rolling through a stop sign. Surveillance video obtained by TMZ shows the luxury SUV cruising down a street before a white sedan speeds past and the Lamborghini drifts onto a property.

The shooting was a triple attack. CBS News reports a second victim, identified by Miramar police as 21-year-old Arthur Lee Johnson III—Brianna's cousin—has also died. A third, unidentified victim was hospitalized but has since been released after having his jaw wired shut.

Just hours before the shooting, Brianna reportedly posted videos from a parking lot gathering where people were throwing money. Police are searching for the white sedan involved and have not announced any arrests. The investigation is ongoing, and many details about the motive and suspects remain unknown.
