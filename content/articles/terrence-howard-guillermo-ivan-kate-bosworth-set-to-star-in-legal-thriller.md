Terrence Howard, Guillermo Iván, and Kate Bosworth are set to star in the new legal thriller “Broken Ones,” according to Variety. The film is written and directed by Timothy Gagliardo.

Inspired by the memoir of producer and co-writer Alejandro Zavala, the project explores themes of redemption, justice, and family. It pulls from real-life experiences within the criminal justice system and legal profession.

## Who is playing who?

Guillermo Iván will star as Joseph Santiago, a former felon who uncovers a corporate cover-up while investigating a car accident. Terrence Howard plays Benjamin Drake, a revered attorney seeking justice for the elite. Kate Bosworth appears as Kathleen Sloane, a corporate defense attorney whose morals are tested by her clients.

## What did Terrence Howard say about the film?

In a statement, Howard shared his perspective on the film's deeper questions. “What are we doing all of this for? At the end of the day, are we turning stone into bread to feed ourselves or are we turning stone into bread to feed others? This film explores those questions in a deeply human way,” he said.

A companion film titled “Real Ones,” also drawing from Zavala’s memoir, is in development. The release date and additional casting for “Broken Ones” will be announced later.
