The Movie: A Star-Studded Biopic of the King of Pop

Jaafar Jackson, [Michael](/movies/michael-jackson-s-michael-crosses-1-billion-becomes-highest-grossing-biopic-ever/) Jackson’s nephew, takes center stage in "Michael," a 128-minute music drama directed by Antoine Fuqua. The film traces the legendary artist’s journey from his early days as the lead singer of the Jackson Five to becoming one of the most influential entertainers in history. The story highlights both Michael’s groundbreaking performances and his complex life off-stage.

## Reception: A Hit with Audiences Despite Mixed Reviews

Fans praised Jaafar Jackson’s authentic portrayal and the film’s emotional depth, which helped propel it to blockbuster status. The Jackson estate’s significant investment and involvement, including executive producer Prince Jackson, added credibility and drew attention, despite some family members publicly criticizing the film’s accuracy.

## The Money: Box Office Triumph and Streaming Debut

The film’s commercial success underscores its broad appeal and the enduring fascination with Michael Jackson’s life and music.
