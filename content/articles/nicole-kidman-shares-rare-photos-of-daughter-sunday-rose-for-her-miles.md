What better way to turn 18 than with a runway debut in Paris and a sweet tribute from your Oscar-winning mom? Nicole Kidman celebrated her daughter Sunday Rose’s 18th birthday with a pair of sweet throwback photos. The “Moulin Rouge!” star took to Instagram Tuesday to share snaps of herself holding Sunday Rose as a baby, with the now-model dolled up in a white dress.

A second snap showed Sunday running around while wearing white angel wings.

The birthday wishes poured in. Reese Witherspoon wrote, “Happy Birthday, Sunny 💝.”

She also walked the runway for the fashion house back in January.

Kidman shares Sunday and daughter Faith Margaret with her ex-husband, Keith Urban.

While the family publicly celebrated Sunday's birthday, reports have suggested a strain in her relationship with her father post-split. Neither Urban nor Sunday Rose has publicly commented on the state of their relationship.
