On a since-deleted June 18 episode of her Dumb Blonde podcast, Bunnie Xo joked about her divorce settlement with Jelly Roll. A source now confirms to E! News that the couple has officially settled their split, which the "Wild Ones" singer initially filed on May 18.

E! News reported this; it has not been officially confirmed by Jelly Roll or their representatives, and The Screen Report has not independently verified it.

According to the source, the confidential settlement allows the exes to close the chapter on their 10-year marriage. Bunnie, 46, previously shared that Jelly Roll would be giving her their family compound "because he knows how special it is to me,". She also hinted at financial support, quipping on her podcast, "I joke around with him.

## An Unconventional Next Chapter

The couple, who separated on May 9, have insisted there was no cheating involved. Their story, however, is far from over. Bunnie noted they would be moving forward with having children via IVF surrogacy following their divorce announcement. "We're still having a baby together." she explained.

They also remain committed to co-parenting Jelly Roll's children from previous relationships: Bailee Ann, 18, and Noah, 9. As Bunnie put it, "We're going to co-parent together. J is my best friend. This isn't what you guys think this is. Nobody cheated on the other person. It's literally just we served our purpose for each other."

The pair's relationship began unconventionally. According to Bunnie, she met Jelly Roll in August 2015 at a Las Vegas Country Saloon show before he was famous, when he was living in a van. Despite the end of their marriage, they continue to speak positively about one another. The exact details of their financial and property settlement remain confidential, as does the specific timeline for their surrogacy plans. E! News has reached out to their representatives about the settlement but has not received a comment.
