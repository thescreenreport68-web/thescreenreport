Stevie Nicks made a surprise appearance at the wedding reception of [Taylor Swift](/celebrity/as-taylor-swift-gets-married-former-best-friend-blake-lively-is-out-of/) and Travis Kelce on Friday night at Madison Square Garden, according to a report by Rolling Stone.

The Fleetwood Mac singer took the stage following the couple's ceremony. Details of her performance, including which song she played, remain unclear. Good Morning America hosts Robin Roberts, Michael Strahan, and George Stephanopoulos, who attended the wedding, confirmed the event on their show. Roberts stated, “We can confirm that, yes, Stevie Nicks did perform.”

## Who Else Was at the Wedding?

The ceremony itself was officiated by actor Adam Sandler. Swift’s brother Austin Swift served as her “Man of Honor” while [Travis Kelce](/celebrity/prince-william-joins-new-heights-podcast-on-taylor-swift-and-travis-ke/)’s brother Jason Kelce was the Best Man. The guest list was a spectacle of A-list talent from music, film, and sports.

Musicians in attendance included Swift’s collaborators Jack Antonoff and Aaron Dessner, along with friends like Ed Sheeran, Selena Gomez, the Haim sisters, [Brad Paisley](/celebrity/brad-paisley-kimberly-williams-paisley-thank-taylor-swift-and-travis-k/), Miranda Lambert, Kelsea Ballerini, and Maren Morris. Actors such as Dakota Johnson, Julianne Moore, Hugh Grant, and Jessica Chastain were also present.

On Kelce’s side, his Kansas City Chiefs teammates and coach Andy Reid attended, alongside other NFL players like Matthew Stafford and sports broadcasters Erin Andrews and Greg Olsen.

## How Close Are Swift and Nicks?

The performance underscores a long-standing mutual admiration between the two artists. In 2024, Nicks penned a poem titled “For T — and me” for Swift’s album *The Tortured Poets Department*, which appears on physical copies. Swift also name-dropped Nicks in the song “Clara Bow” from that album.

Nicks has publicly supported Swift’s relationship with Kelce. In a 2024 Rolling Stone interview, she said, “She’s in a good place right now, and I think she has a good man.” She added her hope that the couple would “ride off into the sunset.”

Swift herself has spoken about the positive impact Nicks has had on her life. In an interview on *The Late Show With Stephen Colbert*, Swift said, “I have Stevie Nicks in my life in a way that affects me positively constantly.”
