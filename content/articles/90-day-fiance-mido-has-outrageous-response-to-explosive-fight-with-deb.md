What happens when an explosive fight is followed by weeks of silence and zero accountability? On Sunday's episode of "90 Day Fiancé," viewers saw Mido, 41, deliver a masterclass in deflection.

The episode, reported by Page Six, showed the aftermath of a massive Halloween fight. It began when Mido was cold and rude to Debby's, 55, friends, telling them he didn't care what they thought. The argument escalated into a public shouting match with her friend Astra. Producers had to step in after Debby kicked Mido out of her car when he cursed at her.

In the new episode, Debby revealed Mido had ignored her for weeks. She took him to a lake to talk, but he refused to take any responsibility for his behavior. Instead, he turned the tables. Mido told her he left Egypt and his family to be with her and that he deserved better. When Debby told him she loved him, he got annoyed. "I’m not talk all the time, ‘I love you. I need you,'" he mocked her. When she insisted she knew he loved her, he sarcastically replied, "Very good. Excellent."

He then placed the burden on her, saying it was up to Debby to "fix" things or he would return to Egypt. It’s so frustrating."

Debby noted his singular focus, saying, "Everything since he’s got here has been all about him," referring to his pursuit of a movie career over their relationship, according to Page Six. "But relationships are about compromise, and he’s been single for a long time. I’m starting to see why."
