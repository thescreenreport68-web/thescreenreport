Lee, the Mötley Crüe co-founder, was also baffled, commenting simply, "Amazing. That’s f – – king inspirational."

Springfield responded to the attention on Tuesday, writing, "Good vibes appreciated! … 🤘🏼." Fans in his Instagram comments were reportedly effusive, with comments like "How is this possible?!? 😍" and "You make us all look forward to our 70s!! 😍."
