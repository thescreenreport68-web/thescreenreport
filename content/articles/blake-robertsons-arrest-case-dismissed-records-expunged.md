In October 2025, Blake Robertson was arrested in Nacogdoches, Texas, according to an arrest record viewed by E! News. The 'Ultimatum' contestant was taken in for an incident in which he allegedly put his hands on his girlfriend and fellow contestant, Hayley Hendrich.

Robertson’s attorney, Sean Hightower, told TMZ, "Blake's case was rightfully dismissed by the prosecutor almost immediately, and all records were expunged.". Robertson himself affirmed the account to E! News, stating, "Everything my attorney has said is correct. The case was dismissed within a month. Hayley signed the affidavit. We are still in contact today. We’ve got the reunion coming up soon so everyone stay tuned.".

Hendrich told TMZ she declined to press charges and that she and Robertson had private conversations about the incident. She also expressed a belief that public accountability should be taken as well, according to the publication.

The timing adds a layer of real-world drama to the currently airing fourth season of 'The Ultimatum', which premiered on Netflix on July 15. The season was filmed in the spring of 2025, months before the October arrest. On the show, Robertson and Hendrich entered as a couple, with Robertson presenting the marriage ultimatum.

While the legal case is closed, the revelation lands as fans are watching their televised relationship journey unfold.
