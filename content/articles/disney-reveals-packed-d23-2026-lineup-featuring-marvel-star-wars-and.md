According to GeekTyrant, the event is shaping up to be one of the biggest celebrations the company has ever put together, promising three days packed with major presentations, celebrity appearances, and exclusive reveals.

## What is the schedule for the main showcases?

The weekend's centerpiece presentations will be held at the Honda Center. On Friday, August 14, the Disney Entertainment Showcase will feature surprise celebrity appearances, exclusive first looks at upcoming films and series, and musical performances. The following day, Saturday, August 15, Neil Patrick Harris will host the Horizons: Disney Experiences Showcase, offering a deeper look at future theme park and experience projects.

## Who is being honored at the event?

The Disney Legends Awards Ceremony, hosted by Ryan Seacrest, will close the event on Sunday, August 16. This year's honorees include a distinguished group of creatives and performers such as Chris Berman, Jerry Bruckheimer, Susan Egan, Anne Hathaway, Bob Iger, [Dwayne Johnson](/movies/moana-box-office-day-5-domestic-total-climbs-to-54-9-million/), the Jonas Brothers, Lin-Manuel Miranda, and Alan Tudyk.

Michael Vargo, head of D23: The Official Disney Fan Club, shared his excitement. "At D23: The Ultimate Disney Fan Event, the magic never stops," Vargo said. "This is where fans become part of the story and every part of the Disney universe comes to life."

## What other programming is available?

Across five stages at the Anaheim Convention Center, fans can experience a wide variety of events. These include a new feature-length documentary from Leslie Iwerks, a live recording of the Jonas Brothers' podcast "Hey Jonas!", a nostalgic Disney Rewind Concert, and a one-man concert with composer Alan Menken. Other highlights are a costume contest hosted by Nina West and panels featuring the casts of *The Simpsons* and *The Muppets*.
