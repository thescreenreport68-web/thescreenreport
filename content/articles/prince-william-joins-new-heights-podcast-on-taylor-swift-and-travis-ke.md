The surprise interview was released on the same day that Travis Kelce and his fiancée [Taylor Swift](/celebrity/as-taylor-swift-gets-married-former-best-friend-blake-lively-is-out-of/) are reportedly set to get married at New York's Madison Square Garden.

Jason Kelce expressed his surprise at the royal guest, saying, "We thought were gonna be talking to, like, fat offensive linemen, not kings, queens, princes of other nations." The conversation turned to Swift's influence and her recent performances.

## What Did They Talk About?

During the 30-minute interview, Jason Kelce posed a question to the Prince about iconic Wembley Stadium moments.

[Travis Kelce](/celebrity/travis-kelce-reacts-to-fake-ai-photo-of-taylor-swift-wedding/) later noted he would happily dance alongside Swift on tour again. "Listen, if Tay's gonna sign up for a tour, I'll happily make sure that we bring her back to life," he said.

## What's the Wedding Context?

The podcast episode's release coincides with widespread reports of the Swift-Kelce wedding. Some reports suggest performances from artists including Stevie Nicks and Tim McGraw.
