The new '[Scary Movie](/movies/scary-movie-2026-has-fans-in-a-chokehold-but-not-everyone-is-buying-it/)' film arrives on digital streaming platforms this Tuesday, July 21, according to Forbes. This follows a lucrative theatrical run where the reboot became one of the franchise's most successful entries.

## How much did Scary Movie make?

It was produced on a reported budget of $30 million before marketing costs.

## Who is in the new Scary Movie?

The reboot brings back franchise veterans, starring Damon Wayans Jr., Marlon Wayans, Shawn Wayans, Anna Faris, and Regina Hall. Michael Tiddes directed the film, which spoofs recent horror hits like 'M3GAN,' 'Terrifier,' and 'Get Out.'
