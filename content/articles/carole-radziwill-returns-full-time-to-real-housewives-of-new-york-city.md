Carole Radziwill is returning as a full-time cast member for the upcoming 16th season of *The Real Housewives of New York City*, according to The Hollywood Reporter.

The former cast member, who was initially announced to be returning in a supporting "friend of" role, has been elevated to a main Housewife.

## What does the season 16 trailer reveal?

The trailer for the new season, which also debuted Tuesday, suggests Radziwill will face questions about her past ties to the late financier Jeffrey Epstein and his associate Ghislaine Maxwell. In the footage, she also makes a reference to her personal history, saying, "Everyone’s American Love Story is my American Horror Story."

## Who else is in the new cast?

Season 16 serves as a significant refresh for the Bravo series. Only three of the Housewives from the previous two rebooted seasons—Sai de Silva, Erin Lichy, and Jessel Taank—are returning. They are joined by three new full-time cast members: Hailey Glassman, Erika Hammond, and Daisy Toye.

The series is produced by Shed Media, with Lisa Shannon, Lori Gordon, and [Andy Cohen](/tv/andy-cohen-will-return-alongside-ariana-madix-to-co-host-the-love-isla/) serving as executive producers.
