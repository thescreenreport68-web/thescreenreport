James Van Der Beek's final onscreen performance has been honored by his co-star, who shared warm reflections on the actor's work and personal happiness.

## What Did His Co-Star Say About Working With Him?
June Diane Raphael, his co-star in the project, emphasized the positive atmosphere on set. She noted that having Van Der Beek's family present was a joyful experience for everyone. Raphael told an interviewer, "It was so much fun to have them there and for them to see him work."

Her most significant observation, however, was about his personal life. Raphael's main takeaway from their time together was Van Der Beek's profound devotion to his family. She said, "He was just so enamored with Kimberly. That was really my big take away."

## How Did Van Der Beek View This Chapter of His Life?
The actor's own words from a 2022 interview align with this portrait of contentment. Speaking about his life off-screen, James Van Der Beek stated simply, "I really feel grounded." This sentiment underscores the peaceful chapter he was in during the making of his final project.

## What Is the Legacy of This Final Performance?
Saving the most authoritative voice for last, co-star June Diane Raphael offered a direct message to his fans regarding his final work. She expressed confidence that his longtime supporters would be moved by his last role. Raphael concluded, "I know his fans will be so excited to see him and I think he gives a beautiful performance in the show." His final role in 'Elle' stands as a testament to an actor who found profound satisfaction both in his craft and in his life beyond it.
