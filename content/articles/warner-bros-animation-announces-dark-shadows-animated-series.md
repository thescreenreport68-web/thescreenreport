Warner Bros. Animation is a new adult animated series based on the classic gothic soap opera *[Dark Shadows](/tv/warner-bros-animation-unveils-new-dark-shadows-animated-series-at-anne/)*, according to from Variety. The announcement was made during the studio's showcase at the Annecy International Animation Film Festival on Friday.

The new project is described as a reimagining of the original 1960s cult television series. The report states the show is currently in "very early development." This move confirms Warner Bros. Animation's continued push into the adult animation space, expanding its slate beyond adult comedies into other genres.

## What is the original 'Dark Shadows'?

*Dark Shadows* is an American Gothic soap opera that aired on ABC from 1966 to 1971. It followed the wealthy, troubled Collins family in the fictional town of Collinsport, Maine. The show became a massive cult hit after introducing the vampire Barnabas Collins, played by Jonathan Frid. **The series was known for its blend of family saga, romance, and supernatural horror**, featuring ghosts, werewolves, witches, and time travel across its 1,225 episodes. It spawned two feature films, a 1991 TV remake, a 2012 Tim Burton film adaptation, and a long-running series of audio dramas.

## What does this mean for Warner Bros. Animation?

The announcement highlights Warner Bros. Animation's strategy to grow its adult-oriented programming. By adapting a property with a built-in fanbase and a rich, gothic mythology, the studio is branching out from its established comedy hits. Annecy has become a key venue for studios to preview upcoming adult animation projects, and Warner Bros. used the platform to tease this and other new titles. The *Dark Shadows* animated series joins a growing list of genre-bending adult cartoons aiming for mature audiences.

## What's the status of the new series?

Specific details on the creative team, voice cast, plot, or release platform are not yet available. With the project in its earliest stages, it will likely be some time before more concrete information is revealed. The original series has maintained a dedicated fan community for decades, which will be watching for updates on this new animated interpretation.
