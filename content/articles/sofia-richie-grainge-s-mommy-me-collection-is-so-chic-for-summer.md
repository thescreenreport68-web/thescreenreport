Looking for a chic way to match your little one this summer? Sofia Richie Grainge has you covered—literally. The fashion icon and new mom has officially launched a 'Mommy Me' capsule collection with Amazon Essentials, bringing her signature style to affordable family fashion.

E! News confirmed the collaboration, describing it as a line of "trendy apparel for the whole family" full of pieces so you can "match your tot all summer long without sacrificing style." The collection leans heavily into the "Quiet Luxury" aesthetic Richie Grainge is known for, offering coordinating sets that look more expensive than their Amazon price tag suggests.

The edit is packed with summer-ready staples. Think matching cotton-sateen midi dresses for mom and baby, available in four patterns including dainty florals and gingham. There are also cozy cotton crochet sweater and tank sets, described as "summer's easiest style hack," and playful toddler playsets with elastic waists for easy changes. The hero piece, according to E!, is an A-line sundress with a shirred waist and ruffle detailing, praised by reviewers for being both flattering and comfortable.

Her personal style evolution—from It-girl to a poster child for understated, high-quality basics—has been closely watched and widely emulated. Launching a line that allows fans to dress themselves and their children in that same curated, effortless look is a savvy play. It taps directly into the enduring trend of mini-me dressing, but with the accessible price point and convenience of Amazon.

The collection is live now, just in time for summer outings and, as E! notes, potential Fourth of July photo ops. Whether you're building a full matching wardrobe or just want a sweet coordinated moment, Sofia Richie Grainge has given the people what they want: chic, family-friendly style that doesn't break the bank.
