Apple TV+ has officially released the trailer and key art for the second season of its Spanish-language crime drama *Women in Blue (Las Azules)*. According to IMDb, the new season will debut globally on Wednesday, August 12, 2026.

## What is the new season about?

The series, created by International Emmy Award-winning showrunner Fernando Rovzar (*Monarca*) and Pablo Aramendi (*Tijuana*), is set in 1971 and follows Mexico's first female police force. The new season will follow María, played by Bárbara Mori, who has been promoted to lieutenant. She finds herself torn between her duty and a relentless pursuit of the truth when the body of a student activist is discovered.

## Who else is in the cast?

The returning ensemble includes Ximena Sariñana as Ángeles, Natalia Téllez as Valentina, and Amorita Rasgado as Gabina. Miguel Rodarte, Leonardo Sbaraglia, Christian Tappan, Horacio García Rojas, and Bruno Bichir also star. The series blends crime and drama, exploring themes of defying ultraconservative norms and a pact for justice, based on a true story.

## When can I watch it?

The global premiere is set for August 12, 2026, on Apple TV+. New episodes will then be released weekly through September 30.
