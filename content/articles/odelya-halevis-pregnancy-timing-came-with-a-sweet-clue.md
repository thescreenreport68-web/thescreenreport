For Odelya Halevi, the end of a TV season wasn't just a wrap—it was the start of a whole new chapter. According to People, the actress announced she is pregnant and expecting her first child, a baby girl due in December 2026, with her husband Aaron Mazor.

The star, who plays Assistant District Attorney Samantha Maroun on *Law & Order*, shared the happy news in an exclusive interview with People on Monday (July 20). She told the outlet she discovered the pregnancy right after filming wrapped. "I found out I was pregnant the traditional way — peeing on a stick," she recalled. "It was the day after we wrapped season 25 of _Law & Order_, and looking back, the signs were definitely there."

Halevi said the signs were there if she'd looked. "I had spent the entire week casually wandering into the producers’ office and helping myself to all the chocolate they had sitting out, which is very unlike me," she said. "I remember thinking, ‘Who is this person?’ And then it hit me."

She described telling her husband, luxury real estate agent Aaron Mazor, as a deeply intimate moment. "It’s something we both really wanted so deeply. I remember shaking [from] excitement," she told People. "It was so incredibly intimate and special."

Now further along, Halevi is savoring the milestones. "Just a few days ago, I felt her kick for the first time," she shared. "I don’t think there’s a way to fully put that feeling into words."

No further details about the pregnancy or baby shower plans have been shared yet.
