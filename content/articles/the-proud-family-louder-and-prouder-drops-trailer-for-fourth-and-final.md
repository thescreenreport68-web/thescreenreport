The farewell season is scheduled to premiere on the streaming platform on July 29.

## What is the final season about?

The new season explores the aftermath of the Season 3 finale, which saw the central character, Penny Proud, run away with her boyfriend, Kareem. Viewers can expect a fresh wave of family adventures that will challenge Penny’s courage and sense of identity. The official synopsis for the series describes it as following "the adventures and misadventures of newly 14-year-old Penny Proud and her family as they navigate modern life with hilarity and heart."

## Who is in the cast?

Kyla Pratt leads the returning voice cast as Penny, alongside Tommy Davidson as Oscar, Paula Jai Parker as Trudy, and Jo Marie Payton as Suga Mama. Karen Malina White and Soleil Moon Frye also return as Dijonay and Zoey, respectively. The final season adds a notable list of guest talent, led by Mariah Carey. She will be joined by Chloe Bailey, Ali Wong, Romany Malco, and Lashana Lynch, among others. Recurring guest stars Anthony Anderson and Tiffany Haddish are set to return as well.

## Is this really the end for the series?

While the series concludes with its fourth season, it has been confirmed that a stop-motion holiday special titled *A Proud Family Wizmas* will premiere on Disney Plus later this year.
