Jennifer Garner and Judy Greer have joined the cast of the upcoming "13 Going on 30" reboot, according to Just Jared.

The actresses were spotted filming a scene with new star Emily Bader in Santa Monica, California, on July 21.

## What are their roles?

It is unclear if Garner and Greer are reprising their original roles as Jenna Rink and Lucy Wyman or playing new characters in the reboot. Plot details remain unknown as of July 2026.

Garner was previously announced as an executive producer on the Netflix film, but her on-screen involvement had not been confirmed until now.

## Who else is in the reboot?

Emily Bader, who recently starred in the Netflix rom-com "People We Meet on Vacation," will lead the cast. Logan Lerman, Taylor Zakhar Perez, Jessica Alba, Adeline Rudolph, Taylor Ortega, Tim Meadows, and Dan Bucatinsky are also set to star. Brett Haley, who directed Bader in "People," is directing the new movie.

The original 2004 film, which starred Garner and Greer and was directed by Gary Winick, follows a 13-year-old girl who magically wakes up as her 30-year-old self. It has a 65% critics score on Rotten Tomatoes and grossed over $96 million worldwide.
