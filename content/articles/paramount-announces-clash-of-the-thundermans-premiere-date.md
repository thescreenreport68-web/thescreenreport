Paramount+ has announced that the movie '[Clash of the Thundermans](/tv/percy-jackson-season-3-sets-november-release-date-with-first-teaser/)' will premiere on Thursday, September 3 on Paramount+ and Nickelodeon, according to TV Series Finale.

The film is based on the Nickelodeon series 'The Thundermans'. The story follows youngest sibling Chloe as she develops a new and unpredictable superpower, causing the Hero League to demand they send her away to a superhero boarding school. This splits the Thunderman family down the middle.

## Who is in the cast?

Modern Family’s Ariel Winter joins returning cast members Kira Kosarin, Jack Griffo, Maya Le Clark, Diego Velazquez, Addison Riecke, Chris Tallman and Rosa Blasi. Dana Snyder, Daniele Gaither, and Robyn Bradley also star.

## When and where can you watch it?

'Clash of the Thundermans' will premiere Thursday, September 3 on Paramount+ and debut on Nickelodeon at 7 p.m. (ET/PT) that same day. The movie is produced by Paramount Television Studios.

Fans of the original series can look forward to the reunion movie arriving soon.
