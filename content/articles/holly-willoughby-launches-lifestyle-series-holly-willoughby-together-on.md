British television presenter Holly Willoughby is launching a new lifestyle series on YouTube titled *Holly Willoughby Together*, according to Deadline. The first episode is scheduled to debut on the platform on July 28.

Willoughby is one of the UK's most prominent TV hosts, known for long-running shows like *This Morning* and *Dancing on Ice*. Her new digital project will see her film episodes in a purpose-built studio, hanging out with guests and discussing a chosen topic each week.

## What is the new YouTube show about?

The series will release two episodes per week organized under five pillars: Talk Together, Cook Together, Look Good Together, Better Together, and Hang Out Together. The initial lineup of guests includes actor Stanley Tucci, comedians Katherine Ryan and Joanne McNally, and former Spice Girls members Emma Bunton and Nicole Appleton. Willoughby will also speak with campaigners like Ellen Froome and chefs including Lauren Leyva.

## What has Holly Willoughby said about the project?

In a statement, Willoughby expressed her excitement about the venture. "I’m so proud of the work all the team who make Together have put into this exciting new project," she said. "We’ve had a lot of fun developing it and look forward to seeing what you think."

The show is produced by Hungry Bear Media, the company run by Willoughby's husband Dan Baldwin. According to her representatives, Willoughby has a digital audience of 16 million. The series marks her first major push onto YouTube, following a return to television with shows like ITV's *You Bet!* and Netflix's *Celebrity Bear Hunt with Bear Grylls*. Tech health provider Voy is the founding brand partner for the new series.
