## What is 'The Baby-Sitters Club' about?

The official synopsis describes a classic coming-of-age story: "Seven junior-high-school girls organize a daycare camp for children while at the same time experiencing classic adolescent growing pains." The film blends comedy, drama, and family themes.

## Who is in the cast?

Directed by Melanie Mayron, the movie features a young ensemble cast. It stars Schuyler Fisk, Bre Blair, and Rachael Leigh Cook. Larisa Oleynik, Tricia Joe, and Stacy Linn Ramsower also appear in the film.

## When did the movie come out?

*The Baby-Sitters Club* was released on August 18, 1995. This new trailer offers a fresh look at the film.
