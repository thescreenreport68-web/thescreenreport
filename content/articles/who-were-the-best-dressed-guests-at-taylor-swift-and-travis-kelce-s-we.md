When Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce said "I do" at Madison Square Garden, the world was watching. While there didn't appear to be a specific dress code, the couple's famous friends delivered a masterclass in glamour.

Selena Gomez, the "Only Murders in the Building" star, was "dressed to kill in all-over-embellished Oscar de la Renta." Swift's supermodel pal Gigi Hadid looked "picture perfect in pink beaded Wiederhoeft." Jennifer Lopez exuded elegance in a drop-waist Bach Mai dress, while Karlie Kloss looked "every inch the golden goddess" in a champagne-colored strapless Tove column gown.

The guest list was a fascinating mix of Swift's inner circle and Kelce's football world. Gracie Hunt, daughter of Chiefs owner Clark Hunt, seemed to pay homage to the New York venue with a "Lady Liberty-like" teal Roberto Cavalli gown. Lena Dunham brought a bold pop of color in a fuchsia Christian Cowan blouse.

The wedding itself was a major event, according to PEOPLE.

After the ceremony, it seems the jumbotrons outside Madison Square Garden displayed the happy news: "JUST&T MARRIED!" The celebration marks the culmination of a relationship that led to an engagement announcement in August 2025. With such a star-studded guest list and impeccable fashion on display, the wedding was as much a red carpet event as it was a personal milestone.
