A new casting call is open for the upcoming Netflix series "[The Roman](/tv/vincent-d-onofrio-kate-mara-join-oscar-isaac-in-netflix-s-las-vegas-drama-t/)," which stars Oscar Isaac and is produced by Martin Scorsese.

According to a report from Backstage, the series is looking for talent to portray high-end casino gamblers in background roles. Roman Candle Casting is seeking New York City-based individuals aged 21 to 75 for the parts.

## What is the filming schedule?

Talent selected for the background roles will work on multiple dates between July and December 2026. The reported pay rate for the work is $187 to $231 per day.

## What is 'The Roman' about?

The project is described as an eight-episode drama series that explores the high-stakes and ruthless present-day Las Vegas casino business.
