Eric McCormack, Andrea Bang, and Josh Ramsay will appear in guest roles for the second season of OUTtv's series *The Dressing Room*, according to The Hollywood Reporter.

McCormack, best known for *Will & Grace*, joins a roster of guest performers that also includes Laura Bell Bundy, Katie Findlay, Eduard Witzke, Jacqueline Robbins, and Joyce Robbins. The series follows the fictional touring musical *Balloon Boy*, chronicling the backstage meltdowns, romantic entanglements, and ambitions of its cast and crew.

## What did the creators say about the casting?

Creators Paul Almeida and Makayla Moore shared their enthusiasm for McCormack's involvement in a statement. "Having Eric join *The Dressing Room* meant the world to a show built by and for theatre people. From our first meeting, he was such a great team player and collaborator, it felt like he wanted us to succeed, and was sending the elevator back down," Almeida said.

Moore added, "We are so lucky to have someone like him (McCormack) in our community and are equally privileged to have so many talented people — all the amazing cast, directors and crew who came out to support this project."

## Who else is returning for Season 2?

The returning core cast includes Irene Karas Loeper, Jason Sakaki, Jeffrey Follis, and Alex Gullason. The second season was shot over 10 days in Vancouver, with episodes directed by Nat Boltt, Jennifer Copping, Milo Shandel, Josh Epstein, and Paul Almeida.
