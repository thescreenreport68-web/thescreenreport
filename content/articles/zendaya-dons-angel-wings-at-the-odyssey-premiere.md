Zendaya attended the New York City premiere of "[The Odyssey](/movies/christopher-nolan-s-the-odyssey-earns-top-reviews-sparks-imax-70mm-ticket/)" this week wearing a white strapless gown and feathered wings.

According to Coveteur, the star completed the look with a gown by Matières Fécales. The outlet described her as looking "angelic with the wings to match."

Her wardrobe for the film's premiere events included multiple standout looks. For the same New York premiere, she also wore a custom white Louis Vuitton cut-out gown with dramatic ruffled sleeves and lace details. For the London premiere of the film, she wore a Daniel Roseberry design from Schiaparelli's Fall 2026 couture collection, featuring a molded bodice and a shimmery skirt.

Coveteur noted that [Zendaya](/movies/zendaya-the-odyssey-christopher-nolan/) "wore goddess-level looks throughout the whole The Odyssey press tour."
