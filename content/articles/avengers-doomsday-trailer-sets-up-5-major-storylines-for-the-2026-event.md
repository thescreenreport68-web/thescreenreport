Marvel's first full trailer for *[Avengers: Doomsday](/movies/avengers-doomsday-trailer-drops-with-fans-cheering-robert-downey-jr-s/)* sets the stage for a massive cinematic collision, according to reporting from thenationalnews.com. The footage confirms the scale of the threat and the surprising alliances—and conflicts—that will define the film.

## Who is the main villain?
Robert Downey Jr. makes his debut as Victor von Doom, and the trailer quickly establishes his power. In a key moment, he stops Thor's enchanted axe, Stormbreaker, with a single hand. This simple act frames Doom as a threat capable of challenging the mightiest heroes. The character is known in the comics for blending technology and magic, a duality the film may explore.

## Which heroes are returning?
Chris Evans is back as Steve Rogers, seen reuniting with Thor and reaching for Mjolnir. This confirms his worthy status but leaves his origin unclear—is this the original Steve restored or a multiversal variant? The trailer also brings together a sprawling cast, including Pedro Pascal's Reed Richards, Anthony Mackie's Sam Wilson, and Florence Pugh's Yelena Belova.

## Why are the heroes fighting each other?
The footage suggests the Avengers, Fantastic Four, and [X-Men](/movies/marvel-s-new-x-men-movie-casting-reveal-expected-at-comic-con/) may not start as allies. Shang-Chi is shown fighting Gambit at the X-Mansion, while Mystique impersonates Yelena. These clashes hint at a deeper crisis where the survival of one universe might endanger another, forcing otherwise heroic characters into conflict before they can unite against Doom.

## What is the larger threat?
This concept, introduced in *Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness*, could mean the heroes must choose which world survives, shaping Doom's potential role as a ruthless problem-solver.

Directed by Joe Russo, *Avengers: Doomsday* is scheduled for release in theaters on December 18, 2026.
