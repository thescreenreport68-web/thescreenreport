What does a bridesmaid wear to a Taylor Swift wedding rehearsal? Selena Gomez gave us a glittering hint.

On Thursday, the 33-year-old singer shared two selfie videos on her Instagram Story, offering a glimpse of her outfit as she headed to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding rehearsal dinner at Madison Square Garden, according to Page Six. The clips showed Gomez in a black floral lace dress, but it was the accessories that truly dazzled.

She accessorized with a pair of Fernando Jorge Flicker drop earrings, featuring 1.92 carats of pear-cut diamonds and retailing for $29,000, and a matching Flicker ring worth $11,000. Completing the look was a swipe of glossy lip color from her own Rare Beauty brand, complementing a smokey eye shadow.

It's no surprise Swift's longtime friend would be in attendance. Page Six exclusively reported earlier in the day that Gomez is set to be a bridesmaid for the main event. According to Page Six, the rehearsal dinner was an intimate event with 100 guests, taking place at the same location where the couple is set to throw an enormous party Friday night. Other A-list attendees reportedly included Jack Antonoff, Bradley Cooper, Gigi Hadid, and Erin Andrews.

The friendship runs deep. Swift was a guest at Gomez's September 2025 wedding to Benny Blanco, dressed in a gold Oscar de la Renta gown with floral appliqués. Now, Gomez returns the favor, stepping into a supporting role for one of the most anticipated celebrity weddings in recent memory.

With the main event still to come, all eyes will be on Gomez and the other potential bridesmaids to see what they wear for the official ceremony. Will the diamonds make another appearance, or is there an even more spectacular look waiting in the wings? For now, Gomez's rehearsal dinner glam has set the bar—and the sparkle—incredibly high.
