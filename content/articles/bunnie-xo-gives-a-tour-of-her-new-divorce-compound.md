The Dumb Blonde podcast host was giving him a tour of her sprawling new residence, which she received as part of her divorce settlement from country star Jelly Roll.

The Instagram footage showcased the home's over-the-top luxuries. Matthews marveled at a rainfall shower head installed on a private open-air balcony, asking, "It's a whole shower outside?" The bathroom featured black-and-white marble floors and floral ceilings. A hidden staircase sat behind a bookshelf. The tour's centerpiece was a massive bed made of two double mattresses placed side-by-side, prompting Matthews to quip, "Who is she f--king in this bed?" He added, "This bed is nuts."

Bunnie Xo, whose real name is Alisa DeFord, also showed off her walk-in closet with a dedicated shoe area and a marble-countertop "makeup room" lit from underneath. "This is stupid."

The house appears to be the compound Bunnie was building with Jelly Roll (born Jason DeFord) before the pair split in May after 10 years of marriage. On a June 18 episode of her podcast, Bunnie confirmed the amicable terms, sharing that her ex-husband was giving her the property. "J is so f--king sweet that he's giving me that compound in the divorce, because he knows how special it is to me," she said, per E! News. The divorce was finalized earlier this month.

Jelly Roll has echoed the friendly nature of the split. During a concert last month, he stated, "Me and my wife are best friends. We will always be best friends," and shut down infidelity rumors by adding, "Nobody cheated on nobody." The former couple reportedly plan to move forward with IVF treatments together.
