The animated movie *Swapped* has officially broken into Netflix’s All-Time Top 10 Most Popular English-Language Films list, according to a report from What’s on Netflix.

**How did ‘Swapped’ reach the Top 10?**
To claim its spot, the film successfully ousted the action blockbuster *The Gray Man*, which previously held the #10 position. This staggering viewership tally also crowns *Swapped* as Netflix’s second best-performing animated movie ever, trailing only *KPop Demon Hunters*. The film’s sustained popularity is attributed to strong word-of-mouth and critical praise for its visuals and emotional resonance.

**What did the director say?**
Director Nathan Greno provided an exclusive statement to What’s on Netflix celebrating the news. “Animation has the power to connect with people in a uniquely deep way, and seeing audiences around the globe embrace Swapped has been truly rewarding,” Greno said. “To see that connection lead to Swapped becoming one of Netflix’s ten most popular films of all time is an incredible honor and a testament to the passion, talent, and heart of our entire crew.”

In an additional quote provided to press, Greno added, “Swapped celebrates the idea that we’re stronger together, and there’s nothing more rewarding than knowing that message has connected with so many people across the globe.”

**What happens next?**
The film still has around 10 days left to accumulate views before its final 91-day premiere window closes. *Swapped* spent 10 consecutive weeks in Netflix’s global top 10 lists, reaching the #1 spot in early May. The success marks a significant win for Netflix’s partnership with Skydance Animation.
