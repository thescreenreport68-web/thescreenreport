You'd think a gathering of online creators would be all good vibes. Instead, Streamer University just became the stage for a very public, very messy feud between Jordyn Lucas and Skai Jackson.

Clips circulating on social media, particularly via The Shade Room, show the two sharing wildly different versions of what went down. The drama kicked off when Jordyn was filmed walking outside at night with a group, airing her frustrations. She claimed she initially tried to be on Skai's side but said the actress eventually left her "fed up.". Jordyn took several shots, calling Skai's attitude "dirty" and questioning why she came to the event in the first place, suggesting her behavior was "Disney Channel" kid energy. She added that "they be off" before making more remarks about Skai's personality.

## Skai Responds, Jordyn Escalates

Skai later appeared to respond in a separate clip. She claimed Jordyn approached her to gossip about someone, but she shut the conversation down. Skai maintained she wasn't wrong and explained she came to Streamer University simply to "have fun.".

Jordyn wasn't letting it slide. In another video, she watched Skai's explanation on her phone before showing it to nearby cameras and reacting with a look. She denied talking negatively about fellow streamer Ezee. Then she escalated the back-and-forth further, saying, "This girl is as slow as she looks,"

Social media is divided, with some supporting Jordyn's critique and others backing Skai's version of events. The incident highlights how Streamer University has become a new stage for viral drama, where celebrity feuds play out in real-time for an eager audience.
