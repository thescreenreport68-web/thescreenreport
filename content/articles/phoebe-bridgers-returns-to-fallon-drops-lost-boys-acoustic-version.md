This marked her second consecutive night on the show. She had performed the original version of the track the previous evening.

## What is the new album?

The single “Lost Boys” is the first preview of Bridgers’ upcoming solo album, titled *Lost Weekend*. The album is scheduled for release on August 14, 2026, via Dead Oceans. This will be her first new solo album since 2020’s *Punisher*.

## What is the context for the song?

The original version of “Lost Boys” was released last month with a Renaissance faire-themed music video. Bridgers co-produced the track with Jack Antonoff, Tony Berg, and Ethan Gruska. Her boygenius bandmates Lucy Dacus and Julien Baker contributed backing vocals.

In a past interview, Bridgers spoke about her creative goals. “I want to listen to music where nobody’s ever said the exact way that I’ve felt,” she said. “And then I listen to it, and I’m like, ‘Holy shit, they nailed it.’ I hope I’m doing that for people.”
