What does a new chapter look like for Amy Schumer? This July 4th, it involved a blue one-piece, a crowd of old friends, and a caption that said it all.

Schumer, 45, shared photos from her holiday weekend on Instagram Sunday, according to Page Six. In one shot, she posed in a blue one-piece bathing suit, coordinated with blue shorts and a matching baseball cap. She credited the clothing brand Mother and *Saturday Night Live*'s Chloe Fineman in the post.

The images quickly sparked chatter online, with fans noting her trim figure. This comes after Schumer has been open about significant life changes over the past year, including a major weight loss and her split from husband Chris Fischer.

Last December, Schumer told her Instagram followers she lost 50 pounds to deal with a hormonal disorder, according to Page Six. In a video last March, she said of the medication, "I couldn’t handle it," adding, "I don't know if they've changed the formula. Whatever." More recently, she revealed she underwent "a botched colonoscopy" that affected her libido, according to Page Six, sharing the health update during a "Dear Media by Night" event for the "Not Skinny But Not Fat" podcast. At that event, she noted she feels "happier than I've ever been before," according to Page Six.

Her personal life has also shifted. Schumer and Fischer, 45, announced their split last December after almost eight years of marriage. In a lengthy Instagram post at the time, she reportedly wrote, "Blah blah blah Chris and I have made the difficult decision to end our marriage after 7 years," adding, "We love each other very much and will continue to focus on raising our son. We would appreciate people respecting our privacy at this time." The star, who shares a son with Fischer, reportedly filed for divorce from the pro chef earlier this year.

Her July 4th post seemed to focus on her support system. Alongside the swimsuit photo, she shared a group shot with the caption: "Just a group of 25 friends who have known each other for 35 years with their families. Love you all." She simply titled the holiday celebration "Epic Forth."

While the photos highlight her physical transformation, Schumer's own words suggest her current priority is her happiness and her circle. The weekend snapshots offer a glimpse into a summer spent with lifelong friends, as she moves forward from a year of profound change.
