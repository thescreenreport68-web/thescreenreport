For years, Jennifer Aniston has been the one answering the questions. This fall, she'll be asking them — and to her own boyfriend, no less.

On July 20, 2026, Just Jared reported that the actress will interview her partner, transformational coach Jim Curtis, live on stage at a 92NY event in New York City on September 2, and it’s all to promote Curtis’s upcoming book, "The Book of Possibility: Release, Align, Become".

In-person attendance starts at $95, while a live-streaming option is also available. Both ticket types include a copy of the book, which officially releases on September 22.

## The Wellness Visionary

According to the event description from 92NY, Curtis blends neuroscience, meditation, and hypnotherapeutic coaching in his work. The evening promises a candid discussion of his personal story and methods, and the description suggests it will conclude with a live, music-accompanied guided visualization for the audience.

This marks a rare public collaboration for the couple, merging Aniston's star power with Curtis's niche in the wellness world. While the event confirms her support for his latest project, her day-to-day involvement in his coaching practice remains a private matter.
