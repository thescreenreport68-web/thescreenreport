The new volume will feature films on Vince Young, Raygun, and Mr. T. The first film, *Untold: The Testimony of Vince Young*, will be directed by John Henion.

## What are the new documentaries about?

The Vince Young film explores the former NFL quarterback's career, described as "riddled by injuries, conflict and sudden loss." The Raygun documentary will cover the 2024 Paris Olympics breakdancer's viral performance and the ensuing memes and conspiracy theories. The Mr. T film promises the "extraordinary origin story" of the actor before his Hollywood fame.

The series hails from creators Chapman Way and Maclain Way, who previously directed *Wild Wild Country* and *The Battered Bastards of Baseball*.
