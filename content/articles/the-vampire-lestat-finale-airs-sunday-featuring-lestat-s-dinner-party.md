The season finale of *[The Vampire Lestat](/tv/costume-designer-lex-wood-breaks-down-the-vampire-lestat-rockstar-wardrobe/)* airs on Sunday, July 19, 2026, on AMC. The episode concludes the show's first season, which premiered in June.

## What Happens in the Finale?

According to TVLine, the finale is set on the night of the band's final show. Lestat becomes the surprise guest of honor at a fancy dinner party. During the event, he must confront past and present demons as various factions within the story become clear.

The show, created by Rolin Jones, stars Sam Reid as Lestat de Lioncourt. The cast also includes Jacob Anderson, Assad Zaman, and Delainey Hayles. The series follows Lestat as he goes on tour with his band while being haunted by muses from his past, with his growing influence causing others to contend with his power.

## Where Can I Watch 'The Vampire Lestat'?

As of July 2026, *The Vampire Lestat* is available to stream on AMC+, Philo, and YouTube TV, among other platforms. The season finale will air live on the AMC channel.
