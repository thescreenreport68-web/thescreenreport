Could a Spider-Man and a rap princess be an item? That's the question buzzing after Tobey Maguire and Ice Spice were reportedly spotted together at a celebrity-packed event in the Hamptons.

The sighting that sparked the chatter happened at Fanatics CEO Michael Rubin's annual Fourth of July White Party. The bash was held on Wednesday night, July 1, at his private estate in the Hamptons, New York. According to photos published by Just Jared, the 51-year-old actor and the 26-year-old rapper were identified among the A-list crowd.

The images, credited to Backgrid, are the fuel for the fire. One photo appears to show the pair embracing, and possibly sharing a kiss. However, the outlet notes it's equally plausible they were simply leaning in close to hear each other talk over the loud party noise. Adding another layer of uncertainty, Just Jared reports it's unclear if the woman in the photos is "100%" Ice Spice.

This has not been confirmed by Tobey Maguire, their representatives, or any official source — it is unverified and currently circulating as speculation.

The rumors come as Maguire has kept his dating life relatively private in recent years. He was previously linked to model Lily Chee in 2024, a rumor his ex-wife later publicly dismissed. Later that same year, he was also linked to model Mona Tougaard. The White Party itself was a major draw, rumored to have been scheduled early this year due to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding happening on July 3. It brought together stars from music, film, sports, and beyond.

For now, the story lives in the realm of fan speculation and grainy party photos. Without clearer confirmation or a statement from either party, the nature of their interaction remains one of the Hamptons' summer mysteries.
