Is Ciara Miller in Fiji or the Hamptons? The answer came straight from the source, and it’s good news for *Summer House* fans.

During the final episode of *Love Island: Aftersun* on Saturday night (July 11), the 30-year-old nurse turned reality star revealed her whereabouts—and her plans. Ciara, who has been co-hosting the weekly recap show for *Love Island USA* season eight alongside Tefi Pessoa this summer, was not on location in Fiji for the finale.

Instead, she was filming remotely from New York City. And the reason? “I have *Summer House* to shoot — Season 11. I gotta get back to the Hamptons,” she revealed, according to Just Jared.

This confirmation puts an end to weeks of speculation about her return. Her future on the Bravo series had seemed uncertain following past relationship drama among castmates. However, it seems a Bravo executive had already teased her comeback back in June, according to a report.

Filming for *Summer House* typically kicks off over the July 4th weekend, but production was delayed by a week this year. Ciara’s announcement coincided with her hosting duties for a Pandora event in Montauk the same day. She reportedly shared a glimpse of the day on Instagram, captioning a post: “A day in Montauk, styled with a lil extra sparkle. Hosting the Pandora Wonders Beach Club was such a beautiful way to celebrate summer, good company, & pieces that elevate every look,” adding, “Thank you,@theofficialpandora, for having me.”

While her return is now official, the dynamics of the new season—and how she’ll navigate the shared history with her castmates—remain to be seen. For now, fans can rest assured that Ciara Miller is officially back in the house.
