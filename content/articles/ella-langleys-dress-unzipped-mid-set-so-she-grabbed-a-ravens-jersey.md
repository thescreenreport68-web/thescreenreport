Country star Ella Langley’s dress came unzipped during her opening set for Morgan Wallen in Baltimore over the weekend, according to her own account on Instagram Stories. The singer, 27, shared the details of the quick-thinking save the following day.

"Yesterday my entire dress came unzipped on song #2 in a 60 minute set in the pouring rain in a stadium full of people," Langley recounted via Instagram Stories. The mishap happened during the second song of her 60-minute set in the pouring rain at a packed stadium.

Langley immediately tossed a purple Baltimore Ravens football jersey over her damaged gown. The oversized jersey covered the problem area while letting the pink fringed skirt of her dress peek out from underneath. It’s unclear who provided the jersey, though Page Six noted speculation that a fan had given it to her.

Fans on TikTok celebrated the clever fix. One user wrote, "He might have chose Texas, but she chose Baltimore," referencing Langley’s hit song "Choosin’ Texas". Other viewers joked they’d prefer to see her in jerseys for their own hometown teams, like the Packers, Rams, Bills, Seahawks, or Cowboys. "She looks good in anything," another fan enthused.

Langley is reportedly riding a wave of chart success, with Page Six noting she currently holds three of the top four spots on the all-genre Billboard Hot 100. The Baltimore audience’s reaction suggests her on-the-fly problem-solving only added to her appeal.
