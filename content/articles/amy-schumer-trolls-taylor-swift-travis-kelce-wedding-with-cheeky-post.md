Ever feel like you missed the party of the year? Amy Schumer seems to.

Sharing a photo of herself in casual athleisure wear, a straw visor, and a fanny pack, Schumer quipped, "Rehearsal dinner was sick. Who know [sic] what she has planned for us tonight."

Though she didn't name the couple, the context was clear. In another post, she shared a still from her Hulu show *Life & Beth* featuring her onscreen wedding to Michael Cera, captioning it, "Okay so why weren’t we allowed in?" The posts, reported by E! News, sparked amusement as fans recognized the playful jab at the high-profile event.

The Swift-Kelce wedding, held on July 3, reportedly included about 1,000 guests. A rehearsal dinner the night before, on July 2, was attended by a star-studded inner circle including Selena Gomez, Jack Antonoff, and Lena Dunham, among others like Dakota Johnson, Bradley Cooper, and Ed Sheeran.

While Schumer's posts are confirmed jokes, they playfully highlight the exclusive nature of one of the year's most talked-about celebrity events. The wedding itself has been kept largely private, with the couple sharing few details publicly since Swift's initial, excited revelations.
