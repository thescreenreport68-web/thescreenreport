Vince Gilligan will be honored with the Eva Monley Award, the highest career prize from the Location Managers Guild International, according to Deadline.

## What is the Eva Monley Award?

The Eva Monley Award is the LMGI’s top honor. In a statement, the guild praised Gilligan’s understanding of how locations shape narrative. “In his work, locations do not simply serve the narrative — they help create it,” the LMGI said. “Landscapes become characters.”

## What did Vince Gilligan say about the honor?

Gilligan expressed deep respect for location professionals in a quote provided to Deadline. “It’s not easy to knock on a homeowner’s door and convince them to let 300 strangers traipse through their kitchen,” he said. “They’re some of the hardest-working folks in the business, and I couldn’t respect them more.”

The guild also announced it will present its 2026 Lifetime Achievement Award to Mike Fantasia and its Trailblazers Award to the Association of Film Commissioners International.
