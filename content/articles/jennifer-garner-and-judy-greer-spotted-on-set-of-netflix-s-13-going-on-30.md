[Jennifer Garner](/celebrity/jennifer-garner-s-new-series-is-a-streaming-hit-this-summer/) and Judy Greer have been spotted on the set of Netflix's upcoming '13 Going on 30' movie in Santa Monica, California, according to What's on Netflix. The pair were photographed filming scenes alongside the project's new leading lady, Emily Bader.

This marks a significant return for the stars of the original 2004 romantic comedy. Garner was previously known to be involved as an executive producer, but her on-camera participation was unconfirmed until now. It is currently unclear if the actors are reprising their original roles as Jenna Rink and Lucy Wyman or playing new characters.

## Who else is in the cast?

In addition to the returning stars, the ensemble has expanded. Taylor Zakhar Perez, known for *Red, White & Royal Blue*, has officially boarded the film. He joins a cast that already includes Emily Bader and Logan Lerman in the lead roles, with Jessica Alba and Adeline Rudolph in supporting parts. The full cast list also features Taylor Ortega, Tim Meadows, and Dan Bucatinsky.

## When does filming wrap?

Production on the movie is currently underway. Cameras began rolling on June 23, 2026, and filming is scheduled to conclude on August 10, 2026. The production is filming exclusively in Los Angeles and the surrounding Thirty Mile Zone. Brett Haley is directing the film from a script by Hannah Marks.
