A new trailer for Marvel's Wolverine is out, but the fan conversation isn't about what the studio likely intended. According to GameSpew, while the preview spotlights Logan fighting for his past, audiences are taking away a completely different message.

This story centers on the 2024 film *Deadpool & Wolverine*. The official synopsis describes a listless Wade Wilson forced to suit up again with a reluctant Wolverine when his homeworld faces a threat. Directed by Shawn Levy, the film blends action, comedy, and science fiction, starring Hugh Jackman as Logan alongside Ryan Reynolds, Emma Corrin, and Matthew Macfadyen.

## What is the movie about?

The plot follows Wade Wilson, who has left his mercenary life as Deadpool behind. He is pulled back into action alongside Wolverine to confront an existential danger. The film is a major superhero team-up set within the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

## Where can I watch it?

*Deadpool & Wolverine* was released in theaters on July 26, 2024. As of July 2026, it is available to stream on Disney Plus in the United States. The film was a massive box office success.
