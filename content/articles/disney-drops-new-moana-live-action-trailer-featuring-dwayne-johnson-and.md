The official trailer for Disney's live-action reimagining of *[Moana](/movies/catherine-laga-aia-and-dwayne-johnson-lead-moana-live-action-cast/)* has arrived, giving fans their first full look at the upcoming adventure. According to ABC7 Bay Area, the new footage stars Catherine Lagaʻaia in the titular role and features Dwayne Johnson returning as the charismatic demigod Maui. Tickets for the film are now on sale ahead of its theatrical debut this summer.

Directed by Thomas Kail, the film follows the journey of teenage Moana, who answers the Ocean's call, voyaging beyond the reef of her island, Motunui, with the infamous demigod Maui to restore prosperity to her people. The story blends family-friendly fantasy, comedy, and adventure on the high seas.

## Who is in the live-action 'Moana' cast?

The ensemble brings together a mix of new faces and returning voices from the animated classic. Catherine Lagaʻaia takes on the lead role of the determined wayfinder Moana. [Dwayne Johnson](/celebrity/wil-wheaton-slams-coward-dwayne-the-rock-johnson-over-keeping-his-poli/) reprises his fan-favorite role as the shape-shifting demigod Maui. They are joined by Rena Owen as Moana's revered Gramma Tala, John Tui as her father, Chief Tui, and Frankie Adams as her mother, Sina. Jemaine Clement also returns as the giant, glitz-obsessed crab Tamatoa.

## When does 'Moana' come out?

*Moana* sets sail exclusively in theaters on **July 10, 2026**. The film is a major theatrical event, with a reported budget of $250 million. The production is backed by a powerhouse team of producers including Johnson, Beau Flynn, Dany Garcia, Hiram Garcia, and Lin-Manuel Miranda.

## What can fans expect from the music?

The film will feature original songs by Lin-Manuel Miranda, Opetaia Foa'i, and Mark Mancina, plus an original score composed by Mancina. This promises to carry forward the musical spirit of the original while offering new compositions for the live-action format. The themes of friendship, exploration, and leaving one's family remain central to this sea-faring tale. With tickets already available, the voyage to Motunui is just over the horizon.
