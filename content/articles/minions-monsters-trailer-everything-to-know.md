The official *[Minions](/movies/minions-monsters-trailer-drops-promises-wild-hollywood-origin-story/) & Monsters* trailer confirms the little yellow henchmen are heading to 1930s Hollywood for their wildest, most meta adventure yet. Directed by Pierre Coffin, the film's synopsis promises a "rambunctious, ridiculous and totally true story" that sees the Minions conquer Tinseltown, lose everything, and accidentally unleash monsters upon the world.

## What is Minions & Monsters about?

According to the official synopsis, the plot follows the Minions as they achieve movie star fame in 1930s Hollywood. Their success is short-lived. After losing everything, they inadvertently unleash a horde of monsters across the planet. The second half of the story sees the chaotic crew banding together to save the world from the very mayhem they created. This premise blends classic Minions comedy with fantasy adventure and a satirical look at old Hollywood filmmaking.

## Who is in the voice cast?

The voice ensemble is packed with major talent. Pierre Coffin, the franchise's co-creator, returns to voice the Minions themselves. He is joined by *South Park* co-creator Trey Parker as Goomi and Oscar winner Allison Janney as Olivia. The cast also features two-time Oscar winner Christoph Waltz as Max and Oscar winner Jeff Bridges in the dual role of Frank and Elwood. Jesse Eisenberg voices Dort, with Zoey Deutch as Debbie, Bobby Moynihan as Phillips, and Phil LaMarr as Howard. In a notable cameo, *Star Wars* creator George Lucas appears as himself.

## Where does this fit in the franchise?

*Minions & Monsters* is a new spin-off, not a direct sequel to *Minions: The Rise of Gru*. Its 1930s Hollywood setting and meta filmmaking themes offer a fresh backdrop. The film operates as a standalone origin tale of sorts, exploring how the Minions first intersected with human celebrity culture. With a reported budget of $85 million, it's a major animated event aiming to expand the universe's lore beyond supervillainy. Pierre Coffin's direction ensures the signature chaotic charm remains intact, even as the story ventures into monster territory.

## When does Minions & Monsters come out?

The film is scheduled for a wide theatrical release on July 1, 2026. As of June 2026, no streaming plans have been announced. The PG rating and 90-minute runtime position it as a summer family movie event. Fans can expect the trailer to play ahead of other major animated and family releases throughout the year.
