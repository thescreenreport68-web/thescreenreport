The upcoming film *Spider-Man: Brand New Day* is teased to feature a major crossover with the Daredevil corner of the [Marvel](/movies/spider-man-brand-new-day-trailer-drops-teasing-a-world-without-recogni/) Cinematic Universe, according to a report from Collider. This connection is suggested through plot details that follow the events of *Daredevil: Born Again* season 2.

## How Does It Connect to Daredevil: Born Again?

According to Collider, this absence creates a power vacuum in New York City's criminal underworld. This vacuum is reportedly a catalyst for the rise of new threats in *Brand New Day*, including the villain known as the Rose.

## What Is the 'Gang War' Storyline?

The report draws a direct line to the "Gang War" storyline from Marvel's comic books. In that arc, the Kingpin's disappearance led various crime lords, including the Rose, Silvermane, and Hammerhead, to battle for control. Spider-Man, Daredevil, and the Punisher all became entangled in the conflict, with the wall-crawler often at odds with the other vigilantes over how to handle the violence.

While *Spider-Man: Brand New Day* turns to Frank Castle's Punisher for help, their alliance is historically shaky. In the comics, Spider-Man stopped Punisher from killing gang lords, leading to a physical confrontation. Daredevil also objected to Spider-Man's intervention, arguing for a legal solution. The film appears to be adapting these tense dynamics for the MCU.
