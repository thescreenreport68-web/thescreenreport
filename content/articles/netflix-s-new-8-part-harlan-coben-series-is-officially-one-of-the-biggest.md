The new Harlan Coben thriller series **“I Will Find You”** has become one of Netflix’s biggest hits of the year, according to a report from MovieWeb. The eight-part series debuted on June 19 and rapidly climbed the streaming charts.

This achievement is notable because the series had only been available for 11 days before the reporting period closed.

## What Is 'I Will Find You' About?

The crime thriller stars Sam Worthington as a father wrongly imprisoned for his son's murder. The cast also includes Britt Lower, Milo Ventimiglia, and Erin Richards. While a major viewership success, the series has drawn mixed critical reactions, holding a 62% rating from critics on Rotten Tomatoes.

## What Is the Next Harlan Coben Netflix Series?

Netflix is already developing its next project based on Coben's work. The streamer is adapting the popular Myron Bolitar character, a basketball star-turned-sports agent. Colin Woodell is set to play Bolitar, with KJ Apa starring as Win Lockwood and Diane Guerrero as Esperanza Diaz. The series will follow Bolitar as he navigates the high-stakes world of sports representation. No release date has been announced, but the project is envisioned to run for multiple seasons.
