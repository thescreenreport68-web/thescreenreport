The best movies on Max offer a stunning mix of modern blockbusters, timeless classics, and animated masterworks, with Hayao Miyazaki's Oscar-winning *Spirited Away* claiming the top spot.

Every film here is a confirmed part of the Max streaming library as of [June 2026](/streaming/best-movies-on-prime-video/), saving you the frustration of searching for a title only to find it's left the service. This is a curated list for viewers who want quality, not just quantity.

## What is the best movie on Max?

The best movie on Max is **Hayao Miyazaki's *Spirited Away***. This 2001 animated fantasy isn't just a film; it's a fully realized world. It follows Chihiro, a sullen ten-year-old who stumbles into a spirit realm and must work in a bathhouse for gods to save her parents. The film’s hand-drawn animation feels alive, its creatures are endlessly inventive, and its story balances wonder with genuine emotional stakes. It remains the only traditionally animated film to win the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature, a testament to its unique power. For sheer artistic ambition and heart, it is the essential Max watch.

## Is The Dark Knight on Max?

Yes, Christopher Nolan's *The Dark Knight* is available to stream on Max. This 2008 superhero film redefined the genre with its gritty, crime-epic scale and Heath Ledger's iconic, Oscar-winning performance as the Joker. It’s more than a comic book movie; it’s a tense thriller about chaos versus order, anchored by Christian Bale's Bruce Wayne and Aaron Eckhart's tragic Harvey Dent. Its practical effects and IMAX-shot sequences still look breathtaking. If you're in the mood for a blockbuster with brains and brawn, this is the one.

## Which classic films are worth watching on Max?

Max's selection of classics is surprisingly robust. Akira Kurosawa's *Seven Samurai* (1954) is the blueprint for the epic action movie, a three-hour saga about a village hiring warriors for protection that has influenced countless films since. Charlie Chaplin's *Modern Times* (1936) is a hilarious and poignant satire of the industrial age that remains remarkably relevant. Satoshi Kon's *Perfect Blue* (1998) is a psychological thriller that dissects fame and identity with frightening precision, its influence seen in works from *Black Swan* to *Inception*. These aren't museum pieces; they are vital, electrifying cinema.

## Can I watch the entire Lord of the Rings trilogy?

All three extended editions of Peter Jackson's *The [Lord of the Rings](/streaming/where-to-watch-the-lord-of-the-rings-movies/)* trilogy are available on Max. This is a rare chance to experience *The Fellowship of the Ring*, *The Two Towers*, and *The Return of the King* in one seamless, marathon-ready location. The epic scale of the world-building, the emotional depth of the character journeys, and the groundbreaking visual effects have lost none of their power. *The Return of the King* swept the Academy Awards, winning Best Picture. For pure, immersive fantasy storytelling, this trilogy is unbeatable.

## What are the best animated movies on Max?

Beyond *Spirited Away*, Max is a treasure trove for animation fans. *Your Name.* (2016) is a breathtakingly beautiful body-swap romance that blends urban and rural Japanese landscapes with a time-bending narrative. Miyazaki's *Princess Mononoke* (1997) is a darker, more complex ecological epic, while *Howl's Moving Castle* (2004) is a whimsical and moving anti-war fable. For a modern masterpiece, *Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse* (2023) pushes visual storytelling into new dimensions with its kaleidoscopic animation styles and heartfelt story. Each film showcases animation not as a genre, but as the pinnacle of cinematic art.
