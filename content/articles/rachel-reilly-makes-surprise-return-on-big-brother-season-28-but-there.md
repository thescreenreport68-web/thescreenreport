How long can a legend last? For Rachel Reilly on the latest *Big Brother*, the answer was about one episode.

The 41-year-old reality star made a surprise return during the CBS competition series' season 28 premiere on Thursday night (July 9), as reported by Just Jared. Her entrance was classic Rachel: full of bravado and ready for a fight. "Get excited, America, because that’s right, I’m back b-tches," she declared in her first confessional. She added, "Now I’m back, with a vengeance, ready to get that double crown."

Her arrival sent shockwaves through the house. Fellow returning houseguest Angela Murray admitted in a confessional, "I did not expect **Rachel**, honestly. My mouth, you could pick it up off the floor. I don’t want to get paranoid, but she can build an army against me if she wanted to."

This marked Reilly's fourth stint in the *Big Brother* house, following her original season in season 12, her win in season 13, and a return as the mystery houseguest in season 27. However, her latest chapter was short-lived. According to Just Jared, at the end of the premiere episode, **Rachel was quickly eliminated after being chased into a volcano by a velociraptor**.

The bizarre exit aligns with teasers from CBS ahead of the season. The network had promised that "additional surprise houseguests will be revealed on air" and described a season where "nothing is as it seems, where every twist rewrites the rules, and where time becomes the ultimate twist." Reilly's whirlwind appearance certainly fit that bill of unexpected twists.

While her time was brief, it reignited fan excitement for the potential of more alumni returns. The question now is whether other familiar faces will pop up, only to meet similarly fantastical fates. For now, Rachel Reilly's quest for a "double crown" is, quite literally, extinct.
