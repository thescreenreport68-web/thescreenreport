Jackie Chan is returning for a new sequel in his long-running action-adventure franchise. According to MovieWeb, the actor is deep into production on *Armour of God IV: Ultimatum*, with filming set to begin in Spring 2026.

The sequel will continue the story of Chan’s recurring character, the Asian Hawk, who is drawn back into action when a global hunt erupts over an ancient artifact known as the Tumar.

## Who is directing the new film?

Robert Kun, known for his stunt work on films like *Day Watch* and *Wanted*, is directing the project. Newly released behind-the-scenes footage shows Chan and Kun in the studio reviewing concept art for the film.

## When will the movie be released?

Production on *Armour of God IV: Ultimatum* is confirmed to begin filming in Kazakhstan in a few months' time, with a planned worldwide release in 2027. This will mark Chan’s first movie filmed in that region.
