The film marks a reunion between the two-time Oscar winner and director Marielle Heller, who previously guided Hanks to an Academy Award nomination for his role as Fred Rogers in *A Beautiful Day in the Neighborhood*.

The studio secured the project after a competitive bidding process, beating out two other interested parties. Hanks' production company, Playtone, is producing the film with Leah Holzer and in cooperation with Major League Baseball.

## What is 'The Comebacker' about?

The film is based on a short story by Dave Eggers. Hanks stars as a struggling sportswriter covering a major league team. The story turns when a pitcher is struck in the head by a line drive on the mound — an incident that reignites the writer's love of the game. Deadline describes the project as "a love letter to baseball."

## Who else is starring in the film?

The cast includes Callum Turner, Bad Bunny, and Colman Domingo. The film is positioned to offer a significant role to an up-and-coming leading man, though specific character details have not been disclosed.

## What else opens on July 30, 2027?

*The Comebacker* will face direct competition at the box office from another major studio release.

The release date places the film squarely in the heart of the lucrative summer movie season, a period often dominated by big-budget action and family films.
