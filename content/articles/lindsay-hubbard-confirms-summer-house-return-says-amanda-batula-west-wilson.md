While some faces are leaving the Hamptons house, one familiar one is firmly back in the mix. Lindsay Hubbard confirmed her return to *Summer House* for its upcoming eleventh season, which is currently filming.

She will once again appear on a limited basis, splitting her time between mom duties and the share house. "I’m excited to spend a hot toddler summer with Gemma every other weekend, and then a hot MILF summer with my friends when she’s with her dad," she told the outlet.

With Amanda Batula and West Wilson not returning, Hubbard endorsed the casting change. "It’s the right decision. We need positive, fun vibes," she said, adding, "Even if people wanted to hate-watch or watch it for the cringe, I don’t think it’d be fun for anyone else."

## A Fresh Start

Hubbard hinted at a lighter season ahead, describing a cast ready to move on. "Pretty much everyone is single, which is very old-school Summer House, and we’re all ready to put this scandal behind us. It’s going to be a good time," She also acknowledged the grind of her dual reality TV life, which includes starring in *In the City*. "Filming two shows back-to-back is not for the weak," she noted, but said she had no real hesitation about coming back.

Her return is framed as a pivot toward fun, with the exits of Batula and Wilson positioned as a necessary step to reset the house's energy after past drama.
