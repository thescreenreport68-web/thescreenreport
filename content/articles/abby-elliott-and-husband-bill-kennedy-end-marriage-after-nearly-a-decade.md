For nearly a decade, Abby Elliott and Bill Kennedy presented a united front. That chapter is now closed. The filing cites "irreconcilable differences" as the reason for the split.

In a statement to the outlet, Elliott addressed the difficult decision. "After much thought and prayer, I have made the difficult decision to file for divorce. This has not been an easy decision," she said, according to Us Weekly. The couple had been married for nearly 10 years.

Elliott emphasized that her focus is now on her family. "As a working mom, I am committed to giving my daughter the love, stability, and support she will need while we navigate this next chapter together." she stated.

## A Year of Splits

The breakup adds to a long list of celebrity separations that defined 2026. Us Weekly's roundup noted splits began in January 2026 with Bill Hader and Ali Wong, and continued through the year with couples like Jason Biggs and Jenny Mollen, Ariana Grande and Ethan Slater, and Courteney Cox and Johnny McDaid. Elliott and Kennedy's filing in July joined that wave.

While the legal proceedings begin, Elliott has asked for space. her statement concluded. No further details about the divorce or custody arrangements have been made public.
