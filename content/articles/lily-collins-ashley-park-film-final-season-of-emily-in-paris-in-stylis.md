What does the final chapter of Emily Cooper's Parisian adventure look like? If the first on-set photos are any indication, it's going to be a fashion spectacle. Lily Collins and Ashley Park were spotted filming scenes for the sixth and final season of *Emily in Paris* on Thursday, July 2, in Paris, France, according to Just Jared.

The co-stars were seen chatting and sitting together outside, fully immersed in their iconic roles. Collins, reprising her role as marketing whiz Emily Cooper, made a dramatic entrance in a red, black, and white patterned coat with shaggy white trim. She completed the look with a sleek short bob, dangling earrings, and a gray shoulder bag. In another moment, she layered a gray coat over the outfit while walking away, phone in hand.

Meanwhile, Ashley Park, who plays aspiring singer Mindy Chen, brought a major pop of color to the Parisian set. She was photographed in a head-to-toe bright pink patterned suit, accessorized with a matching beret, gloves, handbag, and knee-high boots. Park was also seen carrying several boxes and shopping bags, including one from the perfume house L’Artisan Parfumeur, hinting at a potential shopping-centric storyline.

This filming sighting confirms production is fully underway for the show's farewell season. Netflix previously renewed *Emily in Paris* for season 6, announcing it would be the series' final chapter. The cast has been globe-trotting for this last shoot; earlier this year, Collins was spotted filming scenes with co-star Lucas Bravo (who plays chef Gabriel) in Greece. In early June, she was also seen at the Grand Prix in Monaco, filming for the same season.

The stylish new looks have already set fan forums abuzz, with many speculating about the narrative directions these bold fashion choices might signal for Emily and Mindy's concluding arcs. While the plot details for the final season remain under wraps, the commitment to extravagant, character-defining style appears stronger than ever. The sighting solidifies that the show is racing toward its grand Parisian finale, promising one last dose of glamour, romance, and, of course, unforgettable fashion.
