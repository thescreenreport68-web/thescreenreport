Javier Bardem and Amy Adams walked the red carpet for a special screening of their show's season finale on Tuesday, according to upi.com. The event was held at the DGA Theater in New York City.

The actors were joined by their co-stars, showrunner [Nick Antosca](/tv/nick-antosca-adapts-cape-fear-for-apple-tv-sustains-the-original-s-blunt/), and executive producers Martin Scorsese and Steven Spielberg. Scorsese and Spielberg were the director and producer, respectively, of the 1991 film adaptation of the same story.

## What is 'Cape Fear' about?

'Cape Fear' is a psychological thriller series streaming on Apple TV. It is based on the John D. MacDonald novel *The Executioners*. Adams and Patrick Wilson play married attorneys Anna and Tom Bowden. Their family is terrorized by Max Cady (Bardem), a convicted murderer seeking revenge on the couple for his imprisonment.

The series premiered in June, and its season finale is scheduled for release on July 31.

## Who else was at the screening?

In addition to the lead stars and producers, several cast members attended the New York event. According to the report, Lily Collias, Malia Pyles, Jamie Hector, and Margarita Levieva were also present on the red carpet.

The story of *Cape Fear* has been adapted multiple times, including as a 1962 film starring Gregory Peck and Robert Mitchum.
