So much for that plus-one. New York Knicks star Josh Hart is publicly shooting down a report that he and his teammates are on the guest list for Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding.

The rumor started when Page Six reported this week that the couple had invited "the starting lineup for the Knicks" to their nuptials. The report noted Swift was in attendance for one of the Knicks' finals games at MSG.

Fellow Knicks player Jeremy Sochan saw the report and jokingly asked Hart on social media if he could be his plus one. Hart's reply was swift and definitive: "lol bro this fake news."

Josh Hart and their representatives have denied this, and The Screen Report has not independently verified it — it remains unconfirmed.

The wedding is shaping up to be a major celebrity event. Hundreds of stars are expected at the Friday ceremony at Madison Square Garden, following a rehearsal dinner held at the same venue on Thursday night. The connection to the Knicks isn't entirely out of left field; Swift has been a visible courtside supporter of the team, which adds a layer of plausible deniability to the initial rumor.

For now, Hart's denial stands as the only direct comment from the alleged invite list. Whether the other named players received invites—or if the whole team was just caught in some pre-wedding gossip—remains a mystery.
