Prime Video will premiere its Muhammad Ali limited series, 'The Greatest,' on Wednesday, November 4, according to Deadline.

The eight-episode series stars Jaalen Best as the boxing champion, humanitarian, and global icon. The premiere date was revealed Saturday during a presentation at Essence Festival of Culture, where creator, showrunner, and executive producer Ben Watkins, star Jaalen Best, and cast members Dana Gourrier, Amin Joseph, Kai Parham, and Michael Ealy were featured. The streamer also released a first-look teaser.

'The Greatest' is billed as an intimate exploration of Ali's life. Executive produced by Lonnie Ali, the widow of the late boxing legend, the series is the first authorized scripted project about his life. It will feature defining moments in Ali's life and career, both in and out of the ring.

Alongside Watkins' Blue Monday Productions and Michael B. Jordan's Outlier Society, producers include Authentic Studios, Roc Nation, Polygram Entertainment, and Grace: A Storytelling Company.
