Shakira and Burna Boy's FIFA World Cup anthem "Dai Dai" has taken the top spot on Billboard's Latin Airplay chart. According to Billboard, the song vaulted from No. 4 to No. 1 for the chart dated July 25, delivering Shakira her 25th No. 1 on the ranking and handing Burna Boy a chart-topper on his first visit.

Latin radio stations during the July 10-16 tracking week. This latest win extends Shakira's record for the most Latin Airplay No. 1s among women in the chart's 32-year history.

## How many No. 1s has 'Dai Dai' earned worldwide?

Since its debut, "Dai Dai" has collected 10 different No. 1 chart placements globally. Its chart-topping run began on June 27 with the Billboard Colombia Hot 100 and Switzerland Songs charts. It later led the Billboard Global 200 and Global Excl. U.S. charts, and now adds Latin Airplay to its list of achievements.

## What does this mean for each artist's chart history?

For Shakira, this marks a return to the Latin Airplay summit after more than a year, following the one-week reign of "Soltera" in January 2025. For Burna Boy, it's a historic breakthrough, marking his first No. 1 on any Latin chart on his first try. The song is also his second chart-topper of 2026, following a featured run on Gunna's "wgft," which led the Rhythmic Airplay chart earlier this year.

The song's global momentum began with its premiere at the 2026 FIFA World Cup opening ceremony in Mexico City on June 11. Its chart domination may continue, boosted by a halftime-show performance during the World Cup final on July 19.
