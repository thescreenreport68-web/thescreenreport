The Horror Hit That Defied the Odds

Directed by Curry Barker, '[Obsession](/movies/obsession-box-office-day-64-adds-790-000-as-domestic-total-hits-256-8-million/)' is a 108-minute horror-thriller. Its premise taps into a classic horror trope with a modern twist: After breaking the mysterious "One Wish Willow" to win his crush's heart, a hopeless romantic finds himself getting exactly what he asked for but soon discovers that some desires come at a dark, sinister price.

Michael Johnston leads the cast as Bear, the romantic protagonist whose wish spirals into terror. He is joined by Inde Navarrette as Nikki, the object of his affection, and Cooper Tomlinson as Ian. The supporting cast includes Megan Lawless as Sarah, Andy Richter as Carter, Haley Fitzgerald as Viola, Darin Toonder as Harry, and Anthony Pavone as Reggie, all caught in the escalating nightmare.

### A Staggering Box Office Return

The film's financial performance is the stuff of Hollywood legend. The film is now available for streaming on Peacock Premium and Peacock Premium Plus.

## At the Box Office

Obsession has grossed $258,491,000 at the domestic box office, 66 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 1,524 theaters. The film added $730,000 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $443 million. It carries a reported production budget of $750,000.
