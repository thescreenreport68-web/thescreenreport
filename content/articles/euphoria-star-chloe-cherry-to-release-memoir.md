[Chloe Cherry](/celebrity/euphoria-star-chloe-cherry-to-release-memoir-detailing-adult-film-past/) will release a memoir titled *Somewhere Dark and Hot*, according to a report from The Hollywood Reporter.

The *[Euphoria](/celebrity/zendaya-movies-and-tv-shows/)* star's book will recount her time working in the adult entertainment industry and her subsequent ascent into the public eye after being cast in Sam Levinson's HBO drama.

## What will the memoir cover?

The forthcoming book promises an unflinching look at Cherry's personal and professional journey. It will detail her entry into the adult film industry at age 19, her rapid rise to prominence there, and the pivotal shift to mainstream acting. Her breakout role as the scene-stealing Faye Valentine on *Euphoria*'s second season served as a catalyst for her career transition, leading her to retire from pornographic acting in early 2022.

Cherry's story is one of dramatic reinvention. Before landing the role on *Euphoria*, she had even starred in an adult parody of the series itself. Her performance was singled out by critics, making her one of the show's unexpected breakout stars.

## What has Chloe Cherry done since Euphoria?

Since her debut on the HBO series, Cherry has focused on building a career in screen acting and modeling. She signed with the British modeling agency Anti-Agency London and made her runway debut for LaQuan Smith during New York Fashion Week in February 2022.

In 2024, she starred in the experimental sci-fi film *www.RachelOrmont.com* and appeared in Charli XCX's music video for the single "360." Her upcoming projects include a starring role in the comedy-drama film *Tuna Melt*, written and directed by Eddie Huang.

## What is known about Chloe Cherry's background?

Born on August 23, 1997, Cherry was raised in Pennsylvania. She has described herself as a poor student during her school years. At 19, she moved to Miami to begin her career in adult entertainment, eventually appearing in over 200 films and garnering over 125 million views on Pornhub.

Her move to Los Angeles and signing with Spiegler Girls solidified her status in the industry before the opportunity with *Euphoria* arrived. She has been open about her personal life, describing herself as polyamorous.
