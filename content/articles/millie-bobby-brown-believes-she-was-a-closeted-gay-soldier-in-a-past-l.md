What does a birthmark on your lower back mean? For Millie Bobby Brown, it's evidence of a dramatic past life.

The 22-year-old actress shared her unique theory during an interview with Capital Buzz while promoting a project with co-star Louis Partridge. According to a report from Just Jared, Brown stated she believes she was a closeted gay soldier in a former life.

"Oh, I actually know exactly who I was. I died… Basically, I have a birthmark [on] my lower back, the very bottom of my kind of spine, which is obviously meant to be the way that you died in your past life," Millie shared.

She elaborated on her imagined demise, linking it to historical warfare. "In war, some, well, I don’t know if this happened a lot or not, but… to kill people but make them have a painful death, they would put swords up people’s spines," the *Stranger Things* alum explained. "So they were completely paralyzed, but they would obviously kind of die, and that’s how I think I died. So I think I might have been a soldier."

Beyond the soldier identity, Brown added another layer to her past-life persona, saying she was "hopefully a closeted gay man." Her co-star Louis Partridge, however, reportedly doesn't believe in past lives, which ended that particular line of discussion.

The interview was part of a promotional tour. During the same tour, according to Just Jared, Millie also shared a wild story about her friendship with Tom Cruise. The outlet did not include details of that anecdote in its report. Brown's comments represent her personal beliefs and are not presented as established fact.
