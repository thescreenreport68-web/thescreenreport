Is the Summer House gang getting a major makeover? According to Bravo, filming for the 11th season of the reality hit is set to kick off this weekend, just months after the explosive 10th-season reunion wrapped. But the cast list for the new season appears to be undergoing a significant shuffle, with several familiar faces reportedly out and new housemates potentially joining the fray.

Bravo announced the renewal for Season 11 back in May, and a network executive confirmed in early June that casting was underway, though final decisions were still pending. Since then, the rumor mill has been in overdrive. While the network has not officially confirmed the full cast, reports suggest at least three cast members have been announced as not returning.

The timing is tight. Production is reportedly starting this weekend, meaning any new dynamics will be tested immediately. This follows a particularly dramatic Season 10, which ended with a three-part reunion that left plenty of unresolved tension among the cast. A major recast could be Bravo's way of hitting the reset button or injecting fresh conflict into the long-running series.

For now, fans will have to wait for an official announcement from Bravo to see which rumors become reality. The network has not confirmed the cast at this time.
