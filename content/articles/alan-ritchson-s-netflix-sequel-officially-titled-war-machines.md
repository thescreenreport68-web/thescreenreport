The sequel to Alan Ritchson's Netflix film "War Machine" is officially titled "War Machines," according to a report from What's on Netflix. The title reveal came via a social media tease showing the script cover.

## What do we know about the sequel?
According to production listings cited in the report, the film is currently in development. The sequel is expected to be an Australia and United States co-production, with Lionsgate, Hidden Pictures, Range Media Partners, and Huge Film backing the project for Netflix.

Alan Ritchson previously took to social media to celebrate the sequel's confirmation, thanking fans for making the first movie a runaway success. He called its placement in Netflix's Top 10 films of all time "crazy" and said, "this was just the appetizer."

The original "War Machine," released on Netflix in March 2026, was a major viewership hit for the platform. According to What's on Netflix, it topped the platform's movie list for the first half of the year.
