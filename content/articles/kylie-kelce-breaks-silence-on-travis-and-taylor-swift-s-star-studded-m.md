What's the one word to describe the most talked-about wedding of the year? For Kylie Kelce, it's "incredible."

The wife of retired NFL star Jason Kelce offered her glowing review to People magazine on Thursday while accompanying her husband at the American Century Championship golf tournament in Stateline, Nevada. While Kylie, 34, kept other details close to the vest, her husband was a bit more forthcoming with fans at the event.

The wedding, reportedly held on July 3 at New York's Madison Square Garden, remains shrouded in some secrecy, with the couple yet to release any official photos. However, insider reports are painting a picture of a lavish affair. A source told Page Six that Kylie and Jason's four daughters—Wyatt, 6, Elliotte (also known as Ellie), 5, Bennett, 3, and Finnley (Finn), 1—served as flower girls.

Details from People suggest the vows were a highlight. According to an insider, the couple reportedly each exchanged 20-minute vows, with Travis getting more emotional than his bride. The source said, "Taylor talked about how Travis was the guy in high school who, even though he was the star athlete, would go sit with the less-popular kids who were being bullied at lunchtime. And Taylor talked about how she wished she knew someone like that when she was in high school. It was so special."

The celebration's guest list reportedly included A-listers like Brad Pitt, Selena Gomez, and Tom Brady.

Jason had previously expressed his excitement for his brother, reportedly telling reporters in June he was "just very excited, very excited for my brother" and "very happy for both of them," adding he was "looking forward to celebrating with them." Based on his and Kylie's post-wedding comments, it seems the celebration lived up to the hype.
