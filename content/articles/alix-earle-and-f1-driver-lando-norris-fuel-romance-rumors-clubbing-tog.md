What happens when a top influencer and a Formula 1 star cross paths in London? According to Page Six, Alix Earle and Lando Norris set the rumor mill on fire after being spotted leaving the same exclusive private club in the early hours of Thursday morning.

Photos and footage obtained by DeuxMoi show the pair departing The Twenty Two. Norris, 26, was seen sprinting out of the club in a black outfit, white sneakers, and a white ball cap, jumping into a waiting vehicle. Earle, 25, left more leisurely alongside her friend Anastasia Karanikolaou, wearing a golden-hued, ruched dress and strappy heels.

This is reportedly not the first time the two have been spotted in the same orbit. TMZ previously obtained snaps of them together in Cannes, France, after Earle was seen at an F1 race in Monaco in June. These sightings naturally lead to speculation about a potential connection, though it is important to note that nothing has been confirmed. Representatives for both Norris and Earle did not immediately respond to Page Six’s requests for comment.

For Earle, the rumors come after a busy year of high-profile sightings. Prior to her sightings with the driver, Earle — who split from NFL player Braxton Berrios in December after two years of dating — sparked romance speculation with NFL alum Tom Brady, 48. The swimsuit model was spotted chatting with the athlete at a yacht party in St. Barts on New Year’s Eve. An insider divulged to Page Six earlier this year that the “Dancing With the Stars” alum and father of three “broke off together” before they “headed to a private cabin together to talk.” A separate source said at the time that, “They were at a yacht party and met, and broke off from the larger group, talking for a lot of the night.” The pair was subsequently seen getting cozy at a private Super Bowl party in February.

For now, the London club sighting is just that—a sighting. Whether it signals a new romance or simply a night out with mutual friends remains to be seen. The lack of comment from either camp leaves the story firmly in the realm of speculation, fueled by paparazzi photos and a public eager to connect the dots.
