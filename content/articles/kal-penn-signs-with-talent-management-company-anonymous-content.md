Kal Penn has signed with Anonymous Content for representation, according to a report from TheWrap. The announcement was made in a statement from the actor on Tuesday.

Penn is best known for starring as Kumar Patel alongside John Cho in the “Harold & Kumar” franchise.

## What did Kal Penn say about the signing?

In his statement, Penn expressed enthusiasm for the new partnership. "I’m so excited to welcome Anonymous onto my team," he said. "As our business changes and evolves, I’m hungry to continue a career that’s art-and-storytelling forward. They are thoughtful, grounded, so creatively motivated and are well-respected for their solid eye for commerce."

He added that he looks forward to having them as partners "on my journey in features, streaming, comedy, and all the other spaces I’m working, developing, and pitching in."

## What recent work has Kal Penn done?

More recently, Penn appeared in a supporting role in Season 4 of HBO’s “Industry.” His recent credits also include the Disney+ series “The Santa Clauses,” the horror film “Smile,” and the “Silence of the Lambs” prequel series “Clarice.” He has also guest-hosted “The Daily Show.”

Looking ahead, Penn is set to star in the upcoming independent movie “Trust Me I’m a Doctor” alongside Abbie Cornish.

According to the report, Penn will continue to be represented by UTA and Fuller Law.

## Who else has Anonymous Content signed?

The signing continues a recent talent acquisition streak for Anonymous Content. In recent months, the company has also signed Bollywood star Hrithik Roshan and singer-songwriter King Princess.

Anonymous Content’s AC Studios recently produced the Oscar-nominated movie “Nickel Boys” and the Golden Globe nominee “True Detective: Night Country.”
