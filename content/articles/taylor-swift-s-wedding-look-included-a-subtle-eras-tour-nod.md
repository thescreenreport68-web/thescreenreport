[Taylor Swift](/celebrity/every-celeb-spotted-at-taylor-swift-travis-kelce-s-rehearsal-dinner-ah/) married Travis Kelce at Madison Square Garden in New York City on Friday, July 3, 2026. According to a statement from Swift's representative shared with Billboard, the pop superstar wore a Christian Dior Haute Couture gown for the ceremony.

## What was Taylor Swift's wedding look?

The bridal dress was designed by Jonathan Anderson, the creative director of Dior Women’s, Men’s and Haute Couture Collections, in collaboration with Swift and Kelce. Swift completed her look with Cartier jewelry. Both she and Kelce wore custom Christian Louboutin shoes.

## How did the look connect to the Eras Tour?

The choice of footwear provided a subtle nod to Swift's recent tour. On its official website, Christian Louboutin notes that Swift wore custom bejeweled styles from the designer throughout the European leg of The Eras Tour. The Maison created custom and existing silhouettes in different shapes, heel heights and colorways for the tour. Those designs included crystal-covered Eleonora Botta boots for the “Lover” segment and Cate boots for “Fearless.”

## Who officiated the wedding?

Actor and comedian Adam Sandler, whose movie *Happy Gilmore 2* featured a cameo from Kelce, officiated the ceremony. The couple did not have traditional bridesmaids or groomsmen. Swift’s brother, Austin, served as her man of honor, while Jason Kelce was Travis’s best man.

Official photos of Swift’s bridal look had not yet been released as of Friday evening, according to Billboard.
