Matty Healy and Gabbriette Bechtel are married, according to a report from Billboard. The 1975 frontman and the model-musician tied the knot on Saturday, July 18.

## Where Did the Wedding Take Place?
The couple's intimate outdoor ceremony was held at Castillo del Lago in Los Angeles, the former home of music icon Madonna, according to People. The private celebration took place at the Spanish Colonial Revival estate in the Hollywood Hills.

## Who Attended the Wedding?
The guest list included family and close friends, with Charli XCX and her husband—The 1975’s drummer George Daniel—reportedly among those in attendance, according to Page Six. Other guests named in the report include Anastasia Karanikolaou, Fai Khadra, and Devon Lee Carlson.

One day before the wedding, Healy and Bechtel were photographed rehearsing the ceremony in Los Angeles. The couple, dressed in matching black outfits, was seen practicing a romantic dip kiss ahead of the event.

Earlier in the week, Healy celebrated with a bachelor party featuring live entertainment from The 1975 tribute band 9075. He joined the group onstage to perform “The Sound” before crowd-surfing during “Sex.”

Healy and Bechtel began dating in September 2023. News of their engagement broke in June 2024 after Bechtel shared a photo of a black gemstone ring on her left hand, captioning it, “MARRYING THE 1975 IS VERY BRAT.”
