What's the best way to spend a summer Saturday in London? For Andrew Garfield and Monica Barbaro, the answer was a classic: Wimbledon.

The 42-year-old actor and the 36-year-old actress were spotted together in the Ralph Lauren suite at the tennis tournament on Saturday, July 4, 2026, in London, England, according to a report from Just Jared. The sighting confirms the couple continues to enjoy high-profile outings together, over a year into their relationship.

Garfield, dressed in Ralph Lauren, showed off a notably shaggy, grown-out hairstyle. Barbaro complemented the event's chic atmosphere in an all-white Ralph Lauren ensemble. The pair were among the celebrities hosted by Ralph Lauren, British Vogue, and GQ, with Vogue's Anna Wintour also listed as a host.

This Wimbledon appearance is a repeat performance for the duo. Just Jared notes they "also attended Wimbledon together last year," suggesting the tournament has become a favorite date spot. Their relationship timeline aligns with their professional partnership; they are starring together in the upcoming movie *Artificial*, a "comedic drama set in the world of artificial intelligence."

The film itself has been in the news. Reports indicate Amazon MGM Studios dropped the nearly finished project from its lineup, though Neon subsequently acquired it. Their joint public appearance naturally fuels speculation about the film's status and their off-screen chemistry, though no new details about the movie's release were confirmed at the event.

For now, the takeaway is clear: Garfield and Barbaro are still going strong, blending their personal and professional lives while enjoying the tennis.
