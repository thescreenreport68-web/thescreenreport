Netflix has released the official trailer for its upcoming Australian period drama, *My Brilliant Career*. According to Mashable, the footage evokes a *Bridgerton* vibe set in the Australian bush.

The series is a new adaptation of Miles Franklin's classic 1901 novel. It follows the story of aspiring writer Sybylla, who is more interested in building a career than securing a good marriage. The official synopsis frames the central conflict: "Modern, rebellious Sybylla dreams only of writing. Choosing an exciting career over a safe marriage is easy — until she falls in love unexpectedly."

## Who stars in the Netflix series?

Philippa Northeast leads the cast as the headstrong Sybylla. She is joined by Christopher Chung as Harry, Anna Chancellor as Señora Bossier, and Genevieve O'Reilly as Helen. The ensemble also includes Kate Mulvany, Jake Dunn, Alexander England, Sherry-Lee Watson, and Miah Madden.

## When does 'My Brilliant Career' come out?

It is written and produced by Liz Doran, known for her work on the series *Please Like Me*. The story explores themes of women's independence and a father-daughter relationship against the backdrop of a farm, offering a fresh take on a beloved Australian literary classic.
