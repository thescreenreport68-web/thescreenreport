Who was the bigger softie at the altar? According to guests, it wasn't the bride.

Two guests who spoke with NBC News reported that Travis Kelce was overcome with emotion during his wedding vows to Taylor Swift at their ceremony inside Madison Square Garden. One attendee told the outlet, "You would think the bride would be the one crying more, but it was actually Travis that was was more emotional." The emotional exchange reportedly left plenty of guests reaching for tissues.

The ceremony, which took place on Friday, featured deeply personal vows read from "little books" in front of roughly 1,000 friends and family. According to TMZ, the attendees said Taylor even sang part of her vows. The event transformed the massive arena into a surprisingly intimate wedding venue, officiated by Adam Sandler, who also performed an original song for the newlyweds.

The reception took on a whimsical theme, described as a "Wizard of Oz" meets "Alice in Wonderland" vibe, with food from Zero Bond and a performance by Stevie Nicks. Jason and Kylie Kelce's daughters served as flower girls, and Swift walked down the aisle to a string arrangement of one of her own songs.
