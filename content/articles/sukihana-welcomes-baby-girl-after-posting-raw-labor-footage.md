Rapper Sukihana has given birth to a baby girl, The Shade Room confirmed.

She posted a captionless photo of herself after delivery, following footage shared the previous evening, July 19, that showed her hunched over her bed in active labor. The raw clip captured her moaning in pain, setting the stage for her unique announcement.

According to The Shade Room, she previously revealed she was expecting her fourth child. She first revealed the pregnancy back in March, telling PEOPLE the baby arrived at a time she needed "a reminder of purpose and love." A gender reveal at the end of that month confirmed she was expecting a girl.

## Fan Reactions

Social media users flooded the comment section of the birth post with a mix of congratulations and humor. One fan, @4theloveofnj, wrote, "I love her 😂😂😂 she so raw". Another, @faithgaloreeee, added, "i ain’t never bust out laughing the way i just did😭😭😭😭". The candid nature of the announcement even prompted @birthinwithb to declare, "THIS IS CHILDBIRTH".

No further details about the baby's name or the father have been shared publicly.
