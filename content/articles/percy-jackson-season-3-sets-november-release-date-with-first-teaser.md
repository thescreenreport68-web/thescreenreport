Paramount+ will premiere the new movie *Clash of the Thundermans* on Thursday, September 3, according to an announcement made at San Diego Comic-Con. The streaming service revealed the premiere date and new key art for the upcoming film.

The news was reported by TV Series Finale. The film is based on the Nickelodeon series *The Thundermans*.

## What is the plot?

The film will see the heroic family facing an internal crisis. When youngest sibling Chloe develops a dangerous new superpower, the overseeing Hero League demands she be sent to a boarding school to learn control. This forces the family to choose sides, with some members willing to defy orders to keep her home.

## Who is in the cast?

Ariel Winter joins the ensemble in a key role. She is joined by returning series stars Kira Kosarin, Jack Griffo, Maya Le Clark, Diego Velazquez, Addison Riecke, Chris Tallman, and Rosa Blasi. Dana Snyder, Daniele Gaither, and Robyn Bradley also star.

## When and where can I watch it?

The film will debut on Paramount+ on Thursday, September 3. It will also have a linear broadcast premiere on Nickelodeon later that same day.

The film is produced by Paramount Television Studios, with Jed Spingarn, Sean W. Cunningham, and Marc Dworkin serving as writers and executive producers. Kira Kosarin and Jack Griffo are also executive producers, with Trevor Kirschner directing.
