‘[Percy Jackson and the Olympians](/tv/percy-jackson-and-the-olympians-season-3-casting-confirms-5-returning-stars/)’ will return for its third season in November, according to a report from IMDb. The first footage from the season, which adapts ‘The Titan’s Curse’ novel, was shown during a panel at San Diego Comic-Con.

Star Walker Scobell, who plays the titular demigod, stated the new season will stay true to the source material.

## What is the new season about?

The upcoming season will adapt the third book in Rick Riordan’s series, ‘The Titan’s Curse’. The logline for the series states Percy is on a dangerous quest, outrunning monsters and outwitting gods to return Zeus’s master bolt and stop a war. The show is a fantasy adventure series rated TV-PG, streaming exclusively on Disney Plus.

## When can fans expect more?

The November premiere window was the key detail revealed at the convention. With the first footage now shown to attendees, a wider public release of the teaser is likely to follow soon.
