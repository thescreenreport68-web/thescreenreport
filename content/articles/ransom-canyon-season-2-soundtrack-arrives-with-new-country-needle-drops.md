The soundtrack for the second season of Netflix's Western drama *Ransom Canyon* has been released, featuring a fresh slate of country music needle drops. According to Deadline, the series continues its tradition of blending a curated soundtrack with live musical performances.

For the new season, music supervisor Madonna Wade Reed organized singer/songwriter bootcamps in Nashville and Los Angeles to source new and original music. Three songs featured in Season 2 originated from those sessions.

## What songs are in Ransom Canyon Season 2?

New music from artists like Charlie Marie, Elijah Honey, and Andrew Simple appears in the premiere, "Hey There, Cowboy." Later episodes include songs from The Black Keys, Teddy Swims, and Sam Williams. The season also includes a live performance from Thomas Rhett featuring Jordan Davis, continuing the show's pattern of incorporating on-screen musical acts.

## How does the music connect to the show's first season?

The first season of *Ransom Canyon* established the show's musical identity, including a live performance from Charlie Crockett. The newly released list confirms the series is doubling down on its country and folk-infused soundscape as it returns for new episodes.

The full song lists for both seasons are now available for fans to explore.
