Jennifer Fessler claims Ciara Miller has not apologized for publicly accusing her of having a sexual relationship with West Wilson. Fessler, 55, made the statement to Us Weekly at the Nancy Davis Race to Erase MS Gala in Los Angeles on June 5.

## The Accusation and Denials
The conflict began on May 8 when Ciara Miller accused Fessler of sleeping with West Wilson after Fessler defended him amid his controversial relationship with Amanda Batula. Fessler immediately denied the claim on iHeartRadio’s “Two T’s in a Pod,” asserting, "I have never slept with West Wilson. He was not my hall pass.". West Wilson also denied the allegation via his Instagram Stories, remarking, "news to me," while tagging Jennifer. A source close to West dismissed the claims, indicating they were "absolutely not true" and described them as "silly allegations.".

## The Apology Divide
While West Wilson took responsibility, Fessler revealed he personally reached out. "West did reach out and apologize profusely and called and apologized to my husband and kids," she explained. However, the same courtesy has not come from the accuser. "But no, I haven’t gotten an apology [from Ciara]. But who cares at this point? It’s so stupid and not true." Fessler said. In a previous Instagram Stories post addressing the accusations, Fessler reportedly called them defamatory, stating they were "categorically untrue and defamatory on social media.".
