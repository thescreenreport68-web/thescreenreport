Jelly Roll's daughter, Bailee Ann DeFord, went on an expletive-filled TikTok rant Sunday, insisting fans have no idea what caused her father's divorce from Bunnie Xo, a Page Six report details. The 18-year-old's heated live video served as a fierce defense of her father and a warning shot to online commentators.

In the broadcast, DeFord declared, "Don’t y’all ever come for my father’s name like that again. I’m so done." and told critics to "Get the f–k out of my DMs, out of my comments, my life with that s–t. I don’t care," adding, "I don’t want to talk about it." She insisted that infidelity was not the cause, but teased that the real reason for the split would be a shock. "Y’all have no f–king idea what happened, and wait ’till y’all find out. Just f–king wait," she said.

DeFord warned that the public's entire understanding of the situation is fragile. "Because I promise y’all, the view of what y’all have of not only the situation, but their relationship and the internet in totality is about to f–king crumble the day that I open my mouth." She emphasized the private nature of the ordeal, stating, "It’s not a difference in f–king opinions. Nobody else lived in it, so don’t f–king even. I’m for real so done."

This isn't the first time DeFord has pushed back against public scrutiny of the divorce.
