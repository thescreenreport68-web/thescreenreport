A Catastrophic Launch for the Girl of Steel

Milly Alcock's debut as the Girl of Steel has crash-landed at the box office.

## The Movie: An Interstellar Journey of Vengeance

Directed by Craig Gillespie, *[Supergirl](/movies/supergirl-box-office-day-24-adds-460-000-as-domestic-total-hits-70-million/)* is a 108-minute action-adventure sci-fi film. The premise follows Kara Zor-El, who is forced to become Supergirl when a ruthless adversary strikes close to home. Reluctantly, she teams up with an unlikely companion for an epic, interstellar quest driven by vengeance and justice.

The cast is led by Milly Alcock in the titular role. She is joined by Eve Ridley as Ruthye, Matthias Schoenaerts as Krem, and Jason Momoa as the infamous Lobo. David Krumholtz and Emily Beecham play her Kryptonian parents, Zor-El and Alura In-Ze, while David Corenswet makes an appearance as Superman and Kadiff Kirwan plays Bomar Vran.

## The Box Office Numbers Tell a Devastating Story

The financial reality for *Supergirl* is bleak.

## At the Box Office

Supergirl has grossed $70,121,047 at the domestic box office, 25 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 1,572 theaters. The film added $213,141 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $120 million. It carries a reported production budget of $175 million.
