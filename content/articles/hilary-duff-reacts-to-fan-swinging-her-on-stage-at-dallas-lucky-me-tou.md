What happens when a fan gets a little too into the choreography? For Hilary Duff, it meant getting a surprise aerial hug during her latest tour stop—and she's totally here for it.

The moment unfolded on June 30 in Irving, Texas, during Duff's 'The Lucky Me Tour.' As part of her performance of the hit song "With Love," the 38-year-old entertainer routinely brings fans on stage to join her for the iconic dance routine. This time, a fan named Karson, dressed as Duff's character Wendy from *Casper Meets Wendy*, took the invitation to a new level. In a spontaneous move, Karson picked Duff up and twirled her around in a hug mid-performance.

Karson later shared a video on TikTok recounting the experience and apologizing for potentially stressing out Duff's security team. But any worries were quickly put to rest. Duff herself found the video and jumped into the comments with a reassuring message. "I loved my big swing around hug! Don’t think twice!" she wrote, according to Just Jared.

This isn't the first heartwarming fan interaction Duff has facilitated on this tour. Back in February, during the opening night of her Las Vegas residency, she helped a fan propose to his boyfriend right after performing the same "With Love" choreography. It seems these on-stage moments are becoming a signature part of the 'Lucky Me' experience.

For those catching the upcoming Los Angeles shows, there's an extra incentive to bring their A-game: the concerts are being filmed.
