"I am excited to share the trailer for my new film BY ANY MEANS, based on a true story," Mark Wahlberg stated on Facebook earlier this week, per OK! Magazine. The trailer, released July 19, 2026, reveals the actor's dramatic transformation for his role as Gregory Scarpa, a notorious hitman known as the Grim Reaper and the Mad Hatter.

Wahlberg, now 55, is nearly unrecognizable thanks to facial prosthetics, including a prominent nose piece, and a bulky dark wig with retro sideburns. The look is a stark departure for the star, though his physique and a signature white tank top with a gold chain offer clues to his identity.

It follows a Black FBI agent, played by Yahya Abdul-Mateen II, as he investigates the murders of civil rights leaders, a story inspired by real events. Wahlberg's announcement on Facebook expressed excitement about the project.

Fan reactions to the prosthetic-heavy look have been mixed. While some expressed immediate interest in seeing the film, others zeroed in on the altered nose, with one fan directly questioning, "Why the nose? I can’t unsee it." Wahlberg's full commitment to the role of Scarpa is now on full display as the marketing campaign for the true-story thriller begins.
