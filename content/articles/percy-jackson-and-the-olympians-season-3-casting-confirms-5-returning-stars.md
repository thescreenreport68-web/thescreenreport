Season three of the Disney+ series *Percy Jackson and the Olympians* is officially in the works, with five stars confirmed to return and 11 new actors joining the cast, according to a report from Just Jared. The new season is set to debut on the streamer in late 2026.

The series is an adaptation of the popular YA book series by Rick Riordan. The upcoming season will adapt the third book, *The Titan's Curse*.

## What is Season 3 about?

The plot follows Percy Jackson as he and his demigod allies rush to answer a distress call from his friend Grover. Their mission leads them to discover two powerful new half-bloods and puts them in the path of the titan lord Kronos's latest treacherous plot. The young heroes must also find the missing goddess Artemis and solve the mystery of a powerful ancient monster within a week.

## What is the production status?

Filming for the new episodes wrapped earlier this year, the outlet reports. While specific details on the returning and new cast members were not provided in the initial report, the confirmation of the season's development and its 2026 release window marks a major update for fans.

## What happens next?

With production complete, the series moves into post-production ahead of its planned late 2026 premiere on Disney+.
