While many might be settling into domestic bliss, Dua Lipa is hitting the ground running. The pop superstar was spotted arriving at the iconic Electric Lady Studios in New York City on Wednesday afternoon (July 15), marking her second consecutive day at the famed recording space.

The 30-year-old "Levitating" singer was photographed wearing a long-sleeved, white lace mini dress, black boots, and sunglasses for her latest studio session. This followed a sighting the day before, when she was seen leaving the same studio in a black dress, boots, and a navy bandana, carrying a bright yellow bag.

## A Post-Honeymoon Hustle

The studio visits come just weeks after Dua Lipa and her husband, actor Callum Turner, wrapped their Italian honeymoon. According to Just Jared, the couple "jetted right to New York City" afterward, and the Grammy-winning singer has been "spending a lot of time at the iconic recording studio." The back-to-back days of work have set tongues wagging among fans, who are eagerly anticipating new music.

While Lipa is deep in her creative process, Turner has also been busy. He's been doing press for his upcoming film *One Night Only*, where he stars alongside Monica Barbaro, according to Just Jared.

The singer's focused studio time at a legendary venue like Electric Lady—known for historic sessions by artists from Jimi Hendrix to Stevie Wonder—strongly suggests a major new project is in its early stages. For now, fans will have to wait for an official announcement, but the visual cues of a dedicated artist at work are a promising sign that new music from Dua Lipa is on the horizon.
