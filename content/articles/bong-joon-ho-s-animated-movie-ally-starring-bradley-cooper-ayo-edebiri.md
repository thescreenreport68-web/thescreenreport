Bong Joon Ho's first animated feature, "Ally," has sold to multiple international distributors, according to Variety. The film, which features a voice cast including Bradley Cooper and [Ayo Edebiri](/celebrity/the-bear-s-ayo-edebiri-reveals-her-full-name/), is currently in production.

Pathé secured the multiple deals for the film following its launch at the Marche du Film. Neon had already acquired U.S. distribution rights prior to Cannes, reuniting the director with the distributor behind his Oscar-winning film "Parasite."

## What is 'Ally' about?

The film is set in the South Pacific Ocean and centers on a piglet squid named Ally who dreams of seeing the sun and starring in a wildlife documentary. Her peaceful world is disrupted when a mysterious aircraft sinks into her aquatic home, thrusting her and her unlikely companions on a journey to the surface.

## Who is in the voice cast?

The English-language voice cast is led by Alex Jayne Go as the titular Ally. She is joined by Ayo Edebiri, Bradley Cooper, Dave Bautista, Finn Wolfhard, Rachel House, and Werner Herzog.

## When will it be released?

"Ally" is scheduled for a worldwide theatrical release in 2027. Pathé will handle distribution in France, Benelux, Switzerland and West Africa, while CJ ENM and Penture Invest will distribute in South Korea, Vietnam, Turkey and Indonesia. The film is financed by CJ ENM, Penture Invest and Pathé, with production overseen by Barunson C&C.
