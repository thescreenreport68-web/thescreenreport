Olivia Wilde assembles a formidable cast for her latest directorial venture, *The Invite*, a tense comedy-drama that explores the unraveling of a marriage over one fraught evening. The film, which runs 107 minutes, follows Joe and Angela, a couple whose relationship is on thin ice. In an attempt to break the monotony—or perhaps mend fences—they invite their mysterious upstairs neighbors over for a dinner party, a decision that sends the night spiraling into unexpected and dramatic territory.

The ensemble is led by Seth Rogen as the beleaguered Joe and Olivia Wilde herself as his wife, Angela. They are joined by Penélope Cruz as the enigmatic neighbor Pina and Edward Norton as Hawk. The supporting cast includes Skip Howland as a Doorman, Mel Powell as a Bartender, and Rachel Thurow and Mario Valdez as Subway Goers, rounding out the world of this intimate, character-driven story.

A film built on sharp performances and claustrophobic tension, *The Invite* is a showcase for its actors under Wilde's direction.
