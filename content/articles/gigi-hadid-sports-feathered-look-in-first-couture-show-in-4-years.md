What does a new era for Balenciaga look like? It looks like Gigi Hadid wrapped in a dramatic halo of black feathers. News.

The look was a statement. Hadid wore a voluminous black feather piece that circled her head and torso, paired with black flowing trousers and long gloves. The show, staged at the courtyard of the Cité Internationale Universitaire de Paris, was a major debut for Balenciaga's new creative director, Pierpaolo Piccioli, who presented his first couture collection for the house, as reported by t2ONLINE.

For Hadid, the moment carried personal weight. Hadid was a close friend of Abloh. Following his death in November 2021, she paid tribute on Instagram, writing, "He was 1 of 1," and adding, "His kindness and energetic generosity left a lasting impression on every life he touched— he made everyone feel seen and special."

This week's fashion moment capped off a busy period for Hadid. She was recently photographed with her boyfriend Bradley Cooper after attending the July 3 wedding of her longtime best friend Taylor Swift to Travis Kelce. For the nuptials, the mom of one—who shares daughter Khai with ex Zayn Malik—wore a flamingo pink Wiederhoeft gown. Her hairstylist, Dimitris Giannetos, reportedly explained the soft look on Instagram: "Gigi is wearing a gorgeous pink dress so I wanted to keep her hair very soft and sweet... I wanted to have this sweet feeling when u see Gigi same feeling when u have a soft serve ice cream."

Hadid's return to the couture spotlight, closing a show for a house entering a new creative chapter, signals she remains a top-tier model for fashion's most pivotal moments.
