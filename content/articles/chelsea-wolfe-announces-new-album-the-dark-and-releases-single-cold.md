Chelsea Wolfe has announced details for her ninth studio album, *The Dark*, and released its third single, “Cold.”

The new album is set for release on August 21, 2026, via Loma Vista Recordings, according to reports from music outlets ThePRP and Chaoszine. The announcement comes after Wolfe previously shared two other tracks from the project: the album's title track and the song “Death Is Not The End.”

## What is the new single?

The lead single “Cold” arrived alongside an official music video directed by Magdalena Wosinska. The track is the third preview of the forthcoming album, which the artist has described as a work of personal accountability. In a statement shared via ThePRP, Wolfe elaborated on the album's themes: “The ghosts are haunted by the living just as much as the living are haunted by the ghosts. On this album, I’m not pointing the finger at anyone. I’m taking responsibility.”

## Who is featured on the album?

*The Dark* will include guest performances from several notable musicians. The album features contributions from Robin Finck of Nine Inch Nails and drummer Matt Chamberlain, known for his work with A Perfect Circle and Ghost. Members of the Icelandic darkwave group Kælan Mikla also appear on the record.

## What is the tracklist for 'The Dark'?

The album contains ten tracks. The full tracklist, as reported by ThePRP, is as follows:

1. “Cold” 2. “Seethe” 3. “Humours” 4. “Swallower” 5. “Halo” 6. “The Dark” 7. “Turn The Key” 8. “I’m Not There” 9. “Death is Not The End” 10. “Dancer In The Sky”

Pre-orders for the album are currently available through the artist's official website.

## When is the tour?

Wolfe has scheduled an extensive international tour in support of the album, spanning North America and Europe. The tour kicks off with a festival appearance at ArcTangent in Bristol, UK, on August 20, 2026. The North American leg begins in September 2026, with Jonathan Hultén as the supporting act. The European leg in November and December 2026 will feature A.A. Williams as the opener.
