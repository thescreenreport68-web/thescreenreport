Jelly Roll is "doing really well" after finalizing his divorce settlement from wife Bunnie XO, a source close to the singer told People. "He’s focused on his tour and his health," the source said, adding that the musician is "in a good place.".

News of the settlement broke on Friday, per TMZ, following Jelly Roll's initial filing on May 18 in Tennessee. The couple, married for ten years, aimed for an amicable process. "They both wanted to handle the divorce with respect for one another." the People source claimed.

## The Settlement Details
According to TMZ, the agreement splits assets including vehicles, intellectual properties, an aircraft, and their homes. Bunnie XO (born Alisa DeFord), 46, will also receive a one-time lump sum payment from Jelly Roll (born Jason DeFord), 41. The settlement reportedly includes a clause barring both parties from publicly disparaging each other.

While a rep for Jelly Roll did not immediately respond to Page Six's request for comment Saturday afternoon, the singer's positive outlook is clear. He is reportedly on tour, channeling his energy into his music and well-being.
