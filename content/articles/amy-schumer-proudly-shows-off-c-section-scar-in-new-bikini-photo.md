What's a warrior's mark? For Amy Schumer, it's the scar from her C-section, and she's not hiding it.

The comedian posted a photo to her Instagram Stories on July 10, according to E! News, proudly showing off the scar while wearing a cream bikini. Over the picture, she wrote, "C-section peaking out saying, ‘Yeah, I’m a warrior.’"

The post is a candid celebration of her postpartum body, arriving as she focuses on motherhood following her split from ex-husband Chris Fischer. Schumer shares her son, Gene, with Fischer. The Instagram post also included a sweet snap of the mother-son duo watching fireworks on the beach.

Schumer's journey to motherhood was difficult. She has previously described Gene's 2019 birth as "really scary." On a 2019 podcast episode, she recalled, "I was throwing up through the first hour of my C-section," adding, "It’s supposed to take about an hour and a half. Mine took over three hours because of my endometriosis."

Despite the ordeal, she praised Fischer's support at the time: "Chris was so great. We just stared [into] each other’s eyes and he just held me there." She also noted, "Then they let me hold Gene for a good amount of time. I got to see him and hold him."

In a statement shared on December 12, Schumer said, "Chris and I have made the difficult decision to end our marriage," but emphasized, "We love each other very much and will continue to focus on raising our son." She added, "Amicable and all love and respect! Family forever."

Schumer has been open about the complex emotions of motherhood.

Her latest post continues that theme of raw honesty, transforming a physical reminder of a challenging birth into a badge of honor.
