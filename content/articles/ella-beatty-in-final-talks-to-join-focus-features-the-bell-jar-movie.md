Ella Beatty is in final talks to join the cast of Focus Features' adaptation of Sylvia Plath's *The Bell Jar*, according to Deadline.

She would star alongside Billie Eilish and Carey Mulligan in the film, which is set to be directed by Oscar winner Sarah Polley.

## Who is Ella Beatty playing?

The role Beatty would play in the film is not yet clear. According to the report, Eilish is set to portray the protagonist, Esther Greenwood, in her film acting debut, with Mulligan playing her mother.

## What is 'The Bell Jar' about?

The film is an adaptation of Plath's semi-autobiographical novel, which follows a young woman's summer in New York, her return home, and her subsequent mental breakdown against the backdrop of 1950s America. It is the only novel penned by the American writer and poet, originally published in 1963.

Beatty comes to the project after landing the lead role in the new season of Netflix's *Monster*, where she plays alleged axe murderer Lizzie Borden. She is a Juilliard-trained actor whose recent credits include the A24 drama *If I Had Legs I'd Kick You* and the Off-[Broadway](/movies/meg-stalter-shuts-down-broadway-rumors-with-a-smile-and-fans-waste-no-time/) play *Sexual Misconduct of the Middle Classes*.

Polley is directing from her own screenplay, with Plan B, Studiocanal, and Joy Coalition among the producers. Representatives for Focus Features declined to comment on the casting news.
