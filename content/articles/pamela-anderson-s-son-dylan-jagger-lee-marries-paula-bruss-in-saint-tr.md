What does a dream wedding look like for the son of a rock star and a model? For Dylan Jagger Lee, it involved a wildflower garden, a private villa, and the woman he loves.

The 28-year-old model and musician, son of Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee, married interior designer Paula Bruss in Saint-Tropez, according to a report from Just Jared. The ceremony took place at a private villa in Les Parcs, with an intimate gathering of friends and family in attendance, including Dylan's parents and his older brother, Brandon Thomas Lee.

Dylan described the day to Vogue, calling it "a dream come true." He said, "The service was peaceful and sentimental," and shared, "Seeing my beautiful wife walking down the aisle and reciting my vows in the middle of the most beautiful wildflower garden was a dream come true." The newlywed said he and Paula felt "pure happiness" on their special day, adding, "Looking around and seeing everyone we love gathered together in one place was overwhelming in the best possible way. There was so much excitement and love in the air."

For his mother, Pamela Anderson, it was an "emotional moment." She told Vogue, "I’m so happy for Dylan and so excited to send him off into the wild world. Tears are catching me off guard at times — happy tears. But as Khalil Gibran says, ‘As parents, we are the bow from which life springs.'"

The location held special significance for the couple, as it's the same place where Dylan proposed to Paula in July 2024. The wedding marks a new chapter for Dylan, who has followed in his father's musical footsteps while also building a modeling career. His brother Brandon is also a model and actor, making the Lee family a fixture in both Hollywood and the fashion world.

The intimate, flower-filled celebration seems a world away from the high-octane, headline-grabbing lives of his parents in their heyday, suggesting Dylan and Paula are carving out their own path. With the ceremony now confirmed, fans will be watching for the first official photos from the couple's big day.
