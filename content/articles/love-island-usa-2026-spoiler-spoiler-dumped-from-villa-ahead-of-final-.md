Who's out of the villa just before the big finale? The latest episode of *Love Island USA* saw another couple's journey come to an abrupt end, according to a report from Just Jared.

That left the fate of the remaining two couples in the hands of their fellow islanders. The safe couples—Aniya and Carl, Trinity and Bryce, Melanie and Sincere, and Tierra and KC—had to vote on which vulnerable pair to save.

The timing of this elimination is particularly brutal, cutting a couple's dream short right at the finish line. It sets the stage for a tense final week as the remaining islanders navigate their final dates and declarations before America picks its favorite pair.
