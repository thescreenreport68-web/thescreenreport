Jack Quaid discussed the climactic fifth episode of the third season of "My Adventures with Superman," according to TheWrap.

The star, who voices Clark Kent and Superman, highlighted the episode's adaptation of a classic comic storyline and his dual role this season.

## What happens in Episode 5?

The episode puts a new spin on the iconic "Death of Superman" comic book arc. Quaid noted the episode features a last stand involving Clark, Kara, and a future version of Clark's son, Jonathan Kent. He praised the show's ability to balance such heavy themes with lighter moments, like Jimmy Olsen's speed dating adventures.

## Who else does Jack Quaid play?

In Season 3, Quaid also provides the voice for Bizarro, a distorted version of Superman. "Superman I never thought, but Bizarro is another one where I’m like, oh my God," he said in the interview. He expressed excitement about expanding the "House of El" with these characters and new voice actors like Auliʻi Cravalho as Jessica Cruz.

"Now it’s been so long since I’ve recorded it, I get to just kind of enjoy it as a fan," Quaid told TheWrap. "Which is great."

## Is there a spin-off coming?

The interview confirmed the upcoming spin-off series "My Adventures with Green Lantern," which will star Auliʻi Cravalho as Jessica Cruz. Quaid credited the show's creative team for building out this animated universe.

"My Adventures with Superman" is an animated series that first aired in 2023. As of July 2026, it is available to stream on HBO Max and YouTube TV in the US.
