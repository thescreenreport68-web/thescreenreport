While Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's inner circle gathered in New York City for an apparent rehearsal dinner, Blake Lively was more than 200 miles away. According to E! News, the *Gossip Girl* alum and her husband Ryan Reynolds stepped out at a horse show in Lake Placid, N.Y., on July 2.

Photos published by TMZ show Lively in a blue tank top and denim shorts, with Reynolds in a white T-shirt and straw hat. The outlet reported the couple was there for their 6-year-old daughter, Betty, who placed second in a riding competition.

Her absence from the wedding pre-party doesn't necessarily signal bad blood. In fact, newly unsealed court documents from Lively's legal battle with *It Ends With Us* co-star Justin Baldoni reveal a candid text exchange between the two friends from December 2024.

Lively reached out to Swift, who was days from wrapping her Eras Tour, with a vulnerable message. "I have no reason to ask, but I donno, I've been feeling like I should... is everything ok?" she texted, per the docs. "I felt like a bad friend lately because I was such a sad sack who only talked about my own s--t for months. You were generous to not only be the key person there for me during all of it, but also to let me off the hook for being so in it."

She added, "I felt so stupid and like it sounded needy and awkward for everyone, especially bc [sic] I can't explain why I have this feeling. I do want to know everything is good, so I'm asking. I always want the opportunity to be a better friend if there's something I unintentionally did."

The texts show a friendship navigating a rough patch with honesty and humor. Lively and Reynolds, who also share kids James, 11, Inez, 9, and Olin, 3, were simply supporting their daughter at her equestrian event. A family commitment, not a feud, kept her from the city that day.
