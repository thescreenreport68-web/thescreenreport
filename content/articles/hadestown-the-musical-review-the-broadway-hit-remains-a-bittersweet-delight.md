The Broadway musical 'Hadestown' is now more accessible via a new filmed recording, released in theaters as a one-week event, according to IndieWire. The recording compiles footage from three live performances of the West End production, featuring the original Broadway cast.

## What is the 'Hadestown' recording?

The filmed recording captures the West End production of the Tony-winning musical. It is a staged recording that translates the show to the screen.

## What is 'Hadestown' about?

'Hadestown' is a retelling of the Greek myth of Orpheus and Eurydice, set in a landscape reminiscent of the Great Depression. It is a sung-through musical about love, sacrifice, and storytelling.
