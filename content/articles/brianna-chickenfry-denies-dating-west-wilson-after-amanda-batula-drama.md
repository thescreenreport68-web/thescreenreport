Who is your source? That's the question Brianna Chickenfry is asking after finding herself at the center of a surprising new dating rumor.

The speculation started when a TikToker claimed, "West Wilson is now an item with Brianna Chickenfry." The Barstool personality and PlanBri podcast host responded with disbelief in a July 6 TikTok video.

"Me? West Wilson?" LaPaglia, also known as Brianna Chickenfry, said in the clip. "Have you guys even seen the podcast episodes I made about that man?"

She was referencing her previous commentary on the relationship drama between *Summer House* alums West Wilson and Amanda Batula, which came to light in March. LaPaglia had shared her skepticism about the couple's timeline on her podcast, claiming Wilson told her they "were a thing" around the time of the Super Bowl in February.

Now, the 27-year-old influencer is shutting down any notion that she's the one linked to Wilson. In her TikTok response, she made her position clear.

"Not going down that path again. Um yeah, actually no, what the f--k? Who is your source? I haven’t seen that man in months."

LaPaglia's denial is a firm and confirmed statement. The rumor appears to have stemmed solely from her previous involvement in discussing Wilson's love life, not from any recent sightings or interactions. Wilson and Batula, who each confirmed they would not be returning to *Summer House* for season 11, went public with their relationship on March 31.

While LaPaglia has closed the book on this particular rumor, the original *Summer House* love triangle that sparked her commentary continues to generate interest. Wilson's brief romance with Batula's best friend Ciara Miller ended before his relationship with Batula began, fracturing the friend group. For now, the only confirmed item involving Brianna Chickenfry and West Wilson is her podcast critique.
