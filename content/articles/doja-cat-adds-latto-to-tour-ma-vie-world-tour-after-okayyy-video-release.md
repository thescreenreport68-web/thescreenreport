The news arrived on July 21, the same day the rappers dropped a steamy, 2000s-coded music video for their collaboration, "Okayyy."

Doja Cat revealed the tour partnership in a clip posted from what appears to be the set of the "Okayyy" video, using a vintage camcorder. The announcement, confirmed by outlets including Billboard and The Music Universe, means the Grammy-nominated Atlanta artist will be the special guest for the entire 31-city run. This marks Latto's first major arena tour since she gave birth in May.

## What are the tour dates and cities?

The North American leg, promoted by Live Nation, begins at Little Caesars Arena in Detroit, Michigan. The trek will hit major venues across the continent, including stops in Chicago, Los Angeles, Miami, Atlanta, and Toronto. This run follows Doja Cat's successful global tour performances across Asia, Latin America, Europe, and Australia earlier in the year.

## Where did the 'Okayyy' video premiere?

The official video for "Okayyy" premiered on YouTube on July 21. The visual opens with Latto rapping in lingerie from silk bedsheets, channeling a classic MySpace-era aesthetic with a digital camera. Doja Cat enters the frame in pink lingerie for a scene where she gives a lap dance to a chair while visiting a man in prison. The pair reunite for shots against a baby-blue backdrop and later appear with a crew of muscular men washing luxury cars. Fan reaction in the YouTube comments was immediate, with one user writing, "WHOEVER PUT THEM TOGETHER DESERVES A RAISE."

The song is featured on Latto's fourth studio album, *Big Mama*, which arrived in May. The album debuted at No. 16 on the Billboard 200 chart, marking her biggest streaming debut to date. The tour collaboration and video release solidify the pair's status as one of rap's most prominent duos of the year. Tickets for the Tour Ma Vie World Tour, including VIP options, are available now.
