Steven Moffat will be the headline international guest at the Serielizados - Barcelona Series Festival in October, according to a report from Variety. The 'Sherlock' creator and '[Doctor Who](/tv/billie-piper-officially-returns-to-doctor-who-this-summer/)' showrunner will attend the event alongside producer Sue Vertue, CEO of Hartswood Films.

## What is the Serielizados Festival?

The Serielizados - Barcelona Series Festival is an annual television festival. The festival is known for its curated programming.

Organizers said the festival's slogan, "An Eye for It," "emphasizes the role of its programming team in filtering and curating the ever-growing number of television series, helping audiences discover the most exciting and distinctive titles."

## What will be featured at the festival?

The festival will open with the series "In Vitro," a production backed by Catalan public broadcaster 3Cat and HBO Max. It will close with "Episòdic: El principi d’Arquimedes," a six-part anthology series created by audiovisual students. The national competition will feature Filmin Original "Segunda Temporada."

The festival's industry arm, Serielizados Pro, will feature discussions with executives from international broadcasters and producers.
