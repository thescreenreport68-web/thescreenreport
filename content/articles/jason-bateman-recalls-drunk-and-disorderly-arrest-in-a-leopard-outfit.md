A leopard-print-clad Jason Bateman, fresh from the casinos and carrying a pocket full of chips, found himself in handcuffs in Monaco.

He summed up the night simply: "I just got rocked."

## The Path to Sobriety

Bateman credited his wife, Amanda Anka, with helping him turn a corner. He described "a few negotiations" about his drinking because of how "unpredictable" he could become. He also shared with *The Hollywood Reporter* in February that he was conscious of wanting to get a lot of boxes checked before becoming a father.

Reflecting on his wilder days, Bateman noted he was lucky they happened before the era of social media and camera phones. "Fortunately, I was living at a time without social media and camera phones," he said, "so I got away with a lot, but it was definitely close a few times." His candid podcast story offers a playful glimpse into the past he left behind.
