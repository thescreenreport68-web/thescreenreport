Did Travis Kelce let the wedding location slip months before he and Taylor Swift said "I do"?

The NFL star first brought up the famed arena on the May 20 episode of his *New Heights* podcast with brother Jason Kelce. "I’ve always wanted to go to MSG when the f--king Knicks are good and the stadium's rocking," Travis said, adding, "I just don't know if I'm going to be able to get to a game."

He doubled down on his curiosity the very next week. On the May 27 episode, while speaking to longtime Knicks fan Tina Fey, he asked, "How fun is that arena?" and "How electric is it?" He elaborated, "I've never I've never been in. Jason's been to a few college games and stuff like that, but I've always wondered what MSG felt like when it was rocking."

Well, he certainly found out.

Fans are now looking back at those podcast comments as potential, playful hints. According to a report from E! News, the arena also boasts hidden tunnels, private loading areas and a covered roof perfect for keeping prying eyes away.

While the wedding itself is confirmed, the interpretation of Travis's earlier comments as deliberate "Easter eggs" remains a fun piece of fan speculation.
