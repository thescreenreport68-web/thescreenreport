"These two models are both beautiful and so timeless, and I hope they help inspire other singer-songwriters like Martins have inspired me for years," Shawn Mendes wrote on his Instagram earlier this month. The 27-year-old "In My Blood" singer-songwriter seems to be finding inspiration in Brazil, too, where he was spotted with girlfriend Bruna Marquezine over the weekend.

According to Just Jared, the couple enjoyed a visit to a local farmers market on Sunday, July 19, in Rio de Janeiro. The 30-year-old *Blue Beetle* actress and Mendes browsed stalls together in Barra da Tijuca, a neighborhood known for its beaches, and appeared to be in good spirits during the outing.

## Steady Romance

The market stroll continues a string of public appearances for the pair in Rio. Just days before, Mendes was seen soaking up the sun shirtless at São Conrado Beach, and earlier in the week, the couple was photographed sharing some sweet PDA while out together.

Mendes and Marquezine have been dating for several months, first sparking romance rumors back in December 2025. While they enjoy their time in Brazil, both have individual projects keeping them busy. Marquezine recently starred in the film *Velhos Bandidos*, released in March, and has the TV series *Véspera* in the works. Mendes, for his part, just launched two new signature guitars inspired by his favorite 1920s vintage Martin.
