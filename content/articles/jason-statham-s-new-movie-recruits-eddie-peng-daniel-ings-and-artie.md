Eddie Peng, Daniel Ings, and Artie Wilkison-Hunt have joined the cast of the upcoming action-comedy film *[Jason Statham Stole My Bike](/movies/jason-statham-s-meta-comedy-jason-statham-stole-my-bike-adds-eddie-peng/)*, according to MovieWeb. The movie, which stars Jason Statham as himself, is directed by David Leitch and is scheduled for release on August 6, 2027.

The new cast members join a project that already includes Hannah Waddingham. Eddie Peng is known for action films like *Operation Mekong* and *The Rescue*, while Daniel Ings appeared in Guy Ritchie's *The Gentlemen*. Artie Wilkison-Hunt recently played a young Adam in Amazon's *Masters of the Universe* adaptation.

## What is the film about?

Plot details are being kept under wraps, but the movie will reportedly feature "a number of major action set pieces." The meta-comedy sees Statham poking fun at his own gruff action persona. The title suggests a storyline involving the theft of a bicycle.

## When and where is it filming?

Production is now underway, with filming due to take place in the United Kingdom and Malta. The film is written by Alison Flierl, known for her work on *Conan* and *BoJack Horseman*.

Recent reports have claimed the film could head straight-to-streaming, but as of July 2026, it is scheduled for a theatrical release on August 6, 2027.
