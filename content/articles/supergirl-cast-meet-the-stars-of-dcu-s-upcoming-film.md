Milly Alcock stars as [Supergirl](/movies/box-office-supergirl-stumbles-with-38-million-debut-toy-story-5-remain/) in the upcoming DC Universe film set for release in 2026. The movie, directed by Craig Gillespie, assembles a major ensemble cast for an interstellar story of vengeance.

According to ScreenRant, the film is one of the DCU’s most hotly anticipated features. It adapts Tom King's acclaimed 2021–22 comic run, *Supergirl: Woman of Tomorrow*, promising a different take on the character. James Gunn and Peter Safran confirmed this approach to Variety in 2023.

## Who is playing Supergirl?

Milly Alcock was announced as Supergirl in January 2024. She first appeared in the role during the final scene of 2025's *[Superman](/movies/superman-2025-reactions-what-fans-are-saying/)*, stumbling into the Fortress of Solitude.

## Who else is in the cast?

The supporting cast features several key figures from the comics. Eve Ridley plays Ruthye Marye Knoll, the young alien who enlists Supergirl's help after her father's murder. Matthias Schoenaerts portrays the villain, Krem of the Yellow Hills.

David Krumholtz plays Kara’s father, Zor-El. David Corenswet reprises his role as Superman.

## What is the film's connection to the larger DCU?

The movie is a core part of the expanding DC Universe narrative following *Superman*. Alcock's cameo in that film established her version of the character. While the plot centers on Supergirl's solo journey, the return of Superman and the cameo by Lobo suggest ties to a broader universe. Gunn has indicated Lobo is likely to get his own project in the future.

## How has the original Supergirl reacted?

Helen Slater, who played Supergirl in the 1984 film, has voiced strong support for the new movie and its star. “I loved the new Supergirl film,” Slater told The Hollywood Reporter. “I thought Milly Alcock was astonishing — fierce, strong and great comic timing!” She previously congratulated Alcock on Instagram when the casting was first announced.

The film is scheduled for release on June 26, 2026.
