Apple TV will premiere the second season of its German-language dark comedy series **Where’s Wanda?** on October 21, 2026, according to a report from The Movie Blog.

The new season will consist of eight episodes, with new installments releasing weekly through December 9, 2026.

## What is the plot for the new season?

The series follows the Klatt family as they investigate the disappearance of their daughter, Wanda. The first season concluded with the family believing their lives were returning to normal. The second season will pick up with a new crisis, as Wanda is discovered standing over a dead body. The upcoming story will focus on her parents' desperate attempts to prove her innocence while navigating their town's criminal underbelly.

## Who is returning for Season 2?

Lea Drinda plays their daughter, Wanda, with Leo Simon and Nikeata Thompson also returning. The series is created, written, and executive produced by Oliver Lansley.

## Where can I watch the first season?

The first season of Where’s Wanda? is currently available to stream on Apple TV.
