A Live-Action Voyage Off to a Rocky Start

Catherine Lagaʻaia makes her live-action debut as the titular heroine in Disney's latest big-screen remake, '[Moana](/movies/moana-box-office-day-8-adds-5-5-million-as-domestic-total-hits-68-6-million/),' alongside Dwayne Johnson returning to voice the charismatic demigod Maui. The film, directed by Thomas Kail, is a 115-minute family fantasy adventure that retells the story of a teenage girl who answers the Ocean's call. Moana voyages beyond the reef of her island, Motunui, with the infamous Maui on a quest to restore prosperity to her people.

## An Ensemble Cast of Island Legends

The cast brings the vibrant characters of the Pacific to life. Catherine Lagaʻaia stars as the brave and determined Moana, while Dwayne Johnson reprises his role as the shape-shifting, larger-than-life demigod Maui. Rena Owen plays the wise Gramma Tala, with John Tui as Chief Tui, Moana's father, and Frankie Adams as her mother, Sina. Jemaine Clement returns as the voice of the villainous giant crab Tamatoa. Emma Puahi-Shapazian and Amaya Masoli are also featured as younger versions of Moana.

## At the Box Office

Moana has grossed $82,081,410 at the domestic box office, 10 days into its theatrical run, while playing across 4,200 theaters. The film added $5,900,000 in its most recent day of release. Worldwide, it has taken in $116 million. It carries a reported production budget of $250 million.
