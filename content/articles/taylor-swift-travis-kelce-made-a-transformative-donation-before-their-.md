How do you start a marriage? For Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, the answer appears to involve a major act of philanthropy.

Just before attending the couple's wedding, Brad Paisley and Kimberly Williams-Paisley shared a video message thanking Swift and Kelce for a donation to their Nashville nonprofit, The Store. The video was posted "on the eve of the wedding of Tay and Trav," according to the couple's caption.

In the clip, Brad, dressed in a tux, called the contribution "transformative, it will feed so many families and what a way to start a marriage. I’m very excited." Kimberly, wearing a dress, echoed the sentiment, noting the critical timing: "Food insecurity is at a 10-year high in Nashville, and the timing is just so critical we’re just really really grateful for your generosity."

The couple captioned the post, "Taking a moment to say a special thank you to Taylor and Travis for their amazing donation and congratulations on their upcoming wedding!"

The Store itself also thanked the couple on its Instagram, calling the donation a "transformative gift" that will help more families across Middle Tennessee access fresh, healthy food. Founded by Brad and Kimberly, The Store is a free-grocery supermarket in Nashville that allows low-income individuals and families to shop for fresh produce and groceries with dignity.

The gesture sparked warm reactions from fellow celebrities. Selma Blair commented with two heart emojis on the Paisleys' post, while Sharon Lawrence, Amanda Kloots, and Jennifer Garner were among those who liked it.
