Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are making good on their promise of a huge wedding. The festivities have officially begun in New York City, with the couple hosting a rehearsal dinner at Madison Square Garden on July 2, according to a report from MS NOW.

The dinner served as the kickoff to a weekend that will reportedly culminate in their wedding ceremony at the same iconic venue on July 3. Before saying "yes to forever and always," Swift partied with her inner circle. Selena Gomez seemingly attended, sharing a photo of herself in a black lace dress on her Instagram Story. Friends Jack Antonoff, Bradley Cooper, and Lena Dunham were also spotted dressed up in NYC, appearing to head to the venue in luxury cars.

The guest list extended to more close pals, including Swift's childhood best friend Abigail Anderson, her publicist Tree Paine, Travis's best friend Ross Travis, and sports reporter Erin Andrews, who were all seen at a New York hotel before the soirée. Family was also in attendance, with expected guests including both sets of parents—Donna and Ed Kelce, Andrea and Scott Swift—as well as brothers Austin Swift and Jason Kelce, plus sister-in-law Kylie Kelce.

The actual wedding day is expected to host around 1,000 guests at Madison Square Garden, an arena Swift has played eight times to sold-out crowds. However, the "Blank Space" artist reportedly won't be the one performing this time, having planned for other performers to take the stage.

This rehearsal dinner appears to confirm weeks of speculation that their nuptials will be held at MSG. The venue offers maximum space and privacy with its enclosed design and underground tunnels, fitting for the massive event Swift hinted at. "It's huge," she said on *The Graham Norton Show* in October 2024. "I'm so excited about it. I know it's gonna be fun to plan because I think the only stressful weddings are the ones where you have a small amount and people are on the bubble."

The couple and their loved ones have kept wedding details tightly under wraps. Jason Kelce even joked that he "may or may not" be invited to the elusive event. The rehearsal dinner at MSG now makes the location seem clear, though specific details about the ceremony, performers, and other festivities remain unconfirmed.
