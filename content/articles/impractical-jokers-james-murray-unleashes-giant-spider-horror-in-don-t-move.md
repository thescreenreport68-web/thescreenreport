A new trailer for the horror film *Don't Move* has arrived, offering a fresh look at the project starring Impractical Jokers member James Murray. According to IMDb, the preview reveals a new look at the film.

## What is 'Don't Move' about?

The film follows a grieving woman who encounters a killer in a secluded forest. He injects her with a paralytic drug, forcing her to fight for survival as her body shuts down. This official synopsis sets the stage for a tense, claustrophobic thriller.

## Who is in the cast?

James Murray and Rob Riggle star in the film, as reported in the new trailer coverage. The full cast, according to authoritative sources, includes Kelsey Asbille as Iris and Finn Wittrock as Richard, alongside Moray Treadwell, Daniel Francis, and Denis Kostadinov.

## When and where can you watch it?

*Don't Move* is a streaming original film directed by Adam Schindler.
