The project is a collaboration between Lucasfilm and LEGO, continuing the long-running line of comedic [Star Wars](/movies/grogu-comes-home-fans-react-to-the-mandalorian-and-grogu-release-dates-with/) parodies. The report states the special will feature characters from all three seasons of the Disney+ series.

## What can fans expect from the LEGO special?

Like previous LEGO Star Wars projects, the Mandalorian special promises to be filled with action and hilarious jokes. The report notes it will create "an unserious but memorable experience" with weird twists on the familiar characters and stories.

## Is *The Mandalorian and Grogu* movie available yet?

This news follows the recent home media release of the live-action film *Star Wars: The Mandalorian and Grogu*. The movie arrived on digital platforms including Amazon Prime Video, Apple TV, and Fandango on July 21, 2026. A 4K Ultra HD and Blu-ray release is set for August 25, featuring exclusive behind-the-scenes featurettes and audio commentary from director Jon Favreau.

More details about the LEGO Star Wars: The Mandalorian special are expected to be revealed ahead of its September debut.
