Barstool Sports suffered a historic 543-0 loss to the family of Dallas Mavericks All-Star [Cooper Flagg](/tv/cooper-flagg-family-to-battle-barstool-on-celebrity-family-feud/) on the latest episode of Celebrity Family Feud, according to multiple outlets. The defeat marks the worst in the show's 13-season history.

Led by founder Dave Portnoy, the Barstool team competed for charity but failed to score a single point in the main game. They were shut out across four survey questions, which included prompts like "Name something a player probably has that a regular guy might not." For that question, Portnoy's answer of "big d*ck" reportedly left host Steve Harvey momentarily speechless before he apologized to Flagg's mother.

## How Did the Flagg Family Win?

The Flagg family, which included Cooper, his brother Ace, and their parents, dominated the game. In the Fast Money bonus round, Cooper and Ace needed to score over 200 points to win the cash prize for their chosen charity, the Ronald McDonald House of Dallas. They achieved this goal with ease, securing the donation.

## What Were the Funniest Moments?

While the game was a blowout, it provided several memorable exchanges. When asked "What is something you can only stand to do for five minutes?" Cooper's mother, Kelly, said it wasn't her answer, but she knew it was up there: "have sex." Her husband, Ralph, countered with "talk to my wife."

The episode is available to stream on Hulu, fuboTV, and YouTube TV, as of July 2026.
