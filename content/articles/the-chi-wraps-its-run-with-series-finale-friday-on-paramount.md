The Chi ends its run this Friday with its series finale on Paramount+. According to TVLine, the episode will see Emmett and Kiesha "say yes to their fairytale."

Friday also brings a slate of other premieres and fresh episodes across streaming and cable. Jake Johnson stars in [The Dink](/movies/josh-greenbaum-sets-the-dink-as-apple-tv-s-pickleball-comedy/), a new movie premiering on Apple TV. Johnson plays a former tennis prodigy who finds new purpose in pickleball after an injury sidelines his comeback. The film co-stars Chloe Fineman, Ed Harris, Patton Oswalt, and Ben Stiller.

## What else is new to watch this Friday?

Brendan Fraser leads the cast of Pressure, a World War II drama that begins streaming Friday on Peacock. Fraser portrays General Dwight D. Eisenhower in the tense 72 hours before D-Day. Andrew Scott co-stars as Captain James Stagg.

Netflix adds the comedy 72 Hours, starring Kevin Hart, and the Heartstopper retrospective special Heartstopper: Ending on a Hi. HBO Max debuts the action sequel Mortal Kombat II for streaming, while Hulu adds the film Nirvanna the Band the Show the Movie. New episodes of series like Apple TV's Silo and Sugar, and Starz's Power Book III: Raising Kanan, also air.

## Where can I watch The Chi finale?

The series finale of The Chi is available on Paramount+. The platform requires a subscription to access its content, including the show's concluding episode.
