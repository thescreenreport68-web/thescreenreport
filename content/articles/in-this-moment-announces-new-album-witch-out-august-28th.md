In This Moment announced their ninth full-length album, "Witch," will be released on August 28, according to Metal Planet Music. The Grammy-nominated metal band also released a new single and music video titled "Crawl."

The album marks the group's debut with Better Noise Music. The 11-track album includes collaborations with Dayseeker, Kim Dracula, and Kat Von D, who appears on a cover of Pink Floyd's "Another Brick In The Wall."

## What is the new single about?

Frontwoman Maria Brink described "Crawl" as "a reckoning — a reflection that those who dominate and cause pain will ultimately reap what they sow." Co-founder and guitarist Chris Howorth added that the track is "a visceral" observation of powerful entities abusing weaker masses, and is about "standing up and fighting back."

In This Moment will hit the road this fall.
