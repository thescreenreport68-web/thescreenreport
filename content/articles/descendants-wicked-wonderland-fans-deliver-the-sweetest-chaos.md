## The Weight of the Crown

Adding a layer of depth to the lighthearted fanfare are comments from the film’s star, Kylie Cantrall, who plays Red, the daughter of the Queen of Hearts. She spoke candidly about the pressures that came with the leading role. "And I think with Red, since there’s never been a daughter of the Queen of Hearts, I kind of had the liberty to take it in whatever direction I felt like made sense, but I didn’t want to let anyone down, and I did feel a sense of pressure, and especially being the lead and not only doing the character justice, but also being a role model on set and being a leader on set," Cantrall said.

She embraced that leadership role, particularly with the film’s newer cast members. "It was fun to kind of step up and and be that for them this go around."
