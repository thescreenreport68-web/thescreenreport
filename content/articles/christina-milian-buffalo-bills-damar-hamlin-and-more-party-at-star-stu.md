The guest list was a who's who of talent, with singer Christina Milian, Buffalo Bills player Damar Hamlin, Boston Celtics star Jayson Tatum, and social media creators like Mike Majlak and Amanda McCants all spotted at the Château de La Croix des Gardes estate on June 23. The event also welcomed other notable figures including Bozoma Saint John, Cooper Flagg, Adam W., MDMotivator, and the social media creators Ling and Lamb. Music for the evening was provided by DJ Talon Reid, who kept guests dancing the night away.

Photos from the event show Jayson Tatum mingling with Fanatics’ Mike Hermalyn and Influential co-founder Ryan Detert, while social media creator Amanda McCants could be seen with other guests.

The event was planned and produced by Lisa Lafferty of Lisa Lafferty Events. The night featured signature cocktails by Cîroc and Tequila Komos, and rosé from Château Sainte-Marguerite. The château itself is known for its stunning views of Cannes.
