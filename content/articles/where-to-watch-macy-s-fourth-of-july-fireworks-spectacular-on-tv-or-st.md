The Macy's Fourth of July Fireworks Spectacular will air live on ABC and stream on Peacock on July 4th, starting at 8 p.m. Eastern Time. The two-hour event promises a massive fireworks display alongside a lineup of major musical acts, according to a report from TheWrap.

## Where and When to Watch

The live broadcast begins at 8 p.m. ET on ABC. For cord-cutters, the show will be available to stream live simultaneously through the Peacock app. The event is scheduled to run for two hours, making for a full evening of patriotic entertainment.

## Who Is Performing?

This year's musical lineup is a major draw. Performers include Noah Kahan, Post Malone, Salt-N-Pepa, Bebe Rexha, Shaboozey, and Blake Shelton. Additionally, Alexia Jayy, the winner of "The Voice" Season 29, will perform during the fireworks display itself.

## What Makes This Year Special?

According to TheWrap, the 2026 show celebrates two major milestones: the 250th birthday of the United States and the 50th anniversary of the Macy's fireworks show in New York City. Executive producer Will Coss stated the anniversary will be honored with an expanded show, never-before-seen effects, and music's biggest stars for what he called "a truly unforgettable celebration."
